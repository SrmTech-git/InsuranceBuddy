CA 27 07 (2020-11) — Unmanned Aircraft Exclusion For General Liability Coverages – Personal And Advertising Injury Liability Only
==================================================================================================================================
Form number: CA 27 07
Edition: 2020-11
Title: Unmanned Aircraft Exclusion For General Liability Coverages – Personal And Advertising Injury Liability Only
Type: ISO Commercial Auto endorsement
States: All (multi-state)

Policy term: Matches underlying CA 00 25 Auto Dealers Coverage Form
Transaction types: New business; endorsement addition

Purpose:
Excludes unmanned aircraft (drone) claims from Coverage B (Personal
and Advertising Injury Liability) only under Section II — General
Liability Coverages. Coverage A (Bodily Injury and Property Damage
Liability) is not affected.

Captures:
- Exclusion of personal and advertising injury claims arising from
  unmanned aircraft under Section II, Coverage B only

When used:
- Situations where only the personal and advertising injury exposure
  from drones needs to be excluded (e.g., aerial photography raising
  privacy or copyright claims) while retaining BI/PD coverage

Notes:
- CA 27 05 is the full exclusion covering both Coverage A and B
- CA 27 06 is the Coverage A-only (BI/PD) variant
- CA 27 08–10 are the corresponding affirmative coverage endorsements
  for scheduled unmanned aircraft operations
- Part of the November 2020 ISO Auto Dealers form revision cycle
