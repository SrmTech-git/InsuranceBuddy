CA 23 85 (2013-10) — Exclusion Of Terrorism Involving Nuclear, Biological Or Chemical Terrorism
===============================================================================================
Form number: CA 23 85
Edition: 2013-10
Title: Exclusion Of Terrorism Involving Nuclear, Biological Or Chemical Terrorism
Type: ISO Commercial Auto endorsement
States: All

Policy term: Matches underlying policy
Transaction types: New business; renewal; midterm add

Purpose:
Excludes coverage specifically for acts of terrorism involving nuclear,
biological, or chemical (NBC) agents. NBC terrorism represents a distinct
and extreme risk category; even insurers that provide conventional terrorism
coverage under TRIA typically exclude NBC attacks due to the potentially
unlimited, uninsurable scale of such events. This endorsement carves out
the NBC terrorism exclusion while leaving conventional terrorism coverage
(CA 23 84) as a separate decision.

Captures:
- Exclusion of all BI, PD, and physical damage claims arising from terrorism
  involving nuclear detonation, biological agents (anthrax, pathogens), or
  chemical weapons (nerve agents, toxic chemicals)
- Applies regardless of whether the event qualifies as a certified TRIA act
  of terrorism

When used:
- Commercial auto policies where the insurer will not provide NBC terrorism
  coverage under any circumstances
- Used alongside CA 23 84 (full terrorism exclusion) or independently when
  the insured accepts conventional terrorism coverage but the insurer
  excludes NBC
- Standard attachment in many large commercial auto programs due to NBC
  uninsurability

Notes:
- NBC exclusions exist across virtually all property and casualty lines;
  the auto version (CA 23 85) is part of the ISO suite that addresses
  terrorism across all commercial lines
- TRIA does not mandate coverage for NBC acts where the insurer has a
  standard exclusion; the mandate applies to certified conventional
  terrorist acts
- CA 23 87 is the companion form that excludes NBC terrorism ABOVE minimum
  statutory limits (partial exclusion variant)
