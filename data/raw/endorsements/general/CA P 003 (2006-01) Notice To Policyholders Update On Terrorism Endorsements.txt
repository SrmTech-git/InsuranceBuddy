CA P 003 (2006-01) — Notice To Policyholders Update On Terrorism Endorsements
=============================================================================
Form number: CA P 003
Edition: 2006-01
Title: Notice To Policyholders Update On Terrorism Endorsements
Type: ISO Commercial Auto policyholder advisory notice
States: All

Purpose:
Advisory notice providing an update to commercial auto policyholders about
terrorism coverage endorsements following the 2005-2006 changes to the
Terrorism Risk Insurance Act (TRIA) and ISO's corresponding terrorism
endorsement revisions. Explains the impact of TRIA reauthorization on
the CA 23 84 through CA 23 87 terrorism endorsement options and how
terrorism coverage applies or can be elected or excluded under the
policyholder's commercial auto program.

Content:
- Explanation of TRIA reauthorization and its effect on commercial auto
  terrorism coverage
- Summary of CA 23 84-87 terrorism endorsement options (cap on losses,
  exclusions, exceeding minimums, NBC exclusion)
- Guidance on how terrorism coverage elections affect the commercial
  auto policy

When used:
- Issued with policies where terrorism coverage endorsements were revised
  in response to TRIA reauthorization in the 2006-01 timeframe
- Satisfies state policyholder notice requirements for terrorism-related
  form changes

Notes:
- TRIA has been reauthorized multiple times since 2006; this notice
  addresses the specific 2006-era revision
- The terrorism endorsements it references (CA 23 84-87) remain the
  current ISO terrorism endorsement suite; the notice provides historical
  context for their original introduction and structure
- For current terrorism coverage questions, refer to the current CA 23 84-87
  endorsement forms rather than this advisory notice
