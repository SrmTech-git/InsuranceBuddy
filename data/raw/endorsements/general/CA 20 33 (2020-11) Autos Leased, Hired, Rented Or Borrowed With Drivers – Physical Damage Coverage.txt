CA 20 33 (2020-11) — Autos Leased, Hired, Rented Or Borrowed With Drivers – Physical Damage Coverage
====================================================================================================
Form number: CA 20 33
Edition: 2020-11
Title: Autos Leased, Hired, Rented Or Borrowed With Drivers – Physical Damage Coverage
Type: ISO Commercial Auto endorsement
States: All

Policy term: Matches underlying policy
Transaction types: New business; renewal; midterm add

Purpose:
Extends physical damage coverage to autos that are leased, hired, rented, or
borrowed along with their drivers — "staffed vehicles." Standard hired auto
physical damage coverage typically applies to vehicles the insured hires
without a driver (the insured's own employees drive the rented vehicle).
When a vehicle comes with its own driver provided by a third party, the
insured's physical damage exposure is different, and this endorsement
specifically covers that scenario.

Captures:
- Physical damage coverage for vehicles supplied with their own driver
  (e.g., a charter bus with a contracted driver, a hired limousine with
  a chauffeur, a staffed crane or equipment vehicle)
- Coverage is for the named insured's liability for physical damage to
  the vehicle, not the insured's own employees' driving

When used:
- Companies that hire vehicle-and-driver packages for events, projects,
  or operations (chauffeured vehicles, staffed equipment)
- Construction firms using hired cranes or specialty equipment that comes
  with an operator
- Any account where vehicles are rented with the owner's driver as part
  of the transaction

Notes:
- Without this endorsement, physical damage to a hired-with-driver vehicle
  may not be covered under standard hired auto physical damage because the
  vehicle is operated by the owner's driver, not the insured's employee
- The damage exposure still exists if the insured's operations contribute
  to the physical damage (e.g., directing where a crane parks, instructing
  a driver who then collides)
- Liability for third-party BI/PD involving a staffed vehicle is a separate
  question; this endorsement addresses the physical damage coverage only
