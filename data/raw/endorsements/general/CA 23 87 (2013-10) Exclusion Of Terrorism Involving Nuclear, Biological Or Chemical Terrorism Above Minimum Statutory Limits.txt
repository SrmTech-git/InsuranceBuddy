CA 23 87 (2013-10) — Exclusion Of Terrorism Involving Nuclear, Biological Or Chemical Terrorism Above Minimum Statutory Limits
==============================================================================================================================
Form number: CA 23 87
Edition: 2013-10
Title: Exclusion Of Terrorism Involving Nuclear, Biological Or Chemical Terrorism Above Minimum Statutory Limits
Type: ISO Commercial Auto endorsement
States: All

Policy term: Matches underlying policy
Transaction types: New business; renewal; midterm add

Purpose:
Partially excludes nuclear, biological, or chemical (NBC) terrorism coverage
by capping NBC terrorism liability at state-mandated minimum limits while
excluding NBC terrorism losses above those minimums. Combines the partial-
exclusion approach of CA 23 86 (terrorism above minimums) with the NBC-
specific targeting of CA 23 85. Allows the policy to satisfy minimum state
auto liability requirements for NBC terror events while excluding excess
NBC terrorism exposure.

Captures:
- NBC terrorism liability coverage limited to state-mandated financial
  responsibility minimums only
- Exclusion of all NBC terrorism coverage above the statutory minimum limits
- The policy responds at the state minimum level for NBC terrorism events

When used:
- States with mandatory minimum auto liability where even NBC terrorism claims
  must receive some response, but the carrier excludes excess NBC exposure
- Commercial auto programs adopting a partial-exclusion approach specifically
  for NBC terrorism

Notes:
- This is the most specific and narrowest of the four terrorism endorsements:
  CA 23 84 (full exclusion), CA 23 85 (NBC full exclusion), CA 23 86
  (conventional terrorism above minimums excluded), CA 23 87 (NBC terrorism
  above minimums excluded)
- In practice, CA 23 84 + CA 23 85 (full exclusions) are more commonly used;
  CA 23 86 and CA 23 87 are for situations where partial compliance is needed
- The four endorsements can be mixed: e.g., CA 23 86 excludes conventional
  terrorism above minimums while CA 23 85 fully excludes NBC terrorism
