CA 23 01 (2013-10) — Explosives
===============================
Form number: CA 23 01
Edition: 2013-10
Title: Explosives
Type: ISO Commercial Auto endorsement
States: All

Policy term: Matches underlying policy
Transaction types: New business; renewal; midterm add

Purpose:
Excludes liability coverage for bodily injury or property damage arising
from the explosion of explosives that the insured manufactures, sells,
handles, or transports. Attaches to CA 00 01 (Business Auto) or CA 00 20
(Motor Carrier) to remove the policy's liability response for
explosives-related BI/PD claims.

Captures:
- Exclusion of BI and PD liability arising from explosion of explosives
  the insured makes, sells, handles, or transports
- Applies to covered autos liability coverage section

When used:
- Insurers wishing to exclude the explosives liability exposure from a
  commercial auto policy
- Accounts where the underwriter is unwilling to provide explosives
  liability without separate specialty coverage in place

Notes:
- This is an EXCLUSION endorsement — it removes coverage, it does not
  provide it; a card or policy summary describing it as coverage-granting
  would be incorrect
- Insureds with legitimate explosives transport operations need specialty
  hazmat or explosives liability coverage outside the standard CA program
- DOT/PHMSA licensing, placarding, and routing requirements apply to
  explosives transporters independently of this endorsement
