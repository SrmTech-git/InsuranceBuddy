CA 25 59 (2013-10) — Designated Location(s) General Liability Aggregate Limit For Certain General Liability Coverages
======================================================================================================================
Form number: CA 25 59
Edition: 2013-10
Title: Designated Location(s) General Liability Aggregate Limit For Certain General Liability Coverages
Type: ISO Commercial Auto endorsement
States: All

Policy term: Matches underlying CA 00 25 Auto Dealers Coverage Form
Transaction types: New business; endorsement addition

Purpose:
Creates a separate general liability aggregate limit for each location
listed on the endorsement schedule, preventing losses at one location
from eroding the aggregate available to other locations.

Captures:
- Each scheduled location receives its own aggregate limit equal to
  (or as otherwise specified in the schedule) the policy's General
  Aggregate Limit
- Claims arising from a scheduled location draw only against that
  location's aggregate, not the master policy aggregate

When used:
- Auto dealer groups with multiple rooftops on a single CA 00 25 policy
  who want to prevent a major loss at one location from exhausting the
  GL aggregate for all other locations
- Dealers with locations that have materially different risk profiles

Notes:
- Operations, premises, or losses not attributable to any scheduled
  location continue to share the policy's overall aggregate
- Parallel to CG 25 04 (Designated Location(s) General Aggregate Limit)
  in the standard CGL program
