CA P 005 (2010-06) — Replacement Of The Truckers Coverage Form With The Motor Carrier Coverage Form Advisory Notice To Policyholders
====================================================================================================================================
Form number: CA P 005
Edition: 2010-06
Title: Replacement Of The Truckers Coverage Form With The Motor Carrier Coverage Form Advisory Notice To Policyholders
Type: ISO Commercial Auto policyholder advisory notice
States: All

Purpose:
Advisory notice specifically explaining the retirement of the ISO Truckers
Coverage Form and its replacement by the new CA 00 20 Motor Carrier Coverage
Form in the 2010 commercial auto program revision. This was a significant
structural change in the trucking insurance program — the old Truckers form
was replaced by a new form with updated definitions, coverage structure,
and regulatory compliance provisions. This notice explains to policyholders
what the change means for their coverage.

Content:
- Explanation that the prior ISO Truckers Coverage Form is replaced by
  CA 00 20 Motor Carrier Coverage Form effective with the 2010 revision
- Summary of how coverage changes from the old Truckers form to the new
  Motor Carrier form
- Key differences between the prior Truckers form and CA 00 20 (updated
  definitions, revised FMCSA compliance provisions, new trailer interchange
  structure)

When used:
- Issued with trucking policies transitioning from the old Truckers
  Coverage Form to the new CA 00 20 Motor Carrier Coverage Form
- Required notice for policyholders experiencing a material form change
  in their trucking insurance program

Notes:
- This is a significant historical notice; the CA 00 20 Motor Carrier
  Coverage Form is now the current standard for trucking/motor carrier
  programs and has been since 2010
- Trucking accounts that have been with the same insurer since before 2010
  should have received this notice when their program transitioned
- The CA 00 20 introduced the expanded motor carrier definition that covers
  both for-hire and private carriers, replacing the more limited Truckers form
