CA 27 22 (2022-09) — Sexual Abuse Or Sexual Molestation Exclusion For General Liability And Acts, Errors Or Omissions Liability Coverages
============================================================================================================================================
Form number: CA 27 22
Edition: 2022-09
Title: Sexual Abuse Or Sexual Molestation Exclusion For General Liability And Acts, Errors Or Omissions Liability Coverages
Type: ISO Commercial Auto endorsement
States: All except NY, PR

Policy term: Matches underlying CA 00 25 Auto Dealers Coverage Form
Transaction types: New business; endorsement addition

Purpose:
Excludes claims arising specifically from sexual abuse or molestation
from both the General Liability (Section II) and Acts, Errors or
Omissions Liability (Section III) coverages of CA 00 25. Narrower
than CA 27 21 — limited to sexual acts only, leaving coverage for
other forms of abuse intact.

Captures:
- Exclusion of bodily injury, property damage, and personal and
  advertising injury arising from sexual abuse or molestation
- Exclusion of negligent employment, supervision, and retention claims
  connected to sexual abuse or molestation
- Applies to both Section II — General Liability Coverages and
  Section III — Acts, Errors Or Omissions Liability Coverages

When used:
- Dealers or insurers who want to exclude sexual abuse exposure while
  retaining coverage for other forms of abuse or molestation
- Used when only sexual abuse/molestation is the unacceptable exposure
  and a broader exclusion (CA 27 21) would be overbroad

Notes:
- CA 27 21 is the broader alternative that excludes all abuse and
  molestation, not just sexual acts
- CA 27 23 and CA 27 24 are the corresponding affirmative coverage
  endorsements if coverage for sexual abuse is desired instead
- Not available in NY or PR
- Part of the September 2022 ISO filing cycle
