CA 04 49 (2016-11) — Primary And Noncontributory – Other Insurance Condition
============================================================================
Form number: CA 04 49
Edition: 2016-11
Title: Primary And Noncontributory – Other Insurance Condition
Type: ISO Commercial Auto endorsement
States: All

Policy term: Matches underlying policy
Transaction types: New business; renewal; midterm add

Purpose:
Modifies the "other insurance" condition in the CA 00 01 policy so that the
named insured's commercial auto coverage is primary and non-contributing
relative to any other available insurance when required by a written contract
or agreement. Without this endorsement, multiple insurers typically share
a loss proportionally; this endorsement makes the named insured's policy pay
first, with no contribution from the additional insured's own carrier.

Captures:
- Primary and noncontributory treatment for the named insured's CA policy
  when a written contract requires it
- Applies to all additional insureds or specified parties per the endorsement
  schedule (or blanket, depending on version)

When used:
- Required by construction contracts, lease agreements, and vendor contracts
  that specify the insured's carrier must provide primary and noncontributory
  coverage for additional insureds
- Certificate of insurance requests that specifically list "primary and
  noncontributory" as a required condition
- When a general contractor or property owner requires their subcontractor's
  auto policy to be primary ahead of the GC's own coverage

Notes:
- This is one of the most commonly requested endorsements in commercial
  lines — most standard contracts require it
- Without this endorsement, the CA policy's standard "other insurance"
  condition makes it pro rata (proportional) with other available coverage —
  meaning the additional insured's own carrier would also be called upon to
  contribute, which they and their insured typically want to avoid
- The ACORD 25 certificate of insurance has a checkbox for "primary and
  noncontributory" — evidence of this endorsement supports that representation
- The 2016-11 edition appears to be the first (and current) edition of this
  form; no prior edition of CA 04 49 has been identified
