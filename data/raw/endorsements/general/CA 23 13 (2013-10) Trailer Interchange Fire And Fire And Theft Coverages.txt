CA 23 13 (2013-10) — Trailer Interchange Fire And Fire And Theft Coverages
==========================================================================
Form number: CA 23 13
Edition: 2013-10
Title: Trailer Interchange Fire And Fire And Theft Coverages
Type: ISO Commercial Auto endorsement
States: All

Policy term: Matches underlying policy
Transaction types: New business; renewal; midterm add

Purpose:
Provides limited physical damage coverage — fire only, or fire and theft
— for non-owned trailers in the insured's possession under trailer
interchange agreements. Trailer interchange is common in trucking: carriers
temporarily use trailers owned by other carriers. The base CA 00 20 covers
owned trailers; CA 23 13 extends limited physical damage to non-owned
trailers under interchange without providing the full comprehensive and
collision coverage of the broader CA 23 98 trailer interchange endorsement.

Captures:
- Physical damage coverage limited to fire losses, or fire and theft losses,
  on non-owned trailers under interchange agreements
- Applies while the trailer is in the insured's possession under a valid
  trailer interchange agreement

When used:
- Motor carriers whose interchange agreements require them to carry physical
  damage on non-owned trailers but only need fire coverage or fire + theft
  (not full comprehensive and collision)
- Cost-conscious carriers that want to limit their physical damage scope
  to the most likely catastrophic perils (fire, theft) rather than paying
  for collision coverage on borrowed trailers

Notes:
- CA 23 13 is the limited version (fire only or fire + theft); CA 23 98
  is the broader form covering comprehensive and collision for non-owned
  trailers under interchange — use CA 23 98 if the interchange agreement
  requires full physical damage coverage
- Trailer interchange agreements typically specify what physical damage
  coverage the borrowing carrier must maintain; the endorsement should
  match the contractual requirement
- Limit of coverage should reflect the value of the trailers being interchanged
