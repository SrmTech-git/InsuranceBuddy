CA 27 15 (2020-11) — Amendment Of Personal And Advertising Injury Definition – General Liability Coverages
==========================================================================================================
Form number: CA 27 15
Edition: 2020-11
Title: Amendment Of Personal And Advertising Injury Definition – General Liability Coverages
Type: ISO Commercial Auto endorsement
States: All (multi-state)

Policy term: Matches underlying CA 00 25 Auto Dealers Coverage Form
Transaction types: New business; endorsement addition

Purpose:
Narrows the scope of Coverage B (Personal and Advertising Injury
Liability) under the CA 00 25 by removing privacy-violation claims from
the definition of personal and advertising injury.

Captures:
- Removal of "oral or written publication that violates a person's right
  of privacy" from the personal and advertising injury definition in
  Section II — General Liability Coverages
- Effect: data breach, unauthorized disclosure, and other privacy
  violation claims are no longer covered under Coverage B after this
  endorsement is attached

When used:
- Insurers who want to exclude cyber/privacy exposure from the CA 00 25
  GL section, typically when that exposure is addressed by a separate
  cyber liability policy
- Dealers where privacy claims are considered outside the intended scope
  of the CA 00 25

Notes:
- Part of the November 2020 ISO Auto Dealers form revision cycle
