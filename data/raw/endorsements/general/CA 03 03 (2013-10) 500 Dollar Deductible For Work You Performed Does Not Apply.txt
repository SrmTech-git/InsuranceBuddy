CA 03 03 (2013-10) — 500 Dollar Deductible For Work You Performed Does Not Apply
================================================================================
Form number: CA 03 03
Edition: 2013-10
Title: 500 Dollar Deductible For Work You Performed Does Not Apply
Type: ISO Commercial Auto endorsement
States: All

Policy term: Matches underlying policy
Transaction types: New business; renewal; midterm change

Purpose:
Removes the $500 property damage deductible that applies under the Garage
Coverage Form (CA 00 05) and Auto Dealers Coverage Form (CA 00 25) to PD
claims arising from work performed on customers' vehicles. The base garage
and dealer forms impose a $500 deductible on PD claims where the damage
results from the insured's work or service operations; this endorsement
eliminates that built-in deductible.

Captures:
- Elimination of the $500 "work you performed" PD deductible from the
  underlying CA 00 05 or CA 00 25 policy conditions

When used:
- Repair shops and dealerships (CA 00 05 / CA 00 25 insureds) where the
  automatic $500 work-performed deductible is being waived
- When a service contract or customer agreement requires no deductible on
  PD claims from the insured's work
- Accounts where the underwriter agrees to remove the built-in deductible
  based on loss experience or account size

Notes:
- This is a Garage/Auto Dealers endorsement — it applies to CA 00 05 and
  CA 00 25, not to the CA 00 01 Business Auto Coverage Form
- Narrowly targeted: only removes the specific $500 work-performed PD
  deductible in the base form conditions; does not affect any separately
  endorsed deductible from CA 03 01 or CA 03 02
