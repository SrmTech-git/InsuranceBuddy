CA DS 03 (2020-11) — Business Auto Declarations
===============================================
Form number: CA DS 03
Edition: 2020-11
Title: Business Auto Declarations
Type: ISO Commercial Auto declarations form
States: All

Policy term: Sets the policy period for the CA 00 01 Business Auto Coverage Form
Transaction types: New business; renewal; endorsement

Purpose:
The declarations page for the CA 00 01 Business Auto Coverage Form.
Documents all policy-level information that, combined with the CA 00 01
coverage form, constitutes the insured's commercial auto policy. The
declarations summarize the named insured, policy period, covered autos
with their designated coverage symbols, liability limits, physical damage
coverages and deductibles, premium schedule, and all endorsements attached.
Functions as the single-page summary of the complete policy structure.

Captures:
- Named insured, mailing address, and agent/broker information
- Policy period (effective and expiration dates)
- Covered auto symbols assigned to each coverage (liability, MedPay,
  uninsured motorists, physical damage)
- Liability limits (combined single limit or split limits)
- Physical damage coverage selections: comprehensive, collision, or both;
  deductibles per vehicle or by class
- Schedule of covered autos with vehicle descriptions, VINs, garaging
  locations, and values
- Premium breakdown by coverage and vehicle
- List of all endorsements attached to the policy

When used:
- Every CA 00 01 Business Auto Coverage Form policy
- Required as the defining document for all commercial auto liability and
  physical damage coverage structures under the CA 00 01

Notes:
- The declarations + CA 00 01 form set is the complete standard ISO
  commercial auto policy for businesses (non-trucking, non-dealer)
- Coverage symbols in the declarations determine which autos are covered
  under each coverage; incorrect symbols are a leading cause of coverage
  disputes at claim time
- Premium schedule in the declarations provides the rating basis; changes
  to the vehicle schedule require declaration page updates
