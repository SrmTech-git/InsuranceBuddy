CA 25 02 (2013-10) — Dealers Driveaway Collision Coverage
=========================================================
Form number: CA 25 02
Edition: 2013-10
Title: Dealers Driveaway Collision Coverage
Type: ISO Commercial Auto endorsement
States: All

Policy term: Matches underlying CA 00 25 Auto Dealers Coverage Form
Transaction types: New business; endorsement addition

Purpose:
Removes the distance limitation on collision coverage for dealer vehicles
being driven or transported under the CA 00 25 Physical Damage Coverage
section. The base CA 00 25 contains a collision exclusion (Exclusion 3.d.(3))
that limits driveaway collision coverage; this endorsement eliminates that
exclusion, extending collision protection to dealer autos driven or
transported over any distance.

Captures:
- Removal of Exclusion 3.d.(3) from Section I Physical Damage Coverage
  of CA 00 25, enabling collision coverage for driveaway operations
  regardless of distance
- Applies to covered autos driven or transported during the period
  covered by the dealer's regular reports to the insurer

When used:
- Auto dealers who regularly drive or transport inventory vehicles
  beyond the standard distance limit
- Dealers with multi-location operations or long-distance delivery programs

Notes:
- The endorsement requires the dealer to include in regular monthly or
  quarterly reports the point of origin, destination, and factory price
  of each covered auto driven or transported
- If the actual value of covered autos during a report period exceeds
  the reported value, the insurer pays only a proportional percentage
  of the loss
- If the first report due is delinquent at the date of loss, payment
  is capped at 75% of the applicable location's limit of insurance
