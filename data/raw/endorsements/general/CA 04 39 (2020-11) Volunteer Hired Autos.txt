CA 04 39 (2020-11) — Volunteer Hired Autos
==========================================
Form number: CA 04 39
Edition: 2020-11
Title: Volunteer Hired Autos
Type: ISO Commercial Auto endorsement
States: All

Policy term: Matches underlying policy
Transaction types: New business; renewal; midterm add

Purpose:
Adds "volunteer" as an insured under the hired auto coverage of a CA 00 01
policy when a volunteer rents or hires an auto in their own personal name on
behalf of the named insured. Under the base form, a volunteer who rents a car
in their own name for organizational activities is not an insured — the named
insured did not lease or rent the vehicle. This endorsement closes that gap
for hired/rented vehicles; it does not cover volunteers driving their own
personally-owned vehicles (that exposure is addressed by CA 05 24).

Captures:
- Liability coverage for the volunteer as an insured while operating a hired
  or rented auto in their personal name for the named insured's activities
- Physical damage coverage for the hired/rented vehicle (where the underlying
  policy includes hired auto physical damage coverage)
- Clarifies that the named insured's liability exposure for volunteer-rented
  vehicle use is covered

When used:
- Nonprofit organizations, charitable foundations, religious institutions, or
  community groups whose volunteers regularly drive personal vehicles for
  organizational purposes
- Any named insured with Symbol 8 (hired autos) coverage where volunteer-
  driven personal vehicles are a meaningful exposure
- Typical use: delivering meals, transporting clients, running errands for
  the organization

Notes:
- Without this endorsement, a volunteer who rents a car in their own name for
  organizational use is not an insured under the named insured's CA policy —
  the volunteer's own coverage would be their only resource
- CA 05 24 (Non-Ownership Liability for Volunteers) is the companion form for
  volunteers using their own personally-owned vehicles; CA 04 39 covers hired/
  rented vehicles; an organization with both exposures needs both endorsements
- Volunteers renting vehicles for organizational use should still be directed
  to follow the organization's vehicle rental and reimbursement policies
