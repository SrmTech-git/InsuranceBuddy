CA 25 66 (2013-10) — Customer Complaint Legal Defense Coverage
==============================================================
Form number: CA 25 66
Edition: 2013-10
Title: Customer Complaint Legal Defense Coverage
Type: ISO Commercial Auto endorsement
States: All

Policy term: Matches underlying CA 00 25 Auto Dealers Coverage Form
Transaction types: New business; endorsement addition

Purpose:
Adds a separate, affirmative defense-costs-only coverage grant for the
legal expenses incurred by an auto dealer in responding to customer
complaints and regulatory proceedings arising from the dealer's
operations (sale, service, or repair of autos). Does not pay damages,
judgments, or settlements — attorney fees and defense expenses only.

Captures:
- Defense costs for responding to a complaint or regulatory proceeding
  by a customer related to the named insured's auto dealer operations
- Covers sale, service, and repair operations as defined in the
  endorsement

When used:
- Auto dealers seeking protection against defense costs from state
  motor vehicle dealer board complaints, consumer protection agency
  proceedings, or customer-initiated regulatory actions
- Dealers with active F&I, service, or used-car operations where
  regulatory complaints are a realistic exposure

Notes:
- Claims-made basis — claims must be reported during the policy period
- Defense costs only — indemnity payments, judgments, and settlements
  are not covered under this endorsement
- Carries its own per-complaint limit and aggregate limit, entered on
  the endorsement schedule separately from the main policy limits
- Contains its own exclusions section; coverage does not extend beyond
  the defined AEO-type dealer activities
