CA 00 01 (2020-11) — Business Auto Coverage Form
=================================================
Form number: CA 00 01
Edition: 2020-11
Title: Business Auto Coverage Form
Type: ISO Commercial Auto coverage form
States: All

Policy term: 12 months standard; other terms available
Transaction types: New business; renewal; midterm endorsement; policy rewrite

Purpose:
The standard ISO coverage form for commercial auto liability and physical
damage for most non-dealer, non-trucking commercial risks. Provides liability
(BI/PD) for covered autos and physical damage (comprehensive, collision,
specified causes of loss) on a symbol-driven framework that lets the insured
and underwriter control exactly which autos are covered under each coverage.

Captures:
- Liability coverage for bodily injury and property damage arising from
  covered auto use (Symbols 1–9 define which autos qualify)
- Physical damage coverage: comprehensive, collision, specified causes of
  loss; actual cash value or stated amount basis
- Medical payments (attached separately via CA 99 03)
- Uninsured/underinsured motorists (attached via state UM/UIM endorsements)
- Defined coverage territory (U.S., territories, Canada, international
  extension by endorsement)
- Exclusions: expected/intended, contractual, workers comp, pollution
  (broadened by CA 99 48), racing, war

When used:
- New commercial auto policy for a business that owns, hires, or uses autos
  in connection with its operations (not a dealer or motor carrier)
- Renewal of an existing Business Auto policy
- Rewrite when changing program, carrier, or coverage structure
- Does NOT replace the Motor Carrier form (CA 00 20) for FMCSA-regulated
  truckers or the Auto Dealers form (CA 00 25) for dealer inventory risks

Notes:
- Symbol system is the defining feature: Symbol 1 (any auto — broadest)
  through Symbol 9 (non-owned autos only); Symbols 7/8/9 are common for
  hired/non-owned liability; Symbol 2 (owned autos only) is most common
  for physical damage
- 2020-11 edition introduced cyber/hacking exclusion language (see CA 04 65
  for optional hacking expense coverage) and updated pollution exclusion
- CA 00 01 is the backbone for most commercial auto accounts; endorsements
  from the CA 20, CA 23, and CA 99 series modify and extend it
- Not used for personal auto (use PAP/ACORD 80); not used for dealers
  (use CA 00 25) or motor carriers requiring MCS-90 (use CA 00 20)
