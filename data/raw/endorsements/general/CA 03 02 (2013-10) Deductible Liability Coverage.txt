CA 03 02 (2013-10) — Deductible Liability Coverage
==================================================
Form number: CA 03 02
Edition: 2013-10
Title: Deductible Liability Coverage
Type: ISO Commercial Auto endorsement
States: All

Policy term: Matches underlying policy
Transaction types: New business; renewal; midterm change

Purpose:
Introduces a per-accident liability deductible under a commercial auto policy.
The insurer defends and settles claims and is obligated to advance the deductible
on behalf of the insured, then seeks reimbursement. The mandatory payment
obligation ("will pay") is the sole structural distinction from CA 03 01 ("may
pay"). Applies to CA 00 01 (Business Auto), CA 00 20 (Motor Carrier), CA 00 05
(Garage), and CA 00 25 (Auto Dealers).

Captures:
- Per-accident deductible amount, applicable to all liability (BI + PD) or to
  property damage only, depending on schedule selection
- Insurer's mandatory obligation to pay claims and then seek reimbursement
  from the insured for amounts within the deductible
- Premium credit per the ISO rating plan for the selected deductible amount

When used:
- Accounts where the carrier requires mandatory deductible funding rather
  than discretionary reimbursement
- Large commercial or fleet accounts with loss-sensitive program structures
  that need a defined pay-and-reimburse mechanism

Notes:
- The only documented distinction from CA 03 01 is the payment language:
  CA 03 02 = insurer "will" pay and seek reimbursement (mandatory);
  CA 03 01 = insurer "may" seek reimbursement (discretionary)
- Both forms offer flexible deductible structures (all-liability or PD-only);
  neither is a "split BI/PD" deductible form exclusively
- The deductible is a reimbursement obligation, not a true SIR; the insurer
  controls defense and settlement
- Collateral requirements for deductible reimbursement are typically negotiated
  separately in a loss-sensitive program agreement
