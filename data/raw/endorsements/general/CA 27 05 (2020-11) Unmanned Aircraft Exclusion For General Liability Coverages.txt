CA 27 05 (2020-11) — Unmanned Aircraft Exclusion For General Liability Coverages
=================================================================================
Form number: CA 27 05
Edition: 2020-11
Title: Unmanned Aircraft Exclusion For General Liability Coverages
Type: ISO Commercial Auto endorsement
States: All (multi-state)

Policy term: Matches underlying CA 00 25 Auto Dealers Coverage Form
Transaction types: New business; endorsement addition

Purpose:
Excludes bodily injury, property damage, and personal and advertising
injury arising from the ownership, maintenance, use, or entrustment of
unmanned aircraft (drones) from all General Liability sub-coverages
(Coverage A and Coverage B) of the CA 00 25.

Captures:
- Exclusion of BI/PD and personal and advertising injury claims arising
  from unmanned aircraft across all of Section II — General Liability
  Coverages
- Applies to both Coverage A (Bodily Injury and Property Damage
  Liability) and Coverage B (Personal and Advertising Injury Liability)
  simultaneously

When used:
- Dealers who use or permit the use of drones (e.g., for aerial lot
  photography or marketing) where the insurer wants to exclude all GL
  drone exposure
- Full exclusion when no partial unmanned aircraft coverage is desired

Notes:
- CA 27 06 is the Coverage A-only (BI/PD) variant
- CA 27 07 is the Coverage B-only (personal and advertising injury)
  variant
- CA 27 08–10 are the corresponding affirmative coverage endorsements
  for designated unmanned aircraft
- Part of the November 2020 ISO Auto Dealers form revision cycle
