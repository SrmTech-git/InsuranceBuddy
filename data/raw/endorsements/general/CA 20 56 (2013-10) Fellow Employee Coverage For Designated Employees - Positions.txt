CA 20 56 (2013-10) — Fellow Employee Coverage For Designated Employees/Positions
================================================================================
Form number: CA 20 56
Edition: 2013-10
Title: Fellow Employee Coverage For Designated Employees/Positions
Type: ISO Commercial Auto endorsement
States: All

Policy term: Matches underlying policy
Transaction types: New business; renewal; midterm add

Purpose:
Provides the same fellow employee coverage as CA 20 55, but limited to
specifically named individuals or identified job positions rather than all
employees of the named insured. Allows the underwriter or insured to target
the fellow employee exclusion removal at specific roles or high-profile
employees without extending the coverage broadly to the entire workforce.

Captures:
- Removal of the fellow employee exclusion for bodily injury, limited to
  the scheduled employees or positions
- CA liability coverage for employee-vs-employee auto claims involving the
  designated individuals or job classifications
- Schedule of covered employees (by name) or positions (by title or role)

When used:
- When blanket fellow employee coverage (CA 20 55) is not warranted for
  all employees but specific individuals or roles need it
- Executive or high-risk-role employees (company drivers, sales representatives
  with high driving exposure) who face elevated risk of being sued by
  injured coworkers
- When underwriters are willing to grant fellow employee coverage for
  some but not all of the named insured's workforce

Notes:
- CA 20 55 is the blanket (all employees) version; CA 20 56 is the
  scheduled (named employees/positions) version
- The schedule must be kept current — if a covered employee leaves and is
  not removed, or a new employee who should be covered is not added, the
  coverage intent may not align with the policy
- The considerations about state WC exclusive remedy and when employees
  can sue coworkers (see CA 20 55 notes) apply equally here
