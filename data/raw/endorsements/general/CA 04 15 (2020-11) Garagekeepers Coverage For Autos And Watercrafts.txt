CA 04 15 (2020-11) — Garagekeepers Coverage For Autos And Watercrafts
=====================================================================
Form number: CA 04 15
Edition: 2020-11
Title: Garagekeepers Coverage For Autos And Watercrafts
Type: ISO Commercial Auto endorsement
States: All

Policy term: Matches underlying policy
Transaction types: New business; renewal; midterm add

Purpose:
Extends garagekeepers coverage under the CA 00 01 or CA 00 20 policy to
include watercraft left in the insured's care, custody, or control for
service, storage, or safekeeping. Standard garagekeepers coverage applies
only to autos; this endorsement adds watercrafts (boats, personal watercraft)
to that coverage — relevant for marinas, marine dealers, and businesses that
store or service both autos and watercraft.

Captures:
- Physical damage to watercraft in the insured's care, custody, or control
  for service, repair, storage, or safekeeping
- Coverage basis options: direct and excess (or legal liability)
- Limit of insurance for watercraft (may be separate from the auto
  garagekeepers limit)

When used:
- Marina operators that also service vehicles (storage facilities, dealers
  handling both autos and boats)
- Businesses with a boat/watercraft repair or storage component attached to
  an auto-related operation
- Resort or campground operators offering both auto and boat storage

Notes:
- Standard CA 00 01 garagekeepers coverage explicitly covers only "autos";
  without this endorsement, watercraft in the insured's care have no physical
  damage protection under the commercial auto policy
- For standalone marine exposures, inland marine (boat dealers floater) may
  be more appropriate; this endorsement is suited to mixed auto/watercraft
  operations where a single policy is preferred
- Coverage basis (direct vs. legal liability) should be confirmed at binding;
  direct coverage pays regardless of the insured's legal liability, while
  legal liability requires the insured to be at fault
