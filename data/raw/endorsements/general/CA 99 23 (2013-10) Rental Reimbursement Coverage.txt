CA 99 23 (2013-10) — Rental Reimbursement Coverage
==================================================
Form number: CA 99 23
Edition: 2013-10
Title: Rental Reimbursement Coverage
Type: ISO Commercial Auto endorsement
States: All

Policy term: Matches underlying commercial auto policy
Transaction types: New business; endorsement addition

Purpose:
Adds rental reimbursement coverage to a commercial auto policy, covering
the cost of renting a substitute vehicle when a covered auto is out of
service due to a covered physical damage loss. The standard commercial
auto physical damage coverage pays to repair or replace the damaged
vehicle but does not include the cost of a rental during the repair
period. This endorsement fills that gap, providing a daily rental
reimbursement benefit up to a maximum per-day and per-occurrence limit.

Captures:
- Daily rental vehicle cost incurred while a covered auto is being
  repaired or replaced following a covered physical damage loss
- A 24-hour waiting period applies before reimbursement begins
- Subject to a maximum daily limit and a maximum per-loss limit as
  scheduled in the endorsement
- Covers the rental period until the vehicle is repaired, replaced,
  or the benefit maximum is reached
- Coverage is triggered by physical damage losses only — not by liability
  losses or other causes of vehicle downtime

When used:
- Any commercial auto program where the insured needs substitute
  transportation during vehicle repair
- Businesses where a down vehicle creates operational disruption
  requiring a rental replacement
- Fleets of all sizes where downtime creates meaningful rental cost exposure

Notes:
- The daily limit and maximum period should reflect actual rental costs
  in the relevant market — standard limits may be inadequate for
  commercial-class vehicles (trucks, specialty equipment, large SUVs)
- Compare with CA 99 90 (Optional Limits – Loss Of Use Expenses), which
  addresses the insured's loss-of-use liability to rental companies
  when a rented vehicle is damaged
- Rental reimbursement under commercial auto operates similarly to
  personal auto rental reimbursement coverage
- Coverage is not available if the insured has other spare or reserve
  autos available for use in operations
