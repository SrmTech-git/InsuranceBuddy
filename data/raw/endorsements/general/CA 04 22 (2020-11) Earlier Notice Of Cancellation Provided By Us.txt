CA 04 22 (2020-11) — Earlier Notice Of Cancellation Provided By Us
==================================================================
Form number: CA 04 22
Edition: 2020-11
Title: Earlier Notice Of Cancellation Provided By Us
Type: ISO Commercial Auto endorsement
States: All

Policy term: Matches underlying policy
Transaction types: New business; renewal; midterm add

Purpose:
Modifies the standard cancellation notice provision in the CA 00 01 (or other
CA coverage form) to provide the insured with MORE than the minimum statutory
notice period before cancellation takes effect. The base form uses state-
mandated minimums (typically 10–30 days for non-payment, 60–90 days for
underwriting cancellations); this endorsement extends that period to a
longer, scheduled number of days.

Captures:
- The number of days of advance notice the carrier will provide before
  cancellation becomes effective (e.g., 60 days, 90 days, or a custom period)
- Applies to cancellations initiated by the carrier (not to insureds' requests
  for cancellation)

When used:
- Required by lienholders, certificate holders, or additional insureds whose
  contracts specify a longer notice period than the state statutory minimum
- Large construction projects, loan covenants, or government contracts that
  require 60 or 90 days' notice
- When an agent or insured negotiates extended cancellation protection

Notes:
- This endorsement gives the insured MORE notice (more favorable), NOT less
- Distinguished from state-specific cancellation endorsements that are filed
  to comply with individual state statutes — this is a contractual extension
  beyond the statutory minimum
- Providing extended notice does not obligate the carrier to continue coverage;
  it only extends the advance warning period before the cancellation date
