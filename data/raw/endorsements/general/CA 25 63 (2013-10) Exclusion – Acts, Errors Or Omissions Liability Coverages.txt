CA 25 63 (2013-10) — Exclusion – Acts, Errors Or Omissions Liability Coverages
===============================================================================
Form number: CA 25 63
Edition: 2013-10
Title: Exclusion – Acts, Errors Or Omissions Liability Coverages
Type: ISO Commercial Auto endorsement
States: All

Policy term: Matches underlying CA 00 25 Auto Dealers Coverage Form
Transaction types: New business; endorsement addition

Purpose:
Completely removes all Acts, Errors or Omissions (AEO) Liability
coverage from the CA 00 25. This is a blanket, total exclusion — no
AEO sub-coverage survives. The entire Section III of the form is
voided.

Captures:
- Total elimination of Section III — Acts, Errors Or Omissions Liability
  Coverages from the CA 00 25 policy
- No schedule is required; the exclusion covers the entire AEO section
  unconditionally

When used:
- Insurers unwilling to offer AEO coverage for a particular dealer (e.g.,
  due to F&I operations presenting unacceptable claims-made exposure)
- Dealers where AEO exposure is being addressed on a separate standalone
  dealers E&O policy

Notes:
- CA 25 64 is the alternative when only specific AEO sub-coverages need
  to be excluded rather than all of them
- The auto liability (Section I) and GL (Section II) sections of CA 00 25
  are not affected by this endorsement
