CA 23 30 (2020-11) — Motor Carrier Endorsement
==============================================
Form number: CA 23 30
Edition: 2020-11
Title: Motor Carrier Endorsement
Type: ISO Commercial Auto endorsement
States: All

Policy term: Matches underlying policy
Transaction types: New business; renewal; midterm add

Purpose:
Attaches to the CA 00 01 (Business Auto Coverage Form) to bring
motor-carrier-specific provisions into a business auto policy for insureds
that operate as for-hire motor carriers under FMCSA authority but are
written on a BAP rather than the CA 00 20 Motor Carrier Coverage Form.
Adds definitions, conditions, and coverage terms that reflect the regulatory
and operational environment of motor carriers subject to FMCSA authority.

Captures:
- Motor carrier-specific definitions, conditions, and coverage provisions
  added to CA 00 01
- May address leased equipment, owner-operators, and FMCSA endorsement
  interaction as scheduled
- Effectively converts a CA 00 01 into a motor-carrier-capable form for
  applicable accounts

When used:
- For-hire motor carriers written on CA 00 01 (not CA 00 20) that need
  motor-carrier provisions in their policy
- Accounts where the underwriter or program uses a BAP base form but the
  insured's operations require FMCSA-regulated motor carrier coverage terms
- Large fleet or program business where the standard CA 00 01 is customized
  with CA 23 30 to address carrier-specific requirements

Notes:
- CA 23 30 attaches to CA 00 01 (Business Auto), NOT CA 00 20 (Motor
  Carrier Coverage Form); CA 00 20 already contains native motor carrier
  provisions and does not need this endorsement
- Used in conjunction with MCS-90 (FMCSA filing) and other regulatory
  endorsements that attach to motor carrier policies
- For accounts with complex trucking operations, CA 00 20 is generally the
  preferred base form; CA 23 30 is the alternative path when CA 00 01 is used
