CA 25 52 (2013-10) — Exclusion – Locations And Operations Medical Payments
==========================================================================
Form number: CA 25 52
Edition: 2013-10
Title: Exclusion – Locations And Operations Medical Payments
Type: ISO Commercial Auto endorsement
States: All

Policy term: Matches underlying CA 00 25 Auto Dealers Coverage Form
Transaction types: New business; endorsement addition

Purpose:
Eliminates all Medical Payments coverage from the CA 00 25 General
Liability section. In place of Medical Payments, first aid expenses
incurred at the scene of an accident are added to Supplementary
Payments to preserve the insured's ability to render immediate
assistance without triggering coverage concerns.

Captures:
- Removal of the Locations and Operations Medical Payments coverage
  from Section II — General Liability Coverages
- Addition of first aid expenses to the Supplementary Payments
  provision, covering reasonable costs of first aid at the scene

When used:
- Dealers where the insurer does not want to provide Medical Payments
  coverage on a no-fault basis under the GL section
- Situations where Medical Payments exposure is addressed separately
  or where the premium savings justify removal

Notes:
- The addition of first aid to Supplementary Payments ensures dealers
  are not discouraged from providing emergency assistance at the scene
  out of concern that doing so admits liability or triggers coverage
