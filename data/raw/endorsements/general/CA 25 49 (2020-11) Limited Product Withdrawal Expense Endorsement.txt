CA 25 49 (2020-11) — Limited Product Withdrawal Expense Endorsement
====================================================================
Form number: CA 25 49
Edition: 2020-11 (most states); 2013-10 (HI and VI only)
Title: Limited Product Withdrawal Expense Endorsement
Type: ISO Commercial Auto endorsement
States: All

Policy term: Matches underlying CA 00 25 Auto Dealers Coverage Form
Transaction types: New business; endorsement addition

Purpose:
Reimburses the named insured for expenses incurred in withdrawing or
recalling a product from the market. This is a first-party expense
reimbursement coverage — it does not provide third-party liability
coverage or defense costs.

Captures:
- Reimbursement of product withdrawal expenses as defined in the
  endorsement, subject to the limits and conditions on the schedule
- Applies to covered autos and other products associated with the
  dealer's operations as described in the endorsement

When used:
- Auto dealers seeking coverage for the costs of notifying customers
  and retrieving products subject to a recall or withdrawal
- Dealers with parts sales or accessory operations where recall expenses
  are a realistic exposure

Notes:
- Does NOT provide liability coverage or defense cost coverage — expense
  reimbursement only
- Subject to an aggregate limit, deductible, participation percentage,
  and cut-off date, all of which must be entered on the endorsement
  schedule
- Hawaii and Virgin Islands use the older 2013-10 edition; all other
  states and territories use the 2020-11 edition
