CA 25 67 (2013-10) — Extended Reporting Period Endorsement – Employee Benefits Liability Coverage
=================================================================================================
Form number: CA 25 67
Edition: 2013-10
Title: Extended Reporting Period Endorsement – Employee Benefits Liability Coverage
Type: ISO Commercial Auto endorsement
States: All

Policy term: Matches underlying CA 00 25 Auto Dealers Coverage Form with CA 25 48 (Employee Benefits Liability Coverage) attached
Transaction types: Endorsement addition (upon policy cancellation or non-renewal)

Purpose:
Provides a tail period following policy cancellation or non-renewal
during which claims can be reported for acts, errors, or omissions
in the administration of the dealer's employee benefits programs that
occurred during the policy period. Attaches to CA 25 48 (Employee
Benefits Liability Coverage), which is written on a claims-made basis.

Captures:
- Extended reporting period for Employee Benefits Liability claims
  under CA 25 48 after the policy terminates
- Claims arising from benefits administration errors during the policy
  period may be reported during the tail period

When used:
- Dealers who carried CA 25 48 Employee Benefits Liability Coverage
  and are canceling or non-renewing the policy
- Situations where the dealer needs to preserve the ability to report
  late-discovered benefits administration claims

Notes:
- The schedule must specify the limit of insurance, aggregate limit,
  retained limit, retroactive date, and tail premium
- Applies only on a claims-made basis consistent with CA 25 48
