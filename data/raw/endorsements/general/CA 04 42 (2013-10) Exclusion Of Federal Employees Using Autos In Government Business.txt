CA 04 42 (2013-10) — Exclusion Of Federal Employees Using Autos In Government Business
======================================================================================
Form number: CA 04 42
Edition: 2013-10
Title: Exclusion Of Federal Employees Using Autos In Government Business
Type: ISO Commercial Auto endorsement
States: All

Policy term: Matches underlying policy
Transaction types: New business; renewal; midterm add

Purpose:
Excludes liability coverage under a CA 00 01 policy for claims arising from
the use of covered autos by federal government employees while acting in the
course of their official government employment. Used when a contractor's
policy would otherwise be exposed to claims for which the U.S. Government is
the appropriate insurer under the Federal Tort Claims Act or a government
fleet program.

Captures:
- Exclusion of BI and PD liability coverage for any loss or claim arising
  from a federal employee's use of a covered auto while acting within the
  scope of official government duties
- Removes the carrier's obligation to defend or indemnify in such claims

When used:
- Government contractors whose employees include federal workers who
  occasionally use the contractor's vehicles in the performance of government
  contracts
- Federal agencies or contractors whose vehicles are also used by government
  employees — the government carries its own fleet coverage and this
  endorsement prevents double coverage
- Required by some federal contract terms to prevent the government's exposure
  from being shifted to the contractor's carrier

Notes:
- The Federal Tort Claims Act (FTCA) makes the U.S. Government the insurer
  for torts committed by federal employees in the course of employment;
  this endorsement prevents the contractor's CA policy from being reached
  before the FTCA
- Does not affect coverage for non-government employees of the named insured
  using the same vehicles
- Narrow in application — primarily relevant to government contractors and
  federal agency partnerships; not a standard exclusion for typical
  commercial accounts
