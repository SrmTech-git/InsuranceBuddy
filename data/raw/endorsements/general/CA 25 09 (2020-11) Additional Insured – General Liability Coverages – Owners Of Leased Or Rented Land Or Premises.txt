CA 25 09 (2020-11) — Additional Insured – General Liability Coverages – Owners Of Leased Or Rented Land Or Premises
====================================================================================================================
Form number: CA 25 09
Edition: 2020-11
Title: Additional Insured – General Liability Coverages – Owners Of Leased Or Rented Land Or Premises
Type: ISO Commercial Auto endorsement
States: All

Policy term: Matches underlying CA 00 25 Auto Dealers Coverage Form
Transaction types: New business; endorsement addition

Purpose:
Adds the owner or lessor of land or premises leased or rented by the
dealer as an additional insured under the General Liability coverages of
CA 00 25. This is the CA 00 25 analog to CG 20 11 (Additional Insured —
Managers or Lessors of Premises) in the CGL program.

Captures:
- Additional insured status for the scheduled landlord/premises owner
  under Section II — General Liability Coverages
- Coverage limited to liability arising out of the named insured's
  occupancy or use of the described premises or land

When used:
- Auto dealers whose landlord requires additional insured status as a
  condition of the lease
- Common for dealers who lease showroom, service, or lot space from a
  third-party property owner

Notes:
- Does not extend to Covered Autos Liability, Physical Damage, or
  Garagekeepers sections — GL coverage only
- Does not cover the landlord's independent acts or pre-existing
  conditions at the premises; coverage is tied to the dealer's own
  liability
- The 2020-11 edition reflects the November 2020 ISO revision cycle
