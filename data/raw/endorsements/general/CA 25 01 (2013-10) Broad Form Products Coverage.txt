CA 25 01 (2013-10) — Broad Form Products Coverage
=================================================
Form number: CA 25 01
Edition: 2013-10
Title: Broad Form Products Coverage
Type: ISO Commercial Auto endorsement
States: All

Policy term: Matches underlying CA 00 25 Auto Dealers Coverage Form
Transaction types: New business; endorsement addition

Purpose:
Removes the Defective Products exclusion (Exclusion 2.h.) from the Bodily
Injury and Property Damage Liability section of the CA 00 25 General
Liability Coverages. Without this endorsement, property damage caused by
defects in the dealer's own products is excluded. With it, that coverage
is reinstated, subject to a $500 per-accident threshold for property
damage to the dealer's products.

Captures:
- Removal of Exclusion 2.h. (Defective Products) from Section II —
  General Liability Coverages of CA 00 25
- Coverage applies to property damage to the dealer's products that
  exceeds $500 for any one accident, subject to the GL BI/PD each
  accident limit
- Bodily injury from defective products is covered without the $500
  threshold

When used:
- Auto dealers under CA 00 25 who want products liability coverage
  for defective products claims
- Dealers with parts sales, accessories, or service operations where
  product defect claims are a realistic exposure

Notes:
- The $500 property damage threshold per accident applies specifically
  to property damage to the dealer's own products — it does not apply
  to bodily injury claims
- This endorsement modifies Section II (General Liability) of CA 00 25,
  not the covered autos liability section
