CA 27 16 (2020-11) — Exclusion – Cross Suits Liability For General Liability Coverages
========================================================================================
Form number: CA 27 16
Edition: 2020-11
Title: Exclusion – Cross Suits Liability For General Liability Coverages
Type: ISO Commercial Auto endorsement
States: All (multi-state)

Policy term: Matches underlying CA 00 25 Auto Dealers Coverage Form
Transaction types: New business; endorsement addition

Purpose:
Eliminates General Liability coverage for suits brought by one named
insured against another named insured on the same CA 00 25 policy.
Prevents inter-entity claims within a dealer group from triggering
GL coverage.

Captures:
- Exclusion of any claim or suit by one named insured against another
  named insured under Section II — General Liability Coverages

When used:
- Auto dealer groups with multiple entities all named on a single
  CA 00 25 policy, where inter-company suits could otherwise trigger
  GL coverage
- Insurers who want to eliminate the risk of intra-group litigation
  being funded by the GL section

Notes:
- Part of the November 2020 ISO Auto Dealers form revision cycle
