CA 25 51 (2020-11) — Exclusion – Designated Products
=====================================================
Form number: CA 25 51
Edition: 2020-11
Title: Exclusion – Designated Products
Type: ISO Commercial Auto endorsement
States: All

Policy term: Matches underlying CA 00 25 Auto Dealers Coverage Form
Transaction types: New business; endorsement addition

Purpose:
Excludes bodily injury or property damage arising from specifically
designated products listed on the endorsement schedule. Provides a
surgical, schedule-driven alternative to a blanket products exclusion.

Captures:
- Exclusion of BI/PD claims arising from products listed on the
  endorsement schedule when the damage occurs off the dealer's premises
- Only scheduled products are excluded; unscheduled products retain
  coverage under Section II — General Liability Coverages

When used:
- Dealers or insurers who want to carve out a specific product line
  (e.g., a particular parts brand or accessory type) while retaining
  GL products coverage for all other products
- Situations where certain products present disproportionate claims
  exposure warranting separate handling

Notes:
- Exclusion applies to off-premises damage from designated products;
  on-premises incidents may still be covered under the premises liability
  component of Section II
- The schedule must identify the specific products to be excluded
