CA 99 48 (2013-10) — Pollution Liability – Broadened Coverage For Covered Autos – Business Auto And Motor Carrier Coverage Forms
================================================================================================================================
Form number: CA 99 48
Edition: 2013-10
Title: Pollution Liability – Broadened Coverage For Covered Autos – Business Auto And Motor Carrier Coverage Forms
Type: ISO Commercial Auto endorsement
States: All

Policy term: Matches underlying CA 00 01 or CA 00 20 policy
Transaction types: New business; endorsement addition

Purpose:
Broadens the pollution liability coverage for covered autos under the
CA 00 01 Business Auto Coverage Form or CA 00 20 Motor Carrier Coverage
Form by removing or modifying the standard pollution exclusion. The
standard commercial auto liability forms exclude pollution liability
arising from the discharge, dispersal, or release of pollutants from
covered autos — relevant for fuel spills, cargo releases, and hazmat
incidents. This endorsement provides coverage for pollution events arising
from the operation, use, or maintenance of covered autos.

Captures:
- Liability coverage for bodily injury and property damage arising from
  pollution released from or connected to covered autos
- Covers fuel spills, fluid releases, and cargo pollution events
  associated with covered auto operations
- Removes the auto-connected pollution exclusion from CA 00 01 / CA 00 20

When used:
- Trucking and motor carrier operations transporting petroleum products,
  chemicals, or other pollutants
- Business auto fleets with fuel tanks, hydraulic systems, or other
  fluid systems that create pollution spill risk
- Any CA 00 01 or CA 00 20 program where the standard pollution exclusion
  creates a meaningful coverage gap for auto-connected pollution events

Notes:
- The standard commercial auto pollution exclusion is broad; fuel spills
  from vehicle accidents are a common, real-world claim scenario that this
  endorsement covers
- Compare with CA 99 55, which is the equivalent broadened pollution
  coverage endorsement for the CA 00 25 Auto Dealers Coverage Form
- For cargo pollution (vs. vehicle-connected pollution), separate motor
  truck cargo or pollution liability coverage may also be needed
