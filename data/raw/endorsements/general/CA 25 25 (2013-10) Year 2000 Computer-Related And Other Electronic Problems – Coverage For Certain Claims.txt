CA 25 25 (2013-10) — Year 2000 Computer-Related And Other Electronic Problems – Coverage For Certain Claims
===========================================================================================================
Form number: CA 25 25
Edition: 2013-10
Title: Year 2000 Computer-Related And Other Electronic Problems – Coverage For Certain Claims
Type: ISO Commercial Auto endorsement
States: All

Policy term: Matches underlying policy (HISTORICAL — no longer used in active placement)
Transaction types: Historical reference only

Purpose:
Provided affirmative Y2K coverage for specifically defined, limited categories
of claims under the CA 00 25 GL section. The counterpart to the exclusion
forms in the Y2K series — where CA 25 18-24 excluded or limited Y2K coverage,
this form provided it for certain scheduled claim types.

Captures:
- (Historical) Affirmative liability coverage for scheduled Y2K-related
  claims under CA 00 25 general liability coverages

When used:
- HISTORICAL ONLY — no current use
- See CA 25 18 for full Y2K series context
