CA 02 38 (2013-10) — Reinstatement Of Insurance
===============================================
Form number: CA 02 38
Edition: 2013-10
Title: Reinstatement Of Insurance
Type: ISO Commercial Auto endorsement
States: All

Policy term: Attached for the reinstatement transaction only
Transaction types: Policy reinstatement after cancellation

Purpose:
Reinstates coverage on autos that were previously suspended under CA 02 40
(Suspension of Insurance). CA 02 38 is the companion reinstatement form to
CA 02 40 — it is not a post-cancellation reinstatement tool. The endorsement
establishes the reinstatement effective date and lists the specific autos
being reinstated on a schedule; only those autos listed on the schedule have
their coverage restored. Applies to Business Auto, Garage, and Motor Carrier
coverage forms.

Captures:
- Reinstatement effective date for suspended coverage
- Schedule of specific autos being reinstated (only autos listed are
  reinstated; others remain suspended)
- Any applicable premium charges for the reinstated coverage period

When used:
- Insured is returning a previously suspended vehicle to service and needs
  coverage restored under CA 02 40
- Fleet vehicles completing extended storage, repair, or rebuild periods
- Seasonal equipment returning to active use after a lay-up suspension

Notes:
- Reinstatement under CA 02 38 is NOT automatic — the insured or agent must
  proactively request and attach this endorsement; forgetting to do so leaves
  the vehicle uninsured upon return to service
- Coverage resumes from the CA 02 38 effective date only; no retroactive
  coverage for the suspension period
- Any premium charges for reinstated coverage become due upon reinstatement
