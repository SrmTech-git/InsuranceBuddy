CA 26 04 (2020-11) Additional Coverage Endorsement – Single Interest Automobile Physical Damage Insurance Policy
==================================================================================================================
Form number: CA 26 04
Edition: 2020-11
Title: Additional Coverage Endorsement – Single Interest Automobile Physical Damage Insurance Policy
Type: ISO Commercial Auto endorsement
States: All

Policy term: Matches underlying CA 26 01 or CA 26 02 policy
Transaction types: New business; program addition

Purpose:
Amends the CA 26 01 or CA 26 02 single interest physical damage policy to
exclude coverage when the financed vehicle is being used for public or
livery passenger conveyance or on-demand delivery services (ride-share,
transportation network company use, food/package delivery). The confirmed
ISO title is "Amendment of Single Interest Policy Provisions – Public or
Livery Passenger Conveyance and On-Demand Delivery Services Exclusion."
It removes coverage for physical damage losses that occur during those
commercial-use activities.

Captures:
- Exclusion of physical damage coverage under the single interest policy
  when the financed vehicle is being operated as a livery/ride-share vehicle
  or for on-demand delivery services
- Protects the lender-placed insurer from claims arising from commercial
  use that the borrower undertook without obtaining appropriate commercial
  auto or TNC coverage

When used:
- Lender CPI programs where the insurer excludes claims arising from
  ride-share or gig-economy delivery use by the borrower
- Any single interest program where TNC/delivery-use exposure is carved
  out of the physical damage coverage

Notes:
- This is an EXCLUSION endorsement — it removes coverage for commercial
  gig-economy vehicle use; it does not add coverages; the file title
  "Additional Coverage Endorsement" does not match the confirmed ISO
  form content
- Confirmed ISO title: "Amendment of Single Interest Policy Provisions –
  Public or Livery Passenger Conveyance and On-Demand Delivery Services
  Exclusion"
- Borrowers using a financed personal vehicle for ride-share or delivery
  without appropriate commercial/TNC coverage create a gap that this
  endorsement specifically carves out of the lender's CPI policy
