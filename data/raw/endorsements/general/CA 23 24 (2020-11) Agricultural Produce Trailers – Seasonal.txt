CA 23 24 (2020-11) — Agricultural Produce Trailers – Seasonal
=============================================================
Form number: CA 23 24
Edition: 2020-11
Title: Agricultural Produce Trailers – Seasonal
Type: ISO Commercial Auto endorsement
States: All

Policy term: Matches underlying policy (seasonal use periods)
Transaction types: New business; renewal; midterm add

Purpose:
Provides commercial auto coverage for agricultural produce trailers that are
used only seasonally. These trailers are typically pulled by farm tractors
on private property during harvest and may also be towed on public roads
for short distances; their seasonal, dual-use nature (farm equipment vs.
auto) can create classification ambiguity. This endorsement establishes CA
coverage for the defined seasonal use period.

Captures:
- Liability and physical damage coverage for scheduled agricultural produce
  trailers during defined seasonal use periods
- Coverage applies while trailers are in use for produce transport during
  harvest and related operations

When used:
- Agricultural operations and farms that haul produce by trailer during
  harvest season
- Any insured with seasonal trailers that are put away between harvests
  and need coverage only during active use periods

Notes:
- Seasonal coverage means premium is typically pro-rated for the active
  use period; trailers idle in a barn during off-season may be unscheduled
  or suspended
- Agricultural trailers used exclusively on private property may not need
  CA coverage (they are mobile equipment on private land); this endorsement
  addresses trailers that also travel on public roads during harvest season
- Farm Bureau and agricultural insurance markets sometimes handle these
  trailers under farmowners policies; CA 23 24 provides the equivalent
  coverage under a standard commercial auto program
