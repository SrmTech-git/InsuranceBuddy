CA 27 18 (2020-11) — Automatic Insured Status For Newly Acquired Or Formed Limited Liability Companies – Other Than Covered Auto Coverages
=============================================================================================================================================
Form number: CA 27 18
Edition: 2020-11
Title: Automatic Insured Status For Newly Acquired Or Formed Limited Liability Companies – Other Than Covered Auto Coverages
Type: ISO Commercial Auto endorsement
States: All (multi-state)

Policy term: Matches underlying CA 00 25 Auto Dealers Coverage Form
Transaction types: New business; endorsement addition

Purpose:
Grants automatic insured status under the non-auto coverages of the
CA 00 25 (General Liability, Garagekeepers, AEO) to newly acquired or
formed LLCs, without requiring an immediate endorsement to add the
entity. Applies to other-than-covered-auto coverages only.

Captures:
- Automatic extension of insured status to a newly acquired or formed
  LLC under Section II (GL), Section III (AEO), and Garagekeepers
  coverages of CA 00 25 for a limited automatic period
- Coverage ends at the earliest of: 90 days after the LLC is formed
  or acquired, the end of the policy period, or the date the LLC is
  formally added to the policy by endorsement

When used:
- Dealer groups that regularly form or acquire new LLCs and need
  immediate automatic GL/AEO/Garagekeepers coverage before the policy
  can be formally endorsed
- Situations where the covered autos liability automatic extension is
  not the concern, but the non-auto GL exposure is

Notes:
- Does not apply to Covered Autos Liability — that section has its own
  automatic coverage provisions in the base form
- Part of the November 2020 ISO Auto Dealers form revision cycle
