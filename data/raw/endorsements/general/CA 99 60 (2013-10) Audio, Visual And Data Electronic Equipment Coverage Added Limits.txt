CA 99 60 (2013-10) — Audio, Visual And Data Electronic Equipment Coverage Added Limits
======================================================================================
Form number: CA 99 60
Edition: 2013-10
Title: Audio, Visual And Data Electronic Equipment Coverage Added Limits
Type: ISO Commercial Auto endorsement
States: All

Policy term: Matches underlying commercial auto policy
Transaction types: New business; endorsement addition

Purpose:
Adds coverage and/or increases limits for audio, visual, and data
electronic equipment installed in covered autos — including equipment
permanently installed and portable equipment used in the vehicle. The
standard commercial auto physical damage forms limit coverage for
electronic equipment that is not standard factory equipment. For
commercial vehicles with significant investments in non-standard
electronics (GPS, communications systems, data terminals, cameras,
audio systems), this endorsement provides coverage at specified limits
for that equipment.

Captures:
- Coverage for audio, visual, and data electronic equipment permanently
  installed in covered autos
- Coverage for portable electronic equipment regularly used in covered autos
- Added or increased limits beyond the base policy's electronic equipment
  sublimit
- Applies to equipment that is part of the covered auto's use profile
  (trucking communications, route data terminals, dashcams, etc.)

When used:
- Commercial vehicles with significant non-standard electronic equipment
  (GPS fleet management systems, communications equipment, data terminals)
- Specialty vehicles equipped with cameras, sensors, or recording equipment
- Any commercial auto policy where the base electronic equipment sublimit
  is insufficient for the actual equipment investment

Notes:
- The base commercial auto form includes a $1,000 sublimit for audio,
  visual, and data electronic equipment that is not standard factory
  equipment; CA 99 60 allows scheduling higher per-loss limits above
  that $1,000 threshold for specific vehicles
- The endorsement requires identification of covered equipment categories
  or specific items and applicable limits
- Compare with CA 99 59 (Garagekeepers Coverage – Customers' Sound-Receiving
  Equipment) for equipment in customer vehicles in garagekeepers custody
