CA P 009 (2016-11) — 2016 Commercial Auto Miscellaneous Form Revisions Advisory Notice To Policyholders
=======================================================================================================
Form number: CA P 009
Edition: 2016-11
Title: 2016 Commercial Auto Miscellaneous Form Revisions Advisory Notice To Policyholders
Type: ISO Commercial Auto policyholder advisory notice
States: All

Purpose:
Advisory notice explaining miscellaneous commercial auto form revisions
made outside the major program revision cycles — specifically the 2016-11
mid-cycle updates. Between the 2013-10 and 2020-11 major revisions, ISO
filed targeted updates addressing specific coverage questions, state
regulatory requirements, or emerging risk developments. This notice
explains those 2016-11 targeted changes to policyholders.

Content:
- Summary of miscellaneous form changes in the 2016-11 filing
- Explanation of specific endorsements or provisions revised
- Impact on existing commercial auto policyholders

When used:
- Issued with policies affected by the 2016-11 miscellaneous revisions
- Satisfies state notice requirements for mid-cycle form changes

Notes:
- Between major ISO program revisions (2013-10 and 2020-11), targeted
  updates are less frequent than major revisions; CA P 009 covers one
  such targeted update cycle
- The 2016-11 revisions are now superseded by the 2020-11 major revision;
  this notice is of historical interest for policies on the 2016-11 forms
- Agents reviewing a client's current commercial auto program should
  confirm whether forms were updated to 2020-11 at last renewal
