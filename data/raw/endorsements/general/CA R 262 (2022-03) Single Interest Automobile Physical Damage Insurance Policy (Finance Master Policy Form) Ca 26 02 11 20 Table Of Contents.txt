CA R 262 (2022-03) — Single Interest Automobile Physical Damage Insurance Policy (Finance Master Policy Form) Ca 26 02 11 20 Table Of Contents
==============================================================================================================================================
Form number: CA R 262
Edition: 2022-03
Title: Single Interest Automobile Physical Damage Insurance Policy (Finance Master Policy Form) Ca 26 02 11 20 Table Of Contents
Type: ISO Commercial Auto form table of contents
States: All

Purpose:
Table of contents for the CA 26 02 (11 20) Single Interest Automobile
Physical Damage Insurance Policy — Finance Master Policy Form. Provides
a structural index to the master single-interest CPI policy used by
lenders to cover their entire auto loan portfolio under a blanket program,
rather than individual per-vehicle policies. Helps lenders, program
administrators, and claims adjusters navigate the CA 26 02 master policy
form, which is the more common structure for large-volume CPI programs.

Content:
- Section index for CA 26 02 (2020-11 edition): coverage agreement,
  definitions, portfolio-wide coverage triggers, exclusions, conditions,
  lender and borrower notification requirements
- Page references for each section of the master single-interest policy
- Reference to endorsement attachment points for CA 26 04-07

When used:
- Issued with CA 26 02 single-interest master policy programs as a
  navigation reference for lenders and program administrators
- Used by CPI program adjusters handling master policy portfolio claims

Notes:
- CA R 262 numbering aligns with CA 26 02 (262 = CA 26 02); companion to
  CA R 261 (for the CA 26 01 individual policy form)
- The master policy form (CA 26 02) is the standard structure for
  bank and credit union CPI programs covering large auto loan portfolios;
  the individual policy form (CA 26 01) is used for specific high-value loans
- Program administrators for large CPI programs should use this table of
  contents to ensure claims staff can navigate the master policy efficiently
