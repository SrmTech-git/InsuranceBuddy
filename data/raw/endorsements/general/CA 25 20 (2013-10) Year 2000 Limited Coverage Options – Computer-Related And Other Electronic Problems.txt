CA 25 20 (2013-10) — Year 2000 Limited Coverage Options – Computer-Related And Other Electronic Problems
========================================================================================================
Form number: CA 25 20
Edition: 2013-10
Title: Year 2000 Limited Coverage Options – Computer-Related And Other Electronic Problems
Type: ISO Commercial Auto endorsement
States: All

Policy term: Matches underlying policy (HISTORICAL — no longer used in active placement)
Transaction types: Historical reference only

Purpose:
Provided limited, optional Y2K coverage for auto dealers who wanted some
protection against Y2K-related BI/PD claims rather than a total exclusion.
Offered a tiered coverage option as an alternative to the blanket exclusions
in CA 25 18-19.

Captures:
- (Historical) Limited liability coverage options for Y2K-related claims
  under CA 00 25 general liability coverages

When used:
- HISTORICAL ONLY — no current use
- See CA 25 18 for full Y2K series context
