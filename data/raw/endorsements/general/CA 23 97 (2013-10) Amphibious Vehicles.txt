CA 23 97 (2013-10) — Amphibious Vehicles
========================================
Form number: CA 23 97
Edition: 2013-10
Title: Amphibious Vehicles
Type: ISO Commercial Auto endorsement
States: All

Policy term: Matches underlying policy
Transaction types: New business; renewal; midterm add

Purpose:
Excludes coverage for bodily injury, property damage, and physical damage
arising from the operation of an amphibious vehicle while in water mode.
The base commercial auto policy may cover amphibious vehicles on land;
this endorsement removes coverage for the water-mode exposure, which is
considered a watercraft/marine risk rather than an auto risk.

Captures:
- Exclusion of BI/PD liability and physical damage for amphibious vehicles
  while operating on water
- Land-mode auto coverage is unaffected; only water-mode operation is excluded

When used:
- Policies where the underwriter is willing to insure amphibious vehicles
  for road use but not for water operations
- Accounts where water-mode coverage is intended to be placed separately
  under a marine or inland marine policy

Notes:
- This is an EXCLUSION endorsement — it removes water-mode coverage, it
  does not extend coverage to water operation; a card or summary describing
  it as providing dual land/water coverage would be incorrect
- Operators of passenger-carrying amphibious vehicles (Duck Boats) need
  separate marine/vessel liability coverage for water-mode operation
- Coast Guard regulations apply when amphibious vehicles operate on
  navigable waterways; the CA policy with this endorsement does not
  satisfy those requirements for water-mode operation
- Past Duck Boat accidents (Philadelphia 1999, Seattle 2015, Branson 2018)
  illustrate the severity of water-mode losses in this class
