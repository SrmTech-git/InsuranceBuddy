CA 20 54 (2020-11) — Employee Hired Autos
=========================================
Form number: CA 20 54
Edition: 2020-11
Title: Employee Hired Autos
Type: ISO Commercial Auto endorsement
States: All

Policy term: Matches underlying policy
Transaction types: New business; renewal; midterm add

Purpose:
Extends hired auto physical damage coverage to autos rented by an employee
(in the employee's own name) for the named insured's business. Under standard
CA 00 01 hired auto physical damage, the coverage applies when the named
insured (the business) is the rental contracting party. When employees rent
cars personally and the company reimburses them, the business is not the
renter — creating a physical damage coverage gap this endorsement fills.

Captures:
- Physical damage (comprehensive and collision) for vehicles rented by
  an employee in their personal capacity for the named insured's business
  purposes
- Coverage applies as if the named insured had rented the vehicle directly

When used:
- Companies with employee travel programs where employees book rental cars
  on personal credit cards (not corporate accounts) and expense them
- Any account where employees regularly rent vehicles for business travel
  without using a centralized corporate rental account
- Especially important where the employee's personal auto policy excludes
  rental vehicles rented for business (some PAPs have this limitation)

Notes:
- Without this endorsement, physical damage to a rental car rented by an
  employee personally falls outside the CA policy's hired auto coverage —
  the business may face exposure through the rental company's damage waiver
  or through the employee's PAP (which may not cover business rentals)
- This is a physical damage endorsement; liability for accidents in
  employee-rented vehicles is typically covered under the base CA 00 01
  hired auto liability (Symbol 8) when the employee uses the vehicle for
  business
- Some carriers price this endorsement separately; others include it as
  part of a hired auto physical damage package
