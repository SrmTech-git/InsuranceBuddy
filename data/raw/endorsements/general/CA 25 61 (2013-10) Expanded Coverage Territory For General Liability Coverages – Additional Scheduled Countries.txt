CA 25 61 (2013-10) — Expanded Coverage Territory For General Liability Coverages – Additional Scheduled Countries
=================================================================================================================
Form number: CA 25 61
Edition: 2013-10
Title: Expanded Coverage Territory For General Liability Coverages – Additional Scheduled Countries
Type: ISO Commercial Auto endorsement
States: All

Policy term: Matches underlying CA 00 25 Auto Dealers Coverage Form
Transaction types: New business; endorsement addition

Purpose:
A targeted alternative to CA 25 60 (Worldwide General Liability
Coverages). Expands the GL coverage territory to include only the
specific countries listed on the endorsement schedule, rather than
granting full worldwide coverage.

Captures:
- Replaces the Coverage Territory definition with one that includes the
  standard U.S./territories/Canada territory plus any country
  specifically named on the endorsement schedule
- Applies to Section II — General Liability Coverages only

When used:
- Dealers who regularly export vehicles or have warranty/service
  obligations in specific foreign markets (e.g., Mexico, Caribbean
  nations) without needing — or being able to obtain — full worldwide
  coverage
- Insurers who want to grant country-by-country territory expansion for
  more precise premium rating and risk selection

Notes:
- Suit requirement retained: suits must be brought in the U.S., its
  territories/possessions, or Canada
- Unlisted countries remain outside the coverage territory
- CA 25 60 is the alternative when full worldwide coverage is needed
