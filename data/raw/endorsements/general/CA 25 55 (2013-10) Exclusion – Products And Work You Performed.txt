CA 25 55 (2013-10) — Exclusion – Products And Work You Performed
================================================================
Form number: CA 25 55
Edition: 2013-10
Title: Exclusion – Products And Work You Performed
Type: ISO Commercial Auto endorsement
States: All

Policy term: Matches underlying CA 00 25 Auto Dealers Coverage Form
Transaction types: New business; endorsement addition

Purpose:
Excludes all off-premises bodily injury or property damage arising from
the named insured's products or work performed, removing both the
products liability and completed operations components from Section II
— General Liability Coverages. This is a blanket, schedule-free
exclusion covering both exposure types entirely.

Captures:
- Exclusion of off-premises BI/PD arising from any of the named
  insured's products (e.g., vehicles sold with defective parts that
  cause injury after delivery)
- Exclusion of off-premises BI/PD arising from any work the named
  insured performed (e.g., a repair job that fails and causes damage
  elsewhere after leaving the shop)

When used:
- Dealers where products liability and completed operations exposure is
  being written on a separate standalone policy
- Insurers who want to remove these exposures entirely from the CA 00 25
  GL section

Notes:
- On-premises incidents may still be covered under the premises liability
  component of Section II
- CA 25 56 is the scheduled alternative — it excludes only designated
  work types rather than all products and work
