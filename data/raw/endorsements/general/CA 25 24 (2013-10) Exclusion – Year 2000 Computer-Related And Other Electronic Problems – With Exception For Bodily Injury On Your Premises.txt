CA 25 24 (2013-10) — Exclusion – Year 2000 Computer-Related And Other Electronic Problems – With Exception For Bodily Injury On Your Premises
=============================================================================================================================================
Form number: CA 25 24
Edition: 2013-10
Title: Exclusion – Year 2000 Computer-Related And Other Electronic Problems – With Exception For Bodily Injury On Your Premises
Type: ISO Commercial Auto endorsement
States: All

Policy term: Matches underlying policy (HISTORICAL — no longer used in active placement)
Transaction types: Historical reference only

Purpose:
A Y2K exclusion variant that excluded most Y2K-related liability but retained
coverage for bodily injury claims occurring on the insured's premises
(e.g., a customer injured at the dealership due to a Y2K-triggered system
failure). A narrow coverage carve-back within the broader exclusion.

Captures:
- (Historical) Y2K exclusion with a BI-on-premises exception under CA 00 25 GL

When used:
- HISTORICAL ONLY — no current use
- See CA 25 18 for full Y2K series context
