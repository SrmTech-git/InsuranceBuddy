CA 25 19 (2013-10) — Exclusion – Year 2000 Computer-Related And Other Electronic Problems – Products/Work You Performed
=======================================================================================================================
Form number: CA 25 19
Edition: 2013-10
Title: Exclusion – Year 2000 Computer-Related And Other Electronic Problems – Products/Work You Performed
Type: ISO Commercial Auto endorsement
States: All

Policy term: Matches underlying policy (HISTORICAL — no longer used in active placement)
Transaction types: Historical reference only

Purpose:
Excluded products liability coverage for BI or PD arising from Y2K-related
failures in the dealer's products or completed work. Targeted the products
and completed operations exposure specifically, as distinct from CA 25 18
which covered general premises/operations liability.

Captures:
- (Historical) Exclusion of Y2K-related products and completed operations
  claims under CA 00 25 general liability coverages

When used:
- HISTORICAL ONLY — no current use
- Companion to CA 25 18; the 18 covers operations, the 19 covers products
- See CA 25 18 for full Y2K series context
