CA 25 57 (2013-10) — Communicable Disease Exclusion For General Liability Coverages
====================================================================================
Form number: CA 25 57
Edition: 2013-10
Title: Communicable Disease Exclusion For General Liability Coverages
Type: ISO Commercial Auto endorsement
States: All except WA and IL

Policy term: Matches underlying CA 00 25 Auto Dealers Coverage Form
Transaction types: New business; endorsement addition

Purpose:
Excludes all claims arising from the actual or alleged transmission of
communicable diseases from all three subsections of Section II — General
Liability Coverages. The exclusion applies to the GL section only; the
covered autos liability section is not affected.

Captures:
- Exclusion of BI/PD arising from communicable disease transmission
  from Section II, Subsection A (Bodily Injury and Property Damage
  Liability)
- Exclusion of personal and advertising injury claims from communicable
  disease from Section II, Subsection B
- Exclusion of medical payments claims from communicable disease from
  Section II, Subsection C (Locations and Operations Medical Payments)

When used:
- Dealers and insurers seeking to limit GL exposure from communicable
  disease claims (became more widely deployed following COVID-19
  litigation)
- Situations where communicable disease exposure is addressed by a
  separate policy or excluded entirely

Notes:
- Blanket exclusion — no schedule, no disease-specific carve-outs
- Does not affect the auto liability section (Section I) of CA 00 25
- No evidence of a post-2013 revised ISO national edition; the 10 13
  edition appears to be the only published version as of 2024
- Not filed in Washington (WA) or Illinois (IL)
