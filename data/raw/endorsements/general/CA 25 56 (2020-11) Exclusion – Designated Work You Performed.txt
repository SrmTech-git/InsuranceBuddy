CA 25 56 (2020-11) — Exclusion – Designated Work You Performed
==============================================================
Form number: CA 25 56
Edition: 2020-11 (most states); 2013-10 (HI and VI only)
Title: Exclusion – Designated Work You Performed
Type: ISO Commercial Auto endorsement
States: All

Policy term: Matches underlying CA 00 25 Auto Dealers Coverage Form
Transaction types: New business; endorsement addition

Purpose:
A surgical, schedule-driven alternative to CA 25 55. Excludes off-premises
bodily injury or property damage arising only from work types specifically
listed on the endorsement schedule, leaving all other work-performed
exposure covered under Section II — General Liability Coverages.

Captures:
- Exclusion of off-premises BI/PD from completed operations for the
  specific categories of work listed on the endorsement schedule
- All unlisted work types retain coverage under Section II

When used:
- Dealers or insurers who want to carve out a specific high-risk work
  type (e.g., engine rebuilds, transmission overhauls) while retaining
  completed operations coverage for routine service work
- Situations where discrete work exposures need to be priced and managed
  separately rather than excluded wholesale

Notes:
- The schedule must identify the specific work descriptions to be
  excluded; unlisted work is not affected
- CA 25 55 is the blanket alternative that excludes all products and
  work-performed exposure
- Hawaii and Virgin Islands use the older 2013-10 edition; all other
  states and territories use the 2020-11 edition
