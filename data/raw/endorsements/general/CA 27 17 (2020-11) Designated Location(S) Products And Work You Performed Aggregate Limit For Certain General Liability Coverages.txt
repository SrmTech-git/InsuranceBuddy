CA 27 17 (2020-11) — Designated Location(s) Products And Work You Performed Aggregate Limit For Certain General Liability Coverages
=====================================================================================================================================
Form number: CA 27 17
Edition: 2020-11
Title: Designated Location(s) Products And Work You Performed Aggregate Limit For Certain General Liability Coverages
Type: ISO Commercial Auto endorsement
States: All (multi-state)

Policy term: Matches underlying CA 00 25 Auto Dealers Coverage Form
Transaction types: New business; endorsement addition

Purpose:
Creates a separate products and completed operations aggregate limit for
each location listed on the endorsement schedule, preventing losses at
one dealership from exhausting the products/work performed aggregate
available to all other locations.

Captures:
- Per-location products and completed operations aggregate limit for
  each scheduled location under Section II — General Liability Coverages
- Claims attributable to a scheduled location draw against that
  location's dedicated aggregate, not the policy-wide aggregate

When used:
- Auto dealer groups with multiple rooftops where service, parts, or
  repair operations at one location should not erode products/completed
  operations coverage for all other locations
- Multi-location dealer groups with materially different service
  operation risk profiles by location

Notes:
- Parallel in concept to CG 25 04 (Designated Location(s) General
  Aggregate Limit) in the standard CGL program, but specifically
  for the products and completed operations aggregate
- Part of the November 2020 ISO Auto Dealers form revision cycle
