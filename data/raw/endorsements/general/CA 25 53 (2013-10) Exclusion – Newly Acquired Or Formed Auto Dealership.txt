CA 25 53 (2013-10) — Exclusion – Newly Acquired Or Formed Auto Dealership
=========================================================================
Form number: CA 25 53
Edition: 2013-10
Title: Exclusion – Newly Acquired Or Formed Auto Dealership
Type: ISO Commercial Auto endorsement
States: All except WA

Policy term: Matches underlying CA 00 25 Auto Dealers Coverage Form
Transaction types: New business; endorsement addition

Purpose:
Removes the automatic insured status that the CA 00 25 base form would
otherwise extend to newly acquired or formed auto dealership entities
during the policy period. Any newly acquired or formed dealership
operation is flatly excluded — no automatic coverage extension applies.

Captures:
- Removal of automatic coverage for newly acquired or formed dealership
  entities from the "Who Is An Insured" provisions throughout the form
- Applies across both Covered Autos Liability and General Liability
  sections — the exclusion is policy-wide, not section-specific

When used:
- Insurers who want to reserve the right to underwrite any new dealership
  entity before extending coverage, rather than granting automatic
  mid-term coverage
- Dealers with corporate structures where acquisition of new rooftops
  is a realistic mid-term event

Notes:
- No schedule is required — the exclusion is absolute and covers any
  organization that would otherwise qualify as a newly acquired or
  formed dealership under the base form
- New dealership entities must be added by formal endorsement after
  underwriting review; coverage does not attach until then
- Not filed in Washington (WA)
