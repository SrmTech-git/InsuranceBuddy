CA 99 55 (2013-10) — Pollution Liability – Broadened Coverage For Covered Autos – Auto Dealers Coverage Form
============================================================================================================
Form number: CA 99 55
Edition: 2013-10
Title: Pollution Liability – Broadened Coverage For Covered Autos – Auto Dealers Coverage Form
Type: ISO Commercial Auto endorsement
States: All

Policy term: Matches underlying CA 00 25 Auto Dealers Coverage Form
Transaction types: New business; endorsement addition

Purpose:
Broadens the pollution liability coverage for covered autos under the
CA 00 25 Auto Dealers Coverage Form by removing or modifying the standard
auto pollution exclusion. Equivalent to CA 99 48 but specifically for
dealer operations under CA 00 25. Covers pollution events arising from
the operation, use, or maintenance of covered autos in the dealer context —
including new/used vehicle delivery, test drives, loaner operations, and
the movement of inventory. Also relevant for service department vehicles
and fuel system maintenance.

Captures:
- Liability for bodily injury and property damage arising from pollution
  released from or connected to covered autos in dealer operations
- Covers fuel spills, fluid releases, and vehicle-connected pollution
  events during dealer operations
- Removes the auto-connected pollution exclusion from the CA 00 25

When used:
- Auto dealers with covered auto pollution spill exposure (test drives,
  delivery runs, service department vehicle operations)
- Dealers in jurisdictions with active enforcement of environmental
  liability for petroleum spills
- Any CA 00 25 program where auto-connected pollution events are a
  meaningful risk

Notes:
- Distinct from CA 27 08 (Total Pollution Exclusion for Dealer GL) —
  this endorsement broadens auto pollution coverage; CA 27 08 tightens
  the GL pollution exclusion; the two forms address separate portions
  of the dealer's pollution exposure
- Compare with CA 99 48, which is the equivalent form for CA 00 01 /
  CA 00 20 (non-dealer commercial auto)
- Dealer fuel storage tanks, service pit drainage, and vehicle washing
  operations create additional pollution exposure beyond just covered autos;
  those are addressed under the dealer's premises environmental coverage
