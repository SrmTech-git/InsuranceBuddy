CA P 002 (2006-04) — 2006 Commercial Auto Multistate Forms Revision Advisory Notice To Policyholders
====================================================================================================
Form number: CA P 002
Edition: 2006-04
Title: 2006 Commercial Auto Multistate Forms Revision Advisory Notice To Policyholders
Type: ISO Commercial Auto policyholder advisory notice
States: All

Purpose:
Advisory notice explaining changes made in ISO's 2006 commercial auto
multistate forms revision. Notifies policyholders about what changed
in their commercial auto policy as a result of the 2006 program update —
including coverage enhancements, exclusion clarifications, and structural
changes to the base forms. Provides policyholders with a plain-language
explanation of how the 2006 revision affects their coverage compared to
prior editions.

Content:
- Explanation of material changes in the 2006 commercial auto program revision
- Coverage modifications affecting CA 00 01 and other base forms
- Summary of new or revised endorsements introduced in the 2006 filing
- Not a coverage document — no independent coverage effect

When used:
- Issued with policies renewing onto or newly written under the 2006 edition
  commercial auto forms to comply with state policyholder notice requirements

Notes:
- Each major ISO commercial auto program revision generates a corresponding
  CA P advisory notice; CA P 002 covers the 2006-04 revision
- Insurers in states requiring advance notice of form changes use these
  notices to satisfy that regulatory requirement
- The 2006 revision predates the 2010 trucking reform (CA P 005) and the
  current 2020-11 program; this notice is primarily of historical interest
  for policies still running on pre-2010 editions
