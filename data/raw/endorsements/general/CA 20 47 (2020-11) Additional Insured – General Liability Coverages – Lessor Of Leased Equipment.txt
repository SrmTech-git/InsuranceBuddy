CA 20 47 (2020-11) — Additional Insured – General Liability Coverages – Lessor Of Leased Equipment
==================================================================================================
Form number: CA 20 47
Edition: 2020-11
Title: Additional Insured – General Liability Coverages – Lessor Of Leased Equipment
Type: ISO Commercial Auto endorsement
States: All

Policy term: Matches underlying policy
Transaction types: New business; renewal; midterm add

Purpose:
Adds the lessor of leased equipment as an additional insured for the
non-auto liability (garage operations – other than covered autos) coverage
section of the named insured's commercial auto policy. Businesses that lease
equipment for their operations (lifts, compressors, diagnostic tools, etc.)
are commonly required by the equipment lessor to provide AI status on their
liability policy. Available for use with CA 00 01 (Business Auto) as well
as CA 00 25 (Auto Dealers Coverage Form).

Captures:
- Named equipment lessor added as additional insured for liability arising
  from the named insured's use or maintenance of the leased equipment
- AI status is limited to the operations/premises liability section — not
  the covered autos liability or garagekeepers sections
- Schedule identifies the lessor and description of leased equipment

When used:
- Businesses (including auto dealerships) that lease equipment from
  third-party equipment lessors whose agreements require AI coverage
- Any account with an equipment lease agreement requiring AI status on
  the liability policy

Notes:
- Available for use with both CA 00 01 and CA 00 25; not restricted to
  the Auto Dealers form
- The AI protection covers liability from the named insured's use or
  maintenance of the leased equipment — the lessor is NOT made an AI
  for auto liability or garagekeepers coverage
- CA 20 01 covers vehicle lessors (owner of vehicles); CA 20 47 covers
  equipment lessors (owner of tools/machinery); both may be needed
  simultaneously for large operations
