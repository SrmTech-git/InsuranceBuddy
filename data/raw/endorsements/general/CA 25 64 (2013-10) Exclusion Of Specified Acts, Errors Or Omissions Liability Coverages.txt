CA 25 64 (2013-10) — Exclusion Of Specified Acts, Errors Or Omissions Liability Coverages
==========================================================================================
Form number: CA 25 64
Edition: 2013-10
Title: Exclusion Of Specified Acts, Errors Or Omissions Liability Coverages
Type: ISO Commercial Auto endorsement
States: All

Policy term: Matches underlying CA 00 25 Auto Dealers Coverage Form
Transaction types: New business; endorsement addition

Purpose:
A surgical, schedule-driven alternative to CA 25 63. Removes only the
specific AEO sub-coverage categories listed on the endorsement schedule,
leaving all unscheduled AEO coverages intact under Section III.

Captures:
- Exclusion of only the AEO sub-coverages specifically identified in
  the endorsement schedule
- All unlisted AEO sub-coverages remain in force under Section III —
  Acts, Errors Or Omissions Liability Coverages

When used:
- Insurers who want to exclude specific high-risk AEO sub-coverages
  (e.g., Truth-in-Lending Act violations) while retaining coverage
  for other AEO activities (e.g., odometer disclosure errors)
- Situations where certain AEO exposures are being written separately
  while others remain on the CA 00 25

Notes:
- The endorsement schedule must identify the specific sub-coverages to
  be excluded; unlisted items are not affected
- CA 25 63 is the alternative for a total, blanket AEO exclusion
