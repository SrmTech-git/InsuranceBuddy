CA 04 41 (2020-11) — Replacement Cost Coverage – Private Passenger Types
========================================================================
Form number: CA 04 41
Edition: 2020-11
Title: Replacement Cost Coverage – Private Passenger Types
Type: ISO Commercial Auto endorsement
States: All

Policy term: Matches underlying policy
Transaction types: New business; renewal; midterm add

Purpose:
Changes the physical damage valuation basis for private passenger autos
from actual cash value (ACV) to replacement cost — the cost to replace the
damaged or stolen vehicle with a new vehicle of the same make, model, and
equipment without a deduction for depreciation. Eliminates the depreciation
penalty that insured organizations face when newer fleet vehicles are
totaled.

Captures:
- Replacement cost valuation for total loss settlements on private passenger
  type covered autos (sedans, SUVs, minivans, light trucks used as passenger
  vehicles)
- No deduction for depreciation on covered total losses
- Replacement cost benefit applies only when: (1) the auto has been owned
  24 months or less at time of loss, (2) the named insured is the original
  owner, and (3) the odometer shows fewer than 24,000 miles at time of loss
- Replacement cost is capped at 110% of the replaced vehicle's MSRP

When used:
- Company fleets of newer private passenger vehicles where ACV would leave
  the insured short on replacement cost
- Executives or key employees whose company vehicles represent a significant
  benefit
- Any account with high vehicle turnover and newer fleet composition where
  depreciation exposure is meaningful

Notes:
- Applies only to "private passenger types" — typically sedans, SUVs, station
  wagons, and light-duty pickups used for passenger transport; does not
  extend to commercial trucks, trailers, or specialized equipment
- The replacement cost benefit does not apply once the 24-month ownership or
  24,000-mile threshold is exceeded; ACV applies beyond those limits
- Premium impact is meaningful for newer fleets; the benefit diminishes as
  vehicles age and ACV approaches replacement cost
- This endorsement applies to physical damage coverage only; it has no effect
  on liability coverage limits
