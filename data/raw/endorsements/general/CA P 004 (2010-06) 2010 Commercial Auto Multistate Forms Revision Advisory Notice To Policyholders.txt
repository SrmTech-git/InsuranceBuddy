CA P 004 (2010-06) — 2010 Commercial Auto Multistate Forms Revision Advisory Notice To Policyholders
====================================================================================================
Form number: CA P 004
Edition: 2010-06
Title: 2010 Commercial Auto Multistate Forms Revision Advisory Notice To Policyholders
Type: ISO Commercial Auto policyholder advisory notice
States: All

Purpose:
Advisory notice explaining the 2010 commercial auto multistate forms revision
to policyholders on CA 00 01 and related forms. The 2010 revision updated
the Business Auto Coverage Form and related endorsements; this notice
explains what changed for policyholders moving to the 2010 edition,
including coverage clarifications and structural changes to the symbol
system, pollution exclusions, and other program elements.

Content:
- Summary of material changes in the 2010 commercial auto forms revision
- Explanation of coverage modifications to CA 00 01 and the non-trucking
  commercial auto program
- Clarifications to definitions, exclusions, and coverage triggers

When used:
- Issued with policies renewing onto or newly written under the 2010 edition
  commercial auto forms

Notes:
- The 2010 revision is a predecessor to the current 2020-11 program; this
  notice is of historical interest for policies running on 2010 editions
  and for understanding the evolution of the current program
- A separate CA P 005 was issued concurrently to address the trucking-specific
  aspect of the 2010 revision (replacement of the Truckers form with the
  Motor Carrier Coverage Form)
- Insurers who moved policyholders to the 2013-10 or 2020-11 editions
  should have issued the corresponding later CA P notices
