CA 20 15 (2020-11) — Mobile Equipment
=====================================
Form number: CA 20 15
Edition: 2020-11
Title: Mobile Equipment
Type: ISO Commercial Auto endorsement
States: All

Policy term: Matches underlying policy
Transaction types: New business; renewal; midterm add

Purpose:
Reclassifies certain mobile equipment — vehicles that would normally be
treated as "mobile equipment" outside the CA auto definition — as covered
autos under the commercial auto policy. The ISO coverage system splits
vehicles between "autos" (CA policy) and "mobile equipment" (CGL policy);
some equipment falls in a gray area and this endorsement resolves ambiguity
by explicitly bringing the scheduled items into the CA program.

Captures:
- Scheduled mobile equipment items reclassified and treated as covered
  autos for liability and/or physical damage purposes
- Liability and physical damage for the scheduled items under the CA policy
  (removing them from the CGL mobile equipment coverage)

When used:
- Contractors with equipment like large forklifts, cranes, or specialty
  vehicles that travel on public roads but may be classified as mobile
  equipment under standard ISO definitions
- Accounts where the underwriter or insured prefers to have all vehicle-type
  equipment on the CA policy for unified coverage and limit management
- Situations where the CGL insurer excludes mobile equipment that is
  licensed for road use

Notes:
- The auto vs. mobile equipment distinction in ISO forms is based on
  vehicle purpose and road use: equipment designed primarily for off-road
  use is mobile equipment (CGL); vehicles designed for road use are autos (CA)
- When mobile equipment is used on public roads, it can move between
  classifications; CA 20 15 resolves the ambiguity for scheduled items
- Physical damage on mobile equipment may alternatively be handled on a
  contractors' equipment inland marine floater if better rates are available
  through that line
