CA 27 23 (2022-09) — Sexual Abuse Or Sexual Molestation Liability Coverage
==========================================================================
Form number: CA 27 23
Edition: 2022-09
Title: Sexual Abuse Or Sexual Molestation Liability Coverage
Type: ISO Commercial Auto endorsement
States: All except LA, NY (limited multi-state availability)

Policy term: Matches underlying CA 00 25 Auto Dealers Coverage Form
Transaction types: New business; endorsement addition

Purpose:
Provides affirmative liability coverage for sexual abuse or molestation
claims on a claims-made basis. This is a coverage grant — the counterpart
to the exclusion endorsements CA 27 21 and CA 27 22. Covers liability
for sexual abuse or molestation by any person for whom the insured bears
responsibility.

Captures:
- Liability coverage for bodily injury, property damage, and personal
  and advertising injury arising from sexual abuse or molestation by
  any person for whom the named insured is responsible
- Applies to Section II — General Liability Coverages and/or
  Section III — Acts, Errors Or Omissions Liability Coverages as
  specified in the endorsement

When used:
- Dealers who want affirmative coverage for sexual abuse/molestation
  claims rather than an exclusion
- Situations where the underlying CA 00 25 policy otherwise excludes
  this exposure and the insured wants it reinstated

Notes:
- Claims-made basis — coverage applies to claims first made and reported
  during the policy period
- CA 27 24 is the narrower alternative — it limits coverage to acts
  committed directly by the named insured rather than any person
- Parallel to CG 04 14/04 15 series under the standard CGL program
- Part of the September 2022 ISO filing cycle
- Not available in LA or NY
