CA 20 05 (2013-10) — Drive-Away Contractors
===========================================
Form number: CA 20 05
Edition: 2013-10
Title: Drive-Away Contractors
Type: ISO Commercial Auto endorsement
States: All

Policy term: Matches underlying policy
Transaction types: New business; renewal; midterm add

Purpose:
Provides liability coverage for drive-away contractors — businesses or
individuals hired to transport new or used vehicles from one location to
another (manufacturer to dealer, auction to dealer, dealer to customer,
etc.). Drive-away contractors take temporary possession of vehicles they do
not own; their exposure is primarily liability during transit, not physical
damage to the delivered vehicle (which remains the seller's/buyer's risk).

Captures:
- Liability (BI/PD) coverage for the named insured's drive-away operations
- Coverage for vehicles being driven under their own power during drive-away
  transit
- May include liability for vehicles in driveaway convoys

When used:
- Drive-away or auto transport companies that deliver vehicles under their
  own power (as opposed to transported on a car carrier)
- Auto auction companies and dealers that operate driveaway services
- Any account engaged in delivering vehicles for third-party owners

Notes:
- Drive-away contractors are distinct from auto transporters (who load
  vehicles onto carriers) — drive-away means the vehicle is driven to
  its destination under its own power
- Physical damage to the vehicle during drive-away is typically the concern
  of the vehicle owner, not the contractor; but liability for accidents
  during delivery is the contractor's exposure
- Garagekeeper/dealers sometimes use CA 20 05 for their own driveaway
  operations delivering customer vehicles
