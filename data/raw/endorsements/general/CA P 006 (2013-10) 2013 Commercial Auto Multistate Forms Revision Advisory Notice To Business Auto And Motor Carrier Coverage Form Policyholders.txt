CA P 006 (2013-10) — 2013 Commercial Auto Multistate Forms Revision Advisory Notice To Business Auto And Motor Carrier Coverage Form Policyholders
==================================================================================================================================================
Form number: CA P 006
Edition: 2013-10
Title: 2013 Commercial Auto Multistate Forms Revision Advisory Notice To Business Auto And Motor Carrier Coverage Form Policyholders
Type: ISO Commercial Auto policyholder advisory notice
States: All

Purpose:
Advisory notice explaining the 2013-10 commercial auto forms revision to
CA 00 01 Business Auto and CA 00 20 Motor Carrier policyholders. The
2013 revision updated both base forms with clarifications to coverage
triggers, exclusions, and definitions. A companion notice (CA P 007)
addresses the parallel changes to the CA 00 25 Auto Dealers Coverage Form.

Content:
- Material changes to CA 00 01 and CA 00 20 in the 2013-10 revision
- Clarifications to the pollution exclusion, fellow employee exclusion,
  and covered auto definitions
- New and revised endorsements introduced in the 2013-10 program filing
- Explanation of how changes affect policyholders' coverage structure

When used:
- Issued with CA 00 01 and CA 00 20 policies transitioning to or newly
  written on the 2013-10 edition forms

Notes:
- The 2013-10 revision introduced many of the CA 99 series forms currently
  in use; this notice provides the context for those introductions
- A separate CA P 007 was issued for dealer policyholders (CA 00 25)
  and CA P 008 for terrorism form revisions in the same cycle
- The 2013-10 edition was the current standard until the 2020-11 revision;
  many policies ran on 2013-10 forms through the 2020s
