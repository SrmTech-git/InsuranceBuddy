CA 25 18 (2013-10) — Exclusion – Year 2000 Computer-Related And Other Electronic Problems
=========================================================================================
Form number: CA 25 18
Edition: 2013-10
Title: Exclusion – Year 2000 Computer-Related And Other Electronic Problems
Type: ISO Commercial Auto endorsement
States: All

Policy term: Matches underlying policy (HISTORICAL — no longer used in active placement)
Transaction types: Historical reference only

Purpose:
Excluded liability coverage for bodily injury or property damage arising
from Y2K (Year 2000) computer date-processing failures. These forms were
issued in 1997–1999 to address the then-anticipated technology failures
at the turn of the millennium when computer systems using two-digit year
formats were expected to malfunction. The Y2K event passed without major
incident, and these forms are no longer attached to any active policies.

Captures:
- (Historical) Exclusion of BI/PD claims arising from computer or
  electronic equipment failures related to year-2000 date processing

When used:
- HISTORICAL ONLY — no current use
- Included in the ISO CA forms library for completeness and archival reference

Notes:
- Y2K computer exclusions were widely issued across all commercial lines
  in the late 1990s; the CA 25 18-25 series addressed this for auto dealers
- The forms were kept in the ISO filing library through subsequent
  revision cycles; they appear in current forms lists but are not
  used in practice
- CA 25 19, 20, 24, and 25 are companion Y2K forms; all are historical
