CA 23 05 (2013-10) — Wrong Delivery Of Liquid Products
======================================================
Form number: CA 23 05
Edition: 2013-10
Title: Wrong Delivery Of Liquid Products
Type: ISO Commercial Auto endorsement
States: All

Policy term: Matches underlying policy
Transaction types: New business; renewal; midterm add

Purpose:
Excludes liability coverage for property damage arising from the delivery
of the wrong liquid product or delivery to the wrong location. The base
commercial auto policy may otherwise respond to PD claims when a driver
delivers the wrong fuel, chemical, or other liquid into a customer's
receiving tank or equipment; this endorsement removes that coverage.

Captures:
- Exclusion of PD liability arising from wrong-product or wrong-location
  liquid delivery
- Applies to covered autos liability coverage section

When used:
- Policies for petroleum distributors, chemical carriers, or liquid bulk
  haulers where the underwriter excludes wrong-delivery PD from the CA
  policy
- Accounts where wrong-product delivery is considered a non-auto (products
  or professional liability) exposure and is carved out of the CA form

Notes:
- This is an EXCLUSION endorsement — it removes coverage, it does not
  provide it; a card or summary describing CA 23 05 as covering wrong-
  delivery PD claims would be incorrect
- Insureds with meaningful wrong-delivery exposure (fuel oil, chemicals,
  food-grade liquids) need separate products liability or cargo coverage
  to address contamination and wrong-delivery PD
- Pollution exclusions in the CA policy may also be separately implicated
  in liquid delivery scenarios involving environmental release
