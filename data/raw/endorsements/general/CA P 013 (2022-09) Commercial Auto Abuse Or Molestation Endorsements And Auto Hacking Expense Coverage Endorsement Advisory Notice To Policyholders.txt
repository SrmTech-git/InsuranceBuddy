CA P 013 (2022-09) — Commercial Auto Abuse Or Molestation Endorsements And Auto Hacking Expense Coverage Endorsement Advisory Notice To Policyholders
=====================================================================================================================================================
Form number: CA P 013
Edition: 2022-09
Title: Commercial Auto Abuse Or Molestation Endorsements And Auto Hacking Expense Coverage Endorsement Advisory Notice To Policyholders
Type: ISO Commercial Auto policyholder advisory notice
States: All

Purpose:
Advisory notice explaining two sets of new endorsements introduced in
the 2022-09 ISO commercial auto filing: (1) the abuse or molestation
endorsement suite (CA 27 21-24 and CA 28 03) addressing the CA 00 25
dealer program and covered autos liability exposure, and (2) the CA 04 65
Auto Hacking Expense Coverage Endorsement for all commercial auto programs.
Both represent significant new coverage options addressing emerging risks
not previously addressed in the ISO commercial auto program.

Content:
- Introduction of CA 27 21, 22, 23, 24 (abuse/molestation for dealer GL/AEO)
  and CA 28 03 (abuse/molestation for covered autos liability)
- Explanation of how the abuse/molestation exclusion and coverage options
  work — which excludes, which provides affirmative coverage
- Introduction of CA 04 65 Auto Hacking Expense Coverage
- How auto hacking expense coverage addresses cyber incidents involving
  covered autos

When used:
- Issued with commercial auto policies where any of these 2022-09 endorsements
  are attached
- Satisfies state notice requirements for new endorsement introductions

Notes:
- This is the most recent CA P advisory notice in the series as of 2026
- The 2022-09 abuse/molestation endorsement suite and CA 04 65 represent
  ISO's response to two distinct emerging risk categories: institutional
  abuse liability and connected-vehicle cyber risk
- Policyholders receiving CA 27 21/22 (exclusions) should understand
  whether companion coverage (CA 27 23/24) is available through their insurer
