CA P 007 (2013-10) — 2013 Commercial Auto Multistate Forms Revision Advisory Notice To Auto Dealers Coverage Form Policyholders
===============================================================================================================================
Form number: CA P 007
Edition: 2013-10
Title: 2013 Commercial Auto Multistate Forms Revision Advisory Notice To Auto Dealers Coverage Form Policyholders
Type: ISO Commercial Auto policyholder advisory notice
States: All

Purpose:
Advisory notice explaining the 2013-10 commercial auto forms revision
specifically to CA 00 25 Auto Dealers Coverage Form policyholders.
Companion notice to CA P 006 (which addresses CA 00 01 and CA 00 20
policyholders). The 2013 revision updated the dealer form with clarifications
to AEO (Acts, Errors and Omissions) coverage, garagekeepers provisions,
and various exclusions.

Content:
- Material changes to CA 00 25 in the 2013-10 revision
- Clarifications to AEO coverage scope and triggers
- Updates to garagekeepers coverage provisions
- New and revised CA 25 series endorsements introduced in the 2013-10 filing
- Explanation of how changes affect dealer policyholders' coverage structure

When used:
- Issued with CA 00 25 policies transitioning to or newly written on
  the 2013-10 edition forms

Notes:
- The 2013-10 revision was the penultimate major revision before the current
  2020-11 program; dealers on pre-2020 programs would have received this notice
- CA P 007 specifically addresses dealer concerns — AEO coverage is unique
  to the CA 00 25 and warranted a separate advisory from the general
  CA 00 01/CA 00 20 notice (CA P 006)
- The 2020-11 revision superseded the 2013-10 edition; the equivalent
  2020-11 transition notice is CA P 012
