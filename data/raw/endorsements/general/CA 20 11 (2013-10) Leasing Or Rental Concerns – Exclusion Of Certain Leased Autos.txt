CA 20 11 (2013-10) — Leasing Or Rental Concerns – Exclusion Of Certain Leased Autos
===================================================================================
Form number: CA 20 11
Edition: 2013-10
Title: Leasing Or Rental Concerns – Exclusion Of Certain Leased Autos
Type: ISO Commercial Auto endorsement
States: All

Policy term: Matches underlying policy
Transaction types: New business; renewal; midterm change

Purpose:
Removes specific leased autos from coverage under a leasing company's CA
policy. Allows the lessor to carve out particular vehicles or categories
of leased vehicles — for example, vehicles leased to high-risk lessees,
certain use classes, or autos covered separately under another policy.

Captures:
- Exclusion of scheduled autos (by VIN, description, or lessee) from the
  leasing company's CA policy
- Affected autos are removed from all coverages provided under the policy

When used:
- Leasing companies that want to exclude certain risk categories from their
  master fleet policy (e.g., vehicles leased for commercial transport or
  taxi use, which carry elevated risk)
- Situations where a specific lessee or vehicle is being transferred to a
  different policy or program
- Large fleet programs that segment risk into multiple policies by class
  or use

Notes:
- The excluded autos receive no coverage under this policy; the lessee
  or a separate program must arrange coverage independently
- Underwriters may require this endorsement when a lessor's fleet includes
  a mix of standard and non-standard risk vehicles and the non-standard
  vehicles need to be priced separately
- Should be used carefully: improperly excluding a vehicle that later has
  a loss creates coverage disputes; the exclusion schedule must be current
