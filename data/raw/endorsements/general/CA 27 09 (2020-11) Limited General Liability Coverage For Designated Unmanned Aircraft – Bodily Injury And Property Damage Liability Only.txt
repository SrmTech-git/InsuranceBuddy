CA 27 09 (2020-11) — Limited General Liability Coverage For Designated Unmanned Aircraft – Bodily Injury And Property Damage Liability Only
=============================================================================================================================================
Form number: CA 27 09
Edition: 2020-11
Title: Limited General Liability Coverage For Designated Unmanned Aircraft – Bodily Injury And Property Damage Liability Only
Type: ISO Commercial Auto endorsement
States: All (multi-state)

Policy term: Matches underlying CA 00 25 Auto Dealers Coverage Form
Transaction types: New business; endorsement addition

Purpose:
Excludes Coverage A (Bodily Injury and Property Damage Liability)
for drones and restores it only for specifically scheduled unmanned
aircraft and operations. Coverage B (Personal and Advertising Injury
Liability) is not affected by this endorsement.

Captures:
- Exclusion of BI/PD drone claims under Coverage A
- Giveback: BI/PD coverage restored for designated unmanned aircraft
  and scheduled operations
- Does not touch Coverage B

When used:
- Dealers who want scheduled drone BI/PD coverage while leaving their
  personal and advertising injury coverage entirely unmodified

Notes:
- CA 27 08 is the full (both A and B) version with the same scheduled
  giveback structure
- CA 27 10 is the Coverage B-only variant
- Part of the November 2020 ISO Auto Dealers form revision cycle
