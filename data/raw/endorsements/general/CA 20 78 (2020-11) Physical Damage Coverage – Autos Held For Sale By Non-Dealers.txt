CA 20 78 (2020-11) — Physical Damage Coverage – Autos Held For Sale By Non-Dealers
==================================================================================
Form number: CA 20 78
Edition: 2020-11
Title: Physical Damage Coverage – Autos Held For Sale By Non-Dealers
Type: ISO Commercial Auto endorsement
States: All

Policy term: Matches underlying policy
Transaction types: New business; renewal; midterm add

Purpose:
Provides physical damage coverage for autos held for sale by businesses that
are not licensed auto dealers. A licensed dealer has access to open lot
coverage under CA 00 25; a non-dealer business that sells vehicles (fleet
liquidation, rental company remarketing, equipment companies selling old
fleet units) does not have a natural home for inventory physical damage
under standard commercial auto. This endorsement fills that gap.

Captures:
- Physical damage (comprehensive, collision, or specified causes) for
  vehicles in the insured's inventory pending sale
- Coverage for vehicles that are not currently in use by the insured
  but are being actively marketed for resale
- May cover a specific number or value of vehicles at any one time

When used:
- Fleet management companies or rental companies liquidating fleet vehicles
- Corporations selling off excess company vehicles before replacing them
  with a new fleet
- Any non-dealer business with a meaningful number of vehicles awaiting
  sale that represent a physical damage exposure

Notes:
- Licensed auto dealers should use CA 00 25 (Auto Dealers Coverage Form)
  with its integrated open lot coverage; this endorsement is for non-dealer
  entities only
- The vehicles covered by this endorsement are typically no longer in
  active service — they are being stored and marketed for sale; coverage
  should reflect storage location and security
- Valuation is typically ACV at the time of loss; the endorsement limit
  should reflect the maximum aggregate value of vehicles in inventory
  at any one time
