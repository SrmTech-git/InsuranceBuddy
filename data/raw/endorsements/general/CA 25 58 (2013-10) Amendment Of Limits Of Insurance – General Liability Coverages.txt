CA 25 58 (2013-10) — Amendment Of Limits Of Insurance – General Liability Coverages
====================================================================================
Form number: CA 25 58
Edition: 2013-10
Title: Amendment Of Limits Of Insurance – General Liability Coverages
Type: ISO Commercial Auto endorsement
States: All

Policy term: Matches underlying CA 00 25 Auto Dealers Coverage Form
Transaction types: Endorsement addition (mid-term limit change)

Purpose:
Documents a change to the General Liability limits stated in the CA 00 25
declarations. Creates a time-stamped, endorsement-level record of the
revised limits and the period during which they apply, which is important
in coverage disputes over which limits were in force at the time of loss.

Captures:
- Supersedes the declarations page GL limits for the period shown on
  the endorsement schedule
- Carries its own schedule of limits (per occurrence, general aggregate,
  products-completed operations aggregate, personal and advertising
  injury, etc.)

When used:
- Mid-term limit increases or reductions on the GL section of CA 00 25
- Any situation requiring a clear, documented record of GL limits in
  effect at a specific time during the policy period

Notes:
- Parallel in concept to CG 25 02 (Amendment of Limits of Insurance –
  Designated Project or Premises) in the standard CGL program
- The endorsement schedule controls over the declarations page for the
  time period stated
