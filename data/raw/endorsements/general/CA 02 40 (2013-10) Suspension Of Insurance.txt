CA 02 40 (2013-10) — Suspension Of Insurance
============================================
Form number: CA 02 40
Edition: 2013-10
Title: Suspension Of Insurance
Type: ISO Commercial Auto endorsement
States: All

Policy term: Applies for the duration of the suspension period
Transaction types: Midterm policy change — coverage suspension

Purpose:
Temporarily suspends coverage on one or more specifically identified autos
during a defined period when those autos are not in use. The insured receives
a return premium credit if the suspension exceeds 30 days. Coverage does NOT
automatically resume — reinstatement requires the insured to proactively
attach CA 02 38 (Reinstatement of Insurance) with a new effective date.
Applies to Business Auto, Garage, and Motor Carrier coverage forms.

Captures:
- Specific auto(s) being suspended (by schedule)
- Suspension effective date
- Return premium credit for suspensions exceeding 30 days

When used:
- Seasonal equipment or vehicles stored for winter (farm equipment, seasonal
  trucks, construction equipment)
- Fleet vehicles temporarily taken out of service for major repairs or rebuild
- Autos that the insured has surrendered possession of but not yet removed
  from the policy

Notes:
- Coverage does NOT automatically resume when a suspension period ends —
  the insured must request CA 02 38 to reinstate; vehicles returning to
  service without CA 02 38 attached are uninsured
- Return premium credit applies only if the suspension exceeds 30 days;
  shorter suspensions do not generate a return premium
- Suspending liability coverage on a registered vehicle is high-risk:
  many states (including California and Georgia) require liability coverage
  to remain active on registered vehicles; suspending liability while a
  vehicle is still registered can trigger registration suspension or leave
  the insured exposed to uninsured liability claims
- Differs from deleting a vehicle: suspension keeps the vehicle on the policy
  in a suspended state; deletion removes it and requires re-adding as new
