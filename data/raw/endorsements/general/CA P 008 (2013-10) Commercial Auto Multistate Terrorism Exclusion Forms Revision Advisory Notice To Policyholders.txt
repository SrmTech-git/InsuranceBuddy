CA P 008 (2013-10) — Commercial Auto Multistate Terrorism Exclusion Forms Revision Advisory Notice To Policyholders
===================================================================================================================
Form number: CA P 008
Edition: 2013-10
Title: Commercial Auto Multistate Terrorism Exclusion Forms Revision Advisory Notice To Policyholders
Type: ISO Commercial Auto policyholder advisory notice
States: All

Purpose:
Advisory notice explaining revisions to the commercial auto terrorism
exclusion and coverage endorsements (CA 23 84-87 series) made in the
2013-10 program filing. Addresses TRIA reauthorization-related updates
and clarifications to how terrorism coverage is structured, excluded, or
elected under commercial auto programs. The terrorism endorsement suite
required periodic updates as TRIA was reauthorized; this notice explains
the 2013-10 changes.

Content:
- Updates to CA 23 84-87 terrorism endorsements in the 2013-10 revision
- Explanation of any changes to terrorism coverage caps, exclusion triggers,
  or election requirements
- Impact of TRIA-related changes on commercial auto terrorism coverage options

When used:
- Issued with commercial auto policies where terrorism endorsements were
  revised in the 2013-10 filing cycle
- Satisfies state notice requirements for material terrorism endorsement changes

Notes:
- Terrorism endorsements require periodic updates as TRIA is reauthorized
  by Congress; this notice addresses the 2013-era update
- CA P 003 (2006-01) was the earlier terrorism-specific advisory notice;
  CA P 008 is the 2013-10 successor
- For current terrorism coverage questions, the relevant endorsements are
  CA 23 84 through CA 23 87; advisory notices provide historical context
  for how those forms evolved
