CA 27 24 (2022-09) — Sexual Abuse Or Sexual Molestation Of Any Person Committed By The Insured Liability Coverage
==================================================================================================================
Form number: CA 27 24
Edition: 2022-09
Title: Sexual Abuse Or Sexual Molestation Of Any Person Committed By The Insured Liability Coverage
Type: ISO Commercial Auto endorsement
States: All except LA, NY (limited multi-state availability)

Policy term: Matches underlying CA 00 25 Auto Dealers Coverage Form
Transaction types: New business; endorsement addition

Purpose:
Provides affirmative liability coverage for sexual abuse or molestation
claims on a claims-made basis, but only for acts committed directly by
the named insured. Narrower than CA 27 23, which covers acts by any
person for whom the insured bears responsibility.

Captures:
- Liability coverage for sexual abuse or molestation claims arising
  from acts committed directly by the named insured (not employees,
  agents, or other persons)
- Claims-made coverage under Section II and/or Section III of CA 00 25
  as specified in the endorsement

When used:
- Situations where only the named insured's own direct acts need to
  be covered for sexual abuse/molestation, rather than the full scope
  of vicarious liability covered by CA 27 23
- Insurers who want to limit the coverage grant to first-party acts
  only

Notes:
- Claims-made basis
- CA 27 23 is the broader alternative covering any person for whom
  the insured is responsible, not just the named insured's own acts
- Part of the September 2022 ISO filing cycle
- Not available in LA or NY
