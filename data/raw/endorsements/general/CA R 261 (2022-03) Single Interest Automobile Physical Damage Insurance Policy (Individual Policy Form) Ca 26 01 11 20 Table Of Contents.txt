CA R 261 (2022-03) — Single Interest Automobile Physical Damage Insurance Policy (Individual Policy Form) Ca 26 01 11 20 Table Of Contents
==========================================================================================================================================
Form number: CA R 261
Edition: 2022-03
Title: Single Interest Automobile Physical Damage Insurance Policy (Individual Policy Form) Ca 26 01 11 20 Table Of Contents
Type: ISO Commercial Auto form table of contents
States: All

Purpose:
Table of contents for the CA 26 01 (11 20) Single Interest Automobile
Physical Damage Insurance Policy — Individual Policy Form. Provides a
structural index to the individual single-interest CPI policy issued
to a lender for a specific financed vehicle. Helps lenders, program
administrators, and claims adjusters navigate the CA 26 01 policy form,
which is a specialized lender-placed insurance document distinct from
standard commercial auto forms.

Content:
- Section index for CA 26 01 (2020-11 edition): coverage agreement,
  definitions, exclusions, conditions, lender's rights and obligations
- Page references for each section of the individual single-interest policy
- Reference to endorsement attachment points for CA 26 04-07

When used:
- Issued with CA 26 01 single-interest individual policy programs as a
  navigation reference for lenders and program administrators
- Used by CPI program adjusters handling individual policy claims

Notes:
- The CA R 261 numbering aligns with the CA 26 01 base form it references
  (261 = CA 26 01); CA R 262 is the companion for CA 26 02 (master policy)
- Single-interest CPI policies are used in specialty lender-placed insurance
  programs; the table of contents is a program administration tool
- The individual policy form (CA 26 01) covers a single financed vehicle;
  the master policy form (CA 26 02) covers a lender's entire loan portfolio
