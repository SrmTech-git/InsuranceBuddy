CA 99 16 (2013-10) — Hired Autos Specified As Covered Autos You Own
===================================================================
Form number: CA 99 16
Edition: 2013-10
Title: Hired Autos Specified As Covered Autos You Own
Type: ISO Commercial Auto endorsement
States: All

Policy term: Matches underlying commercial auto policy
Transaction types: New business; endorsement addition

Purpose:
Treats specifically identified hired autos (leased, rented, or borrowed
vehicles) as if they were covered autos the insured owns, for purposes
of physical damage coverage. Under standard commercial auto coverage,
hired autos have liability coverage but may not have physical damage
coverage equivalent to owned vehicles. This endorsement designates specific
hired autos as "autos you own," triggering the same physical damage
coverage terms (comprehensive, collision) that apply to owned fleet vehicles.

Captures:
- Physical damage coverage for specifically identified hired/rented/leased
  autos treated as owned for coverage purposes
- Comprehensive and collision coverage applies to the designated hired autos
  at the same terms as owned vehicles on the policy
- Eliminates the gap between owned auto physical damage and hired auto
  physical damage coverage

When used:
- Long-term vehicle leases where the lessee needs owned-vehicle-equivalent
  physical damage coverage
- Specific equipment or specialty vehicle rentals where the insured has
  ongoing physical damage exposure
- Any program where a hired auto needs to be fully integrated into the
  physical damage coverage structure

Notes:
- Distinct from general hired auto physical damage coverage — this form
  specifies particular autos rather than providing blanket hired auto coverage
- Lessors (rental car companies, fleet leasing companies) typically require
  physical damage coverage on leased units; this endorsement can satisfy
  that requirement for designated vehicles
- The coverage limit for each specified hired auto must be reviewed against
  the vehicle's actual value
