CA P 001 (2004-09) — Advisory Notice To Policyholders
=====================================================
Form number: CA P 001
Edition: 2004-09
Title: Advisory Notice To Policyholders
Type: ISO Commercial Auto policyholder advisory notice
States: All

Purpose:
General advisory notice issued to commercial auto policyholders to explain
changes in the commercial auto program or to communicate information
about policy terms and conditions. The CA P 001 is the baseline ISO
advisory notice form introduced in the 2004-09 program filing, providing
policyholders with transparency about how their commercial auto coverage
works or how recent program changes affect them.

Content:
- General policyholder communication about commercial auto program terms
- Explanation of coverage features, limitations, or program changes
  relevant at the 2004-09 filing date
- Not a coverage form — provides no insurance coverage independently

When used:
- Issued with new or renewal policies where policyholder communication
  about program terms is required
- Used by insurers adopting ISO advisory notices as part of their
  policyholder communication program

Notes:
- Advisory notices in the CA P series are non-coverage documents;
  they communicate information but do not modify coverage
- Each CA P form corresponds to a specific ISO filing or program revision
  cycle; CA P 001 is the earliest in the current series
- Successive CA P forms (002 through 013) correspond to subsequent ISO
  program revisions and topic-specific notices
