CA P 012 (2020-12) — 2020 Commercial Auto Multistate Form Revisions Advisory Notice To Policyholders
====================================================================================================
Form number: CA P 012
Edition: 2020-12
Title: 2020 Commercial Auto Multistate Form Revisions Advisory Notice To Policyholders
Type: ISO Commercial Auto policyholder advisory notice
States: All

Purpose:
Advisory notice explaining the 2020-11 commercial auto multistate forms
revision — the most significant ISO commercial auto program update since
the 2013-10 revision. The 2020-11 revision updated all three base coverage
forms (CA 00 01, CA 00 20, CA 00 25) and their associated endorsements,
incorporating over a decade of coverage evolution including cyber risk
(auto hacking exclusion), ridesharing, communicable disease, and updated
regulatory provisions. This notice explains those changes to policyholders.

Content:
- Summary of material changes in the 2020-11 commercial auto program revision
- New coverages introduced: CA 04 65 (Auto Hacking Expense)
- New exclusions added: communicable disease (CA 27 16 for dealers),
  cannabis, and updated cyber provisions
- Structural changes to all three base forms
- New and revised endorsements across the CA 00, 04, 20-27, and 99 series
- Explanation of how the 2020-11 revision affects policyholders

When used:
- Issued with all commercial auto policies transitioning to or newly written
  on 2020-11 edition forms
- Required by most states' policyholder notice regulations for major form changes

Notes:
- The 2020-11 revision is the current standard ISO commercial auto program
  as of 2026; most active policies should be on 2020-11 forms
- Policyholders renewing from 2013-10 or earlier editions experienced the
  full scope of changes described in this notice
- Note: CA P 011 does not appear in the current program — numbering skips
  from CA P 010 (2019-12) to CA P 012 (2020-12)
