CA 25 16 (2013-10) — Auto Dealers Coverage Form – General Liability Coverages – Total Pollution Exclusion
=========================================================================================================
Form number: CA 25 16
Edition: 2013-10
Title: Auto Dealers Coverage Form – General Liability Coverages – Total Pollution Exclusion
Type: ISO Commercial Auto endorsement
States: All

Policy term: Matches underlying CA 00 25 Auto Dealers Coverage Form
Transaction types: New business; endorsement addition

Purpose:
Completely eliminates pollution coverage from Section II — General
Liability Coverages of the CA 00 25. No exceptions are retained for
any pollution-related claims under the GL sections.

Captures:
- Total exclusion of all pollution liability under Section II — General
  Liability Coverages (Bodily Injury and Property Damage Liability,
  Personal and Advertising Injury Liability, and Medical Payments)

When used:
- Dealers where the insurer is unwilling to provide any pollution
  coverage under the GL sections
- Used when pollution exposure is being addressed on a separate policy
  or when the dealer has no meaningful pollution exposure warranting
  coverage

Notes:
- CA 25 36 is the alternative form for situations where partial pollution
  coverage is appropriate; unlike CA 25 16, CA 25 36 retains exceptions
  for building heating/cooling/dehumidifying equipment and hostile fires
- This endorsement applies to the GL sections only and does not affect
  the covered autos liability section's treatment of pollution
