CA 23 98 (2013-10) — Trailer Interchange Coverage
=================================================
Form number: CA 23 98
Edition: 2013-10
Title: Trailer Interchange Coverage
Type: ISO Commercial Auto endorsement
States: All

Policy term: Matches underlying policy
Transaction types: New business; renewal; midterm add

Purpose:
Provides comprehensive and collision physical damage coverage for non-owned
trailers in the insured's possession under trailer interchange agreements.
The full-coverage version of trailer interchange physical damage — covering
all physical damage perils (comprehensive and collision) rather than just
fire or fire+theft as in CA 23 13. Essential for motor carriers who regularly
interchange trailers with other carriers and are contractually responsible
for physical damage during their custody period.

Captures:
- Comprehensive and collision physical damage coverage for non-owned trailers
  while in the insured's possession under trailer interchange agreements
- Applies per the limit of insurance scheduled for interchange coverage
- Covers the trailer from pickup through return to the owning carrier

When used:
- Motor carriers with active trailer interchange programs who need full
  physical damage coverage on borrowed trailers
- Carriers whose interchange agreements require comprehensive and collision
  coverage (not just fire/theft)
- LTL carriers, truckload carriers, and intermodal operators who regularly
  pull trailers owned by other carriers or leasing companies

Notes:
- CA 23 13 is the limited version (fire only or fire + theft); CA 23 98
  provides full comprehensive + collision coverage — choose based on the
  interchange agreement's requirements
- CA 23 98 attaches to CA 00 01 (Business Auto Coverage Form); CA 00 20
  (Motor Carrier Coverage Form) includes native trailer interchange
  physical damage provisions and does not require CA 23 98
- Trailer interchange coverage is valued per-trailer or per-occurrence at a
  scheduled limit; the limit should reflect the value of trailers being
  interchanged (many van trailers are $30K–$70K; refrigerated trailers more)
