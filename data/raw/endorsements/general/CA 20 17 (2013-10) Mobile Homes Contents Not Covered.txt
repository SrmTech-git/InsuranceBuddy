CA 20 17 (2013-10) — Mobile Homes Contents Not Covered
======================================================
Form number: CA 20 17
Edition: 2013-10
Title: Mobile Homes Contents Not Covered
Type: ISO Commercial Auto endorsement
States: All

Policy term: Matches underlying policy
Transaction types: New business; renewal; midterm add

Purpose:
Explicitly excludes coverage for personal property and contents inside
mobile homes under the CA policy. Used when there is any ambiguity about
whether the policy might respond to contents claims — the endorsement
removes that ambiguity and makes clear that only the mobile home structure
(as a vehicle) is covered, not its contents.

Captures:
- Exclusion of coverage for personal property, furnishings, and contents
  located inside any covered mobile home auto
- Applies to all physical damage coverages under the policy

When used:
- Any mobile home on the CA policy where the carrier does not intend to
  cover personal property inside the home
- When the insured has or will have a separate policy (dwelling, renter's,
  or manufactured home) that covers contents
- As a companion to standard CA coverage where the underwriter wants to
  make explicit what the base policy already excludes

Notes:
- CA 20 16 is the mirror endorsement that ADDS contents coverage;
  use one or the other depending on the program intent
- This exclusion endorsement may be standard carrier practice for mobile
  home fleet operators (transport companies) that have no interest in
  insuring residents' personal property
- The contents exclusion should be disclosed to the insured so they
  understand their contents are not protected under the CA policy
