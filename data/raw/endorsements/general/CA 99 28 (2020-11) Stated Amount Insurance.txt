CA 99 28 (2020-11) — Stated Amount Insurance
============================================
Form number: CA 99 28
Edition: 2020-11
Title: Stated Amount Insurance
Type: ISO Commercial Auto endorsement
States: All

Policy term: Matches underlying commercial auto policy
Transaction types: New business; endorsement addition

Purpose:
Modifies the physical damage coverage on a commercial auto policy to pay
on a stated amount basis rather than actual cash value (ACV) or replacement
cost. The insured and insurer agree in advance on a stated value for each
covered vehicle; in the event of a total loss, the insurer pays the lesser
of the stated amount, ACV, or the cost of repair. Used for vehicles where
ACV is difficult to determine or where the insured wants certainty in
claim settlement value — such as classic, antique, modified, or specialty
vehicles.

Captures:
- Physical damage settlement based on a pre-agreed stated amount
  for each covered vehicle
- In total loss situations, pays the lesser of stated amount, ACV,
  or repair cost — not automatically the stated amount
- Provides settlement certainty for vehicles where market value is
  unclear or disputed

When used:
- Classic, antique, or collector vehicles where standard ACV is
  not a meaningful measure of value
- Custom or modified vehicles whose value exceeds standard book value
- High-value specialty vehicles where the insured wants to cap exposure
  uncertainty in claim settlement
- Any vehicle where the standard ACV calculation would undercompensate
  the insured for a total loss

Notes:
- 2020-11 edition; updated in the current commercial auto program revision
- Stated amount is NOT the same as agreed value — stated amount pays
  the lesser of the stated amount or ACV; agreed value pays the stated
  amount regardless of ACV
- Insureds seeking guaranteed-value coverage should confirm whether this
  form provides stated amount (lesser-of) or agreed value (full amount)
- Stated amounts should be reviewed regularly to ensure they reflect
  current market values for the vehicle
