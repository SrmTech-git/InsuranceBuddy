CA 20 18 (2013-10) — Professional Services Not Covered
======================================================
Form number: CA 20 18
Edition: 2013-10
Title: Professional Services Not Covered
Type: ISO Commercial Auto endorsement
States: All

Policy term: Matches underlying policy
Transaction types: New business; renewal; midterm add

Purpose:
Excludes liability coverage for bodily injury or property damage arising
from the rendering or failure to render professional services, even when the
injury occurs in connection with the use of a covered auto. Designed for
businesses where vehicle-based professional services create a blended
liability exposure — this endorsement restricts the CA policy to the
vehicle-operation exposure only, leaving the professional services liability
to a separate professional liability or E&O policy.

Captures:
- Exclusion of claims arising from professional services rendered in
  connection with covered auto use
- Applies to BI and PD liability under the CA policy

When used:
- Medical transport companies, ambulance services, or mobile healthcare
  providers where auto accidents may involve both driving negligence and
  medical care negligence
- Mobile service businesses (mobile veterinary, mobile mechanics, mobile
  IT) where professional errors and vehicle accidents could be intertwined
- When the underwriter wants to clearly separate the vehicle exposure
  from the professional exposure across two different policies

Notes:
- Without this endorsement, a CA insurer could face arguments that a
  professional services claim arising during vehicle use (e.g., a paramedic
  error during patient transport) is covered under the CA policy because
  the vehicle was involved
- Professional liability policies often have their own auto exclusions
  (or vice versa); coordination between the CA policy and the professional
  liability policy requires clear exclusionary language on both sides
- Agents placing ambulance services or mobile professional services should
  confirm both the CA and professional liability policies coordinate
  properly before binding
