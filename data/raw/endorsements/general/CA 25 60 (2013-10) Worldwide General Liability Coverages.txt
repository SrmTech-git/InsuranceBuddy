CA 25 60 (2013-10) — Worldwide General Liability Coverages
===========================================================
Form number: CA 25 60
Edition: 2013-10
Title: Worldwide General Liability Coverages
Type: ISO Commercial Auto endorsement
States: All

Policy term: Matches underlying CA 00 25 Auto Dealers Coverage Form
Transaction types: New business; endorsement addition

Purpose:
Expands the coverage territory for the CA 00 25 General Liability
coverages to anywhere in the world, removing the default restriction
to the United States, its territories and possessions, and Canada.

Captures:
- Replaces the standard Coverage Territory definition with a worldwide
  definition for Section II — General Liability Coverages
- Coverage applies to bodily injury, property damage, personal and
  advertising injury, and products/completed operations occurring
  anywhere in the world

When used:
- Dealers who export vehicles or have warranty, service, or sales
  operations outside the standard U.S./Canada territory
- Situations where GL exposure may arise from activities in foreign
  markets

Notes:
- OFAC carve-out: countries or jurisdictions subject to U.S. economic
  or trade sanctions or embargoes remain excluded
- Suit requirement retained: any suit to collect covered damages must
  be brought in the United States, its territories or possessions, or
  Canada — the insured has worldwide exposure coverage but must be
  sued in U.S. or Canadian courts
- CA 25 61 is the targeted alternative when only specific foreign
  countries need to be added rather than all jurisdictions
- Parallel to CG 24 22 (Amendment of Coverage Territory – Worldwide
  Coverage) in the standard CGL program
