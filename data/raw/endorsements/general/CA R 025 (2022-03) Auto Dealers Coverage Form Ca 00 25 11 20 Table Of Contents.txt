CA R 025 (2022-03) — Auto Dealers Coverage Form Ca 00 25 11 20 Table Of Contents
================================================================================
Form number: CA R 025
Edition: 2022-03
Title: Auto Dealers Coverage Form Ca 00 25 11 20 Table Of Contents
Type: ISO Commercial Auto form table of contents
States: All

Purpose:
Table of contents for the CA 00 25 (11 20) Auto Dealers Coverage Form,
providing a structural index to the most complex of the three ISO commercial
auto base forms. The CA 00 25 bundles auto liability, general liability, AEO
liability, garagekeepers, and open lot physical damage into a single form;
its table of contents is correspondingly more complex than CA R 001 or
CA R 020, reflecting the multi-coverage structure of the dealer program.

Content:
- Section index for CA 00 25 (2020-11 edition): covered autos liability,
  general liability coverages, AEO (acts, errors and omissions) coverages,
  garagekeepers, physical damage, conditions, definitions
- Reference to dealer-specific coverage sections not found in CA 00 01
  or CA 00 20 (AEO, open lot, garagekeepers)
- Page references for each coverage section and sub-section

When used:
- Issued with CA 00 25 policies as a navigation aid
- Used by dealers, agents, program underwriters, and claims adjusters
  to locate specific provisions within the complex dealer policy form

Notes:
- The CA 00 25's table of contents reflects the bundled nature of the
  dealer form — navigating between auto liability (Section I), GL (Section II),
  AEO (Section III), garagekeepers (Section IV), and physical damage (Section V)
  requires a clear structural reference
- The CA 27 series endorsements modify specific sections of the CA 00 25;
  the table of contents helps identify which section each CA 27 form modifies
- Issued 2022-03, coordinated with the CA R 001 and CA R 020 companion TOC forms
