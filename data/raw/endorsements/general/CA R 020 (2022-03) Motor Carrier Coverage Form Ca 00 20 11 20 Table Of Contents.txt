CA R 020 (2022-03) — Motor Carrier Coverage Form Ca 00 20 11 20 Table Of Contents
=================================================================================
Form number: CA R 020
Edition: 2022-03
Title: Motor Carrier Coverage Form Ca 00 20 11 20 Table Of Contents
Type: ISO Commercial Auto form table of contents
States: All

Purpose:
Table of contents for the CA 00 20 (11 20) Motor Carrier Coverage Form,
providing a structural index to the trucking policy form. Issued as a
companion reference document to the CA 00 20 to help insureds, agents,
brokers, and adjusters navigate the motor carrier policy. The CA 00 20 is
more complex than the CA 00 01 due to its trucking-specific provisions,
trailer interchange sections, and FMCSA regulatory compliance requirements.

Content:
- Section index for CA 00 20 (2020-11 edition): covered autos liability,
  physical damage, trailer interchange, trucking-specific conditions
- Reference to motor carrier-specific definitions (for-hire motor carrier,
  trailer interchange, etc.)
- Page references for each component of the Motor Carrier Coverage Form

When used:
- Issued with CA 00 20 policies as a navigation aid
- Used by agents, trucking program underwriters, claims adjusters, and
  policyholders to locate specific motor carrier policy provisions

Notes:
- The CA 00 20 is the successor to the retired Truckers Coverage Form;
  its table of contents reflects a more complex structure than CA 00 01
  due to trucking-specific coverages and FMCSA requirements
- Trailer interchange sections, motor carrier-specific definitions, and
  FMCSA filing provisions are key areas referenced in this table of contents
- Used in combination with CA R 001 (CA 00 01 TOC) and CA R 025 (CA 00 25 TOC)
  for agents who need to navigate across multiple base forms
