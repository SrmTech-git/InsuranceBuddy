CA 04 44 (2013-10) — Waiver Of Transfer Of Rights Of Recovery Against Others To Us (Waiver Of Subrogation)
==========================================================================================================
Form number: CA 04 44
Edition: 2013-10
Title: Waiver Of Transfer Of Rights Of Recovery Against Others To Us (Waiver Of Subrogation)
Type: ISO Commercial Auto endorsement
States: All

Policy term: Matches underlying policy
Transaction types: New business; renewal; midterm add

Purpose:
Waives the insurer's subrogation rights against specifically named third
parties. The scheduled (non-automatic) version of the commercial auto waiver
of subrogation. The insured must list each party by name on the endorsement
schedule; the waiver applies only to those listed parties, not to all
contract counterparties.

Captures:
- Waiver of the insurer's right to recover payments made on behalf of the
  insured from the scheduled third parties
- Applies only to the specifically named persons or organizations listed
  on the endorsement schedule

When used:
- When only one or a few specific parties need the waiver and the carrier
  or underwriter prefers to control scope
- Joint venture partners, named lessors, or specific contractors where
  the waiver obligation is clear and limited
- As an alternative to CA 04 43 (automatic blanket version) when the carrier
  wants to review and approve each waiver recipient

Notes:
- CA 04 43 (automatic version) is more commonly requested because commercial
  contracts routinely include blanket waiver-of-subrogation language;
  CA 04 44 requires the insured to actively identify and schedule each party
- The scheduling requirement can create a coverage gap if the insured forgets
  to add a party with whom they have a waiver obligation
- Underwriters may prefer this form to maintain oversight of which parties
  the carrier is waiving rights against, particularly for high-value accounts
- Certificates of insurance should reflect this endorsement for the listed
  parties when contractually required
