CA 20 49 (2020-11) — Additional Insured – General Liability Coverages – Grantor Of Franchise
============================================================================================
Form number: CA 20 49
Edition: 2020-11
Title: Additional Insured – General Liability Coverages – Grantor Of Franchise
Type: ISO Commercial Auto endorsement
States: All

Policy term: Matches underlying policy
Transaction types: New business; renewal; midterm add

Purpose:
Used with the Auto Dealers Coverage Form (CA 00 25) to add the grantor of
a franchise as an additional insured for the general liability coverages
section of the dealer form. The franchisor may be a vehicle manufacturer,
a service franchisor, or any other entity that has granted the dealer a
franchise; their franchise agreement typically requires the dealer to provide
AI status on the dealer's liability policy.

Captures:
- Named grantor of franchise added as additional insured for the CA 00 25
  general liability coverages section
- AI protection extends to claims arising from the dealer's operations as
  a franchisee
- Schedule identifies the franchisor

When used:
- Franchised auto dealers (new-car dealers holding a manufacturer franchise)
  whose franchise agreements require the manufacturer to be named as AI
- Any dealer with a franchisor that requires AI status as a condition of
  the franchise agreement

Notes:
- Specific to CA 00 25 (Auto Dealers Coverage Form) — the only ISO CA form
  with an integrated general liability section; cannot be used with CA 00 01
- The manufacturer's AI status under this endorsement is for the dealer's
  GL exposure arising from dealership operations, not for auto liability
  claims under the dealer's CA coverages section
- Automakers typically require this endorsement from all of their franchised
  dealers as part of standard franchise requirements — it is a routine part
  of any new-car dealer's insurance program
