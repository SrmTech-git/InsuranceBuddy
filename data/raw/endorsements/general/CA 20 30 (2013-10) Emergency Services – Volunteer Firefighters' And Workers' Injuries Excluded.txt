CA 20 30 (2013-10) — Emergency Services – Volunteer Firefighters' And Workers' Injuries Excluded
================================================================================================
Form number: CA 20 30
Edition: 2013-10
Title: Emergency Services – Volunteer Firefighters' And Workers' Injuries Excluded
Type: ISO Commercial Auto endorsement
States: All

Policy term: Matches underlying policy
Transaction types: New business; renewal; midterm add

Purpose:
Excludes bodily injury coverage under the CA policy for volunteer firefighters
and emergency service workers who are injured while responding to emergencies
using covered autos (fire trucks, rescue vehicles, etc.). These workers may
be covered under separate state volunteer firefighter compensation programs,
accident insurance plans, or dedicated municipal benefit programs; this
endorsement prevents the CA auto policy from becoming an unintended first
responder for those BI claims.

Captures:
- Exclusion of BI coverage for volunteer firefighters and emergency service
  personnel injured in the course of emergency response operations
- Applies while the insured's covered autos (fire apparatus, ambulances,
  rescue vehicles) are used in emergency service operations

When used:
- Volunteer fire departments and emergency service organizations
- Municipalities that operate fire/rescue/EMS services using volunteer personnel
- Any account where separate state or organizational programs cover volunteer
  worker injuries and the CA policy should not overlap

Notes:
- Many states have dedicated benefit programs for injured volunteer
  firefighters (e.g., state firefighter relief funds, Workers' Comp
  extensions for volunteers) — these programs are designed to be the primary
  payer; this endorsement ensures the CA policy does not conflict
- Without this exclusion, a BI claim by an injured volunteer responder
  against the municipality's fire truck policy could create unexpected
  CA liability exposure
- This endorsement does not exclude coverage for third parties injured by
  emergency vehicles — only for volunteer responders riding in or operating
  the insured's emergency vehicles
