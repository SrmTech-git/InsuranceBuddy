CA 25 07 (2013-10) — Locations And Operations Not Covered
=========================================================
Form number: CA 25 07
Edition: 2013-10
Title: Locations And Operations Not Covered
Type: ISO Commercial Auto endorsement
States: All

Policy term: Matches underlying CA 00 25 Auto Dealers Coverage Form
Transaction types: New business; endorsement addition

Purpose:
Carves out scheduled locations or business operations from all coverage
under the CA 00 25. The exclusion is blanket — it applies simultaneously
across every section of the form.

Captures:
- Removal of coverage for any location or operation specifically listed
  on the endorsement schedule
- Applies across all sections: Covered Autos Liability, General Liability,
  Physical Damage, and Garagekeepers

When used:
- Dealers who operate a satellite lot, body shop, or service-only facility
  at a separate address that is not intended to be insured on this policy
- Insurers who want to carve out a specific operation (e.g., a high-risk
  body shop) while retaining coverage for the main dealership

Notes:
- Only the locations or operations expressly listed on the schedule are
  excluded; all unlisted locations and operations retain full policy
  coverage
- The exclusion is absolute for scheduled items — no partial coverage
  under any section survives for a scheduled location or operation
