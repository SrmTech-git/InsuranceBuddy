CA 23 17 (2020-11) — Truckers – Uniform Intermodal Interchange Endorsement Form UIIE-1
========================================================================================
Form number: CA 23 17
Edition: 2020-11
Title: Truckers – Uniform Intermodal Interchange Endorsement Form Uiie – 1
Type: ISO Commercial Auto endorsement
States: All

Policy term: Matches underlying policy
Transaction types: New business; renewal; midterm add

Purpose:
Extends liability coverage to satisfy the insured trucker's indemnity
obligations under Section F.4 of the Uniform Intermodal Interchange and
Facilities Agreement (UIIA). The UIIA requires participating truckers to
defend and indemnify equipment providers (chassis lessors, rail carriers,
ocean carriers) for losses arising from the trucker's use of intermodal
equipment; this endorsement modifies the CA policy to provide that
liability/indemnity protection. This is a LIABILITY endorsement, not a
physical damage endorsement.

Captures:
- Liability coverage for the insured trucker's UIIA Section F.4 indemnity
  obligations to intermodal equipment providers
- Coverage for BI/PD and related defense costs arising from the trucker's
  use of UIIA-governed intermodal equipment (chassis, containers)
- Does not provide physical damage coverage for the equipment itself —
  that is a separate intermodal physical damage coverage question

When used:
- Drayage carriers and truckers enrolled in the UIIA who must satisfy
  the liability/indemnity insurance requirements to participate in the
  intermodal program
- Any trucker that picks up and delivers intermodal containers from ports
  or rail yards using equipment governed by UIIA agreements

Notes:
- CA 23 17 is a LIABILITY/INDEMNITY endorsement — it does not provide
  physical damage coverage for intermodal equipment; a card or summary
  describing it as physical damage coverage would be incorrect
- The UIIA is the governing contract for most intermodal interchange in
  North America; UIIA membership requires the trucker's policy to include
  a UIIE-1 or equivalent endorsement
- Physical damage coverage for non-owned intermodal chassis while in
  the trucker's custody is a separate coverage need, typically addressed
  through a trailer interchange or inland marine endorsement
