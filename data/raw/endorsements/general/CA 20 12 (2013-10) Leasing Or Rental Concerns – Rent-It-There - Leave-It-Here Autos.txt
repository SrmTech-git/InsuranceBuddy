CA 20 12 (2013-10) — Leasing Or Rental Concerns – Rent-It-There/Leave-It-Here Autos
===================================================================================
Form number: CA 20 12
Edition: 2013-10
Title: Leasing Or Rental Concerns – Rent-It-There/Leave-It-Here Autos
Type: ISO Commercial Auto endorsement
States: All

Policy term: Matches underlying policy
Transaction types: New business; renewal; midterm add

Purpose:
Excludes coverage under a leasing or rental company's CA policy for non-owned
vehicles that were rented from a different rental location ("rent it there")
and returned to the named insured's location ("leave it here"). These are
vehicles belonging to another rental program that are temporarily at the
named insured's lot; the endorsement removes any coverage ambiguity by
explicitly excluding them from the named insured's policy.

Captures:
- Exclusion of liability and physical damage coverage for non-owned
  rent-it-there/leave-it-here vehicles on the named insured's policy

When used:
- Rental companies that accept vehicle drop-offs from other rental programs
  or competitor locations, and do not intend to extend their policy's
  coverage to those non-owned vehicles
- Programs where the vehicle owner's own policy should remain operative
  during drop-off and repositioning

Notes:
- This endorsement EXCLUDES coverage — it does not provide coverage
- The returning non-owned vehicle's owner should maintain coverage through
  their own policy during transit and drop-off
