CA 25 54 (2013-10) — Exclusion – Personal And Advertising Injury Liability Coverage
====================================================================================
Form number: CA 25 54
Edition: 2013-10
Title: Exclusion – Personal And Advertising Injury Liability Coverage
Type: ISO Commercial Auto endorsement
States: All

Policy term: Matches underlying CA 00 25 Auto Dealers Coverage Form
Transaction types: New business; endorsement addition

Purpose:
Completely removes the Personal and Advertising Injury Liability
sub-coverage from Section II — General Liability Coverages of the
CA 00 25. This is a full elimination, not a narrowing.

Captures:
- Deletion of Section II, Subsection B — Personal and Advertising
  Injury Liability from the policy
- Removes coverage for claims such as defamation, false arrest,
  malicious prosecution, wrongful eviction, copyright infringement,
  and advertising injury arising from the dealership's operations

When used:
- Dealers whose insurer determines the personal and advertising injury
  exposure is being addressed by a separate professional liability or
  media liability policy
- Situations where the PA&I exposure is considered outside the scope
  of the intended CA 00 25 coverage

Notes:
- No schedule or carve-outs — once attached, there is zero coverage for
  personal injury or advertising injury claims regardless of the nature
  of the offense
- This endorsement modifies Section II only; it does not affect the
  auto liability section
