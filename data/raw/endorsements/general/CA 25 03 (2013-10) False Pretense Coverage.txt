CA 25 03 (2013-10) — False Pretense Coverage
============================================
Form number: CA 25 03
Edition: 2013-10
Title: False Pretense Coverage
Type: ISO Commercial Auto endorsement
States: All

Policy term: Matches underlying CA 00 25 Auto Dealers Coverage Form
Transaction types: New business; endorsement addition

Purpose:
Provides physical damage coverage for a dealer who voluntarily parts with
a covered auto as a result of a trick, scheme, or false pretenses — losses
not covered under standard comprehensive coverage, which requires a taking
without consent. Also covers the dealer who acquires a vehicle from someone
who did not have legal title to it.

Captures:
- Loss of a covered auto voluntarily surrendered due to fraud, trick,
  scheme, or false pretenses by the person who obtained it
- Loss arising from the dealer acquiring a vehicle from a person who
  lacked legal title

When used:
- Auto dealers under CA 00 25 seeking coverage for fraud-related
  vehicle losses beyond what standard comprehensive provides
- Dealers exposed to stolen-identity transactions, fraudulent financing
  approvals, or title fraud

Notes:
- Excludes losses where a bank or drawee fails to pay a negotiable
  instrument for any reason — bounced checks alone do not trigger coverage
- Coverage is subject to a limit scheduled in the endorsement
