CA 24 02 (2013-10) — Public Transportation Autos
================================================
Form number: CA 24 02
Edition: 2013-10
Title: Public Transportation Autos
Type: ISO Commercial Auto endorsement
States: All

Policy term: Matches underlying policy
Transaction types: New business; renewal; midterm add

Purpose:
Modifies the CA 00 01 Business Auto Coverage Form to enable coverage for
autos used in public transportation operations. The standard CA 00 01 excludes
coverage for "public or livery conveyance" use; CA 24 02 removes that exclusion
for designated vehicles and also removes the care, custody, or control exclusion
as it applies to property of passengers carried in the covered auto. Applies
broadly to bus companies, shuttle services, charter/tour operators, and
paratransit providers — not limited to municipal or public-sector operators.

Captures:
- Removal of the public or livery conveyance exclusion for covered autos used
  in public transportation operations
- Removal of the care, custody, or control exclusion for passenger property
  carried in a covered auto
- Liability coverage for the insured's bus, shuttle, charter, and paratransit
  operations

When used:
- Scheduled-route bus operators and regional transit providers
- Charter and tour bus operators
- Shuttle services and non-emergency medical transport providers
- ADA paratransit providers (demand-response service for disabled passengers)
- Any for-hire passenger carrier operation using covered autos

Notes:
- The critical function of this endorsement is removing the livery conveyance
  exclusion — without it, the CA 00 01 would not respond to claims arising
  from carrying fare-paying or contract passengers
- Also modifies the care/custody exclusion so passenger property losses are
  covered under the auto policy
- State public utility commission (PUC) authority and CDL requirements may
  apply depending on vehicle size and state; regulatory compliance is separate
  from this endorsement's coverage function
