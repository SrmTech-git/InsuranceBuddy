CA 20 16 (2020-11) — Mobile Homes Contents Coverage
===================================================
Form number: CA 20 16
Edition: 2020-11
Title: Mobile Homes Contents Coverage
Type: ISO Commercial Auto endorsement
States: All

Policy term: Matches underlying policy
Transaction types: New business; renewal; midterm add

Purpose:
Adds coverage for personal property and contents inside mobile homes that
are scheduled as covered autos. The physical structure of a mobile home
qualifies as an "auto" under CA 00 01 while it is in transit; however,
the personal contents inside are typically excluded from physical damage
coverage. This endorsement extends coverage to the personal property within
the mobile home.

Captures:
- Physical damage coverage for personal property/contents inside a covered
  mobile home auto
- Coverage applies to the same perils covered under the mobile home's
  physical damage coverage (comprehensive, collision, specified causes)
- Limit for contents separate from the vehicle limit

When used:
- Manufactured home transport companies or dealers that regularly move
  mobile homes containing furnishings or personal property
- Individuals transporting a mobile home (as an auto) with contents inside
- Any insured whose mobile home in transit contains property that would
  be a meaningful loss if damaged

Notes:
- The physical mobile home structure is covered as an auto; this endorsement
  adds the contents layer
- CA 20 17 is the companion endorsement that explicitly excludes contents
  coverage — use one or the other (not both)
- Contents coverage under a CA policy is unusual; for mobile homes that
  are stationary and used as residences, a separate dwelling or manufactured
  home policy covers contents (not the CA policy)
- Coverage applies to contents inside the mobile home and to property within
  25 feet of it; there is no confirmed "in transit only" restriction — for
  stationary mobile homes used as residences, a separate dwelling or
  manufactured home policy is the appropriate vehicle for contents coverage
