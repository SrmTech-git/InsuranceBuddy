CA 99 34 (2013-10) — Social Service Agencies – Volunteers As Insureds
=====================================================================
Form number: CA 99 34
Edition: 2013-10
Title: Social Service Agencies – Volunteers As Insureds
Type: ISO Commercial Auto endorsement
States: All

Policy term: Matches underlying commercial auto policy
Transaction types: New business; endorsement addition

Purpose:
Extends insured status under a social service agency's commercial auto
policy to volunteers who use their personally-owned vehicles in the
agency's service. Social service organizations (food banks, Meals on
Wheels, nonprofits, transportation assistance programs) rely heavily on
volunteer drivers who use personal vehicles. The standard commercial auto
non-owned auto coverage may not adequately protect volunteers as insureds;
this endorsement adds them explicitly, ensuring volunteers are covered
when transporting clients or performing agency functions.

Captures:
- Volunteer insured status for auto liability coverage when volunteers
  operate their personally-owned autos on behalf of the social service
  agency (the key gap this form addresses)
- Protects both the agency and the individual volunteer from auto
  liability claims arising from agency volunteer activities
- Applies to non-owned autos used in agency service

When used:
- Nonprofits and social service agencies with volunteer driver programs
- Meal delivery, client transportation, and similar volunteer driving programs
- Any organization where unpaid volunteers regularly drive for agency
  purposes and need insured status under the agency's commercial auto policy

Notes:
- Volunteer drivers using their personal vehicles for agency activities
  may have a coverage gap between their personal auto policy (which may
  exclude commercial use) and the agency's commercial auto policy (which
  may not cover volunteers)
- This endorsement bridges that gap by making volunteers insureds under
  the agency's policy
- Compare with CA 04 39 (Volunteer Hired Autos) and CA 05 24 (Non-Ownership
  Liability Volunteers), which address related but distinct aspects of
  volunteer driver coverage
