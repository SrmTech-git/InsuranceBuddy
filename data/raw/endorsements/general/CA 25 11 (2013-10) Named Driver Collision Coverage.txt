CA 25 11 (2013-10) — Named Driver Collision Coverage
====================================================
Form number: CA 25 11
Edition: 2013-10
Title: Named Driver Collision Coverage
Type: ISO Commercial Auto endorsement
States: All

Policy term: Matches underlying CA 00 25 Auto Dealers Coverage Form
Transaction types: New business; endorsement addition

Purpose:
Restricts collision coverage under the dealer's physical damage section
to trips operated by specifically named drivers within a 50-mile radius.
Reduces moral hazard associated with broad dealer-lot vehicle movement by
tying collision coverage to a known, limited pool of drivers.

Captures:
- Three simultaneous restrictions on collision coverage:
  1. Named driver requirement — collision applies only when a driver
     listed by name on the endorsement schedule is operating the vehicle
  2. Distance limit — coverage is limited to trips where the distance
     between pickup and destination does not exceed 50 miles
  3. Scheduled deductible — a deductible specific to this endorsement
     applies rather than the policy's standard deductible

When used:
- Dealers who use a limited pool of known delivery or transport drivers
  for moving inventory
- Situations where the insurer requires driver identification and distance
  controls as a condition of collision coverage

Notes:
- Unnamed drivers operating covered autos have no collision coverage
  while this endorsement is attached
- Losses occurring beyond the 50-mile radius are not covered regardless
  of who is driving
