CA 26 05 (2020-11) Additional Covered Property Endorsement – Single Interest Automobile Physical Damage Insurance Policy
==========================================================================================================================
Form number: CA 26 05
Edition: 2020-11
Title: Additional Covered Property Endorsement – Single Interest Automobile Physical Damage Insurance Policy
Type: ISO Commercial Auto endorsement
States: All

Policy term: Matches underlying CA 26 01 or CA 26 02 policy
Transaction types: New business; program addition

Purpose:
Introduces deductible amounts to the physical damage coverages provided
under the CA 26 01 or CA 26 02 single interest policy. The confirmed ISO
title for this endorsement is "Single Interest Deductibles." By adding a
deductible to comprehensive and/or collision coverage, this endorsement
adjusts the cost-sharing structure of the single interest program.

Captures:
- Scheduled deductible amounts applied to comprehensive and/or collision
  physical damage claims under the single interest policy
- Modifies the base CA 26 01/02 coverage to impose a deductible on
  covered losses

When used:
- Lender CPI programs where the insurer or program administrator introduces
  a deductible to manage claim frequency and premium cost
- Any single interest program structured with a cost-sharing deductible

Notes:
- This endorsement adds deductibles to existing coverage — it is not an
  additional covered property form; the file title does not match the
  confirmed ISO form content
- Confirmed ISO title: "Single Interest Deductibles"
- The four confirmed active CA 26 series forms are CA 26 01, CA 26 02,
  CA 26 04, and CA 26 05
