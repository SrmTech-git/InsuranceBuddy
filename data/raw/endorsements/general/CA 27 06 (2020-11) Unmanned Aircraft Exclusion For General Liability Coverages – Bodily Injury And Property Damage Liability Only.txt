CA 27 06 (2020-11) — Unmanned Aircraft Exclusion For General Liability Coverages – Bodily Injury And Property Damage Liability Only
=====================================================================================================================================
Form number: CA 27 06
Edition: 2020-11
Title: Unmanned Aircraft Exclusion For General Liability Coverages – Bodily Injury And Property Damage Liability Only
Type: ISO Commercial Auto endorsement
States: All (multi-state)

Policy term: Matches underlying CA 00 25 Auto Dealers Coverage Form
Transaction types: New business; endorsement addition

Purpose:
Excludes unmanned aircraft (drone) claims from Coverage A (Bodily
Injury and Property Damage Liability) only under Section II — General
Liability Coverages. Coverage B (Personal and Advertising Injury
Liability) is not affected.

Captures:
- Exclusion of BI/PD claims arising from unmanned aircraft under
  Section II, Coverage A — Bodily Injury and Property Damage Liability
  only

When used:
- Situations where only the physical injury and property damage exposure
  from drones needs to be excluded while retaining personal and
  advertising injury coverage related to drone use (e.g., aerial photo
  copyright or privacy claims)

Notes:
- CA 27 05 is the full exclusion covering both Coverage A and Coverage B
- CA 27 07 is the Coverage B-only (personal and advertising injury)
  variant
- Part of the November 2020 ISO Auto Dealers form revision cycle
