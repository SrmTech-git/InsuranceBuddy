CA 25 65 (2013-10) — Total Bankruptcy Or Insolvency Exclusion For Acts, Errors Or Omissions Liability Coverages
================================================================================================================
Form number: CA 25 65
Edition: 2013-10
Title: Total Bankruptcy Or Insolvency Exclusion For Acts, Errors Or Omissions Liability Coverages
Type: ISO Commercial Auto endorsement
States: All

Policy term: Matches underlying CA 00 25 Auto Dealers Coverage Form
Transaction types: New business; endorsement addition

Purpose:
Strengthens the standard bankruptcy/insolvency exclusion in the AEO
section by eliminating all exceptions to it. The base CA 00 25 AEO
section excludes bankruptcy/insolvency claims but preserves an exception
when the insurer placed had an A.M. Best's B+ or higher rating.
CA 25 65 removes that exception entirely, making the exclusion absolute.

Captures:
- Replacement of the modified bankruptcy/insolvency exclusion in
  Section III — Acts, Errors Or Omissions Liability Coverages with
  an absolute exclusion containing no exceptions
- If the named insured or any party in the covered transaction is in
  bankruptcy or insolvency proceedings, no AEO coverage applies —
  period

When used:
- Insurers who want to ensure that AEO coverage cannot be invoked
  by a bankruptcy trustee or creditor on behalf of an insolvent dealer
- Situations where even the A.M. Best's B+ exception in the base form
  presents unacceptable claims exposure

Notes:
- CA 25 62 is the alternative when the goal is only to change the
  rating threshold, not eliminate the exception entirely
- The base CA 00 25 uses A.M. Best's B+ as the threshold for the
  exception; CA 25 65 voids this exception without substitution
