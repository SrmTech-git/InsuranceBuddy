CA DS 21 (2020-11) — Motor Carrier Declarations
===============================================
Form number: CA DS 21
Edition: 2020-11
Title: Motor Carrier Declarations
Type: ISO Commercial Auto declarations form
States: All

Policy term: Sets the policy period for the CA 00 20 Motor Carrier Coverage Form
Transaction types: New business; renewal; endorsement

Purpose:
The declarations page for the CA 00 20 Motor Carrier Coverage Form.
Documents all policy-level information for trucking and motor carrier
commercial auto policies. In addition to the standard auto declarations
information, the motor carrier declarations captures trucking-specific
items: FMCSA carrier authority information, MCS-90 endorsement details,
trailer interchange schedules, and the unique coverage features relevant
to for-hire and private motor carrier operations.

Captures:
- Named insured, carrier authority information (USDOT, MC numbers)
- Policy period
- Covered auto symbols and liability limits
- Physical damage coverages and deductibles
- Schedule of covered autos (tractors, trailers, interchanged trailers)
- Trailer interchange agreement details if CA 23 13 or CA 23 98 is attached
- MCS-90 endorsement details and filing limit
- Premium breakdown including trucking-specific rating factors
- All endorsements attached including motor carrier-specific forms

When used:
- Every CA 00 20 Motor Carrier Coverage Form policy
- Required for all for-hire motor carrier programs and private carrier
  programs using the CA 00 20 as the base form

Notes:
- The CA DS 21 must be updated when FMCSA authority information changes,
  when the motor carrier adds or removes authority, or when the trailer
  interchange agreement changes
- MCS-90 endorsement details in the declarations must match the FMCSA
  filing limits required for the carrier's authority type
- Trucking programs have significantly more complex declarations than
  standard business auto due to regulatory filing requirements
