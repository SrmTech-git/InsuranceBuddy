CA 25 50 (2013-10) — Exclusion – Damage To Rented Premises
===========================================================
Form number: CA 25 50
Edition: 2013-10
Title: Exclusion – Damage To Rented Premises
Type: ISO Commercial Auto endorsement
States: All

Policy term: Matches underlying CA 00 25 Auto Dealers Coverage Form
Transaction types: New business; endorsement addition

Purpose:
Removes coverage for property damage to premises rented to or temporarily
occupied by the named insured. Eliminates the "Damage to Premises Rented
to You" coverage available under the base CA 00 25 General Liability
section.

Captures:
- Exclusion of property damage to premises rented to or temporarily
  occupied by the named insured from Section II — General Liability
  Coverages
- Removal of the "Damage to Premises Rented to You" limit from the
  policy declarations

When used:
- Dealers where the rented premises exposure is being addressed by a
  separate policy (e.g., a commercial property or GL policy held by
  the dealer or landlord)
- Insurers who want to remove the rented premises fire liability
  sublimit from the CA 00 25 GL section

Notes:
- Without this endorsement, the base CA 00 25 GL section provides
  limited coverage (subject to a sublimit shown in declarations) for
  fire damage to rented premises
