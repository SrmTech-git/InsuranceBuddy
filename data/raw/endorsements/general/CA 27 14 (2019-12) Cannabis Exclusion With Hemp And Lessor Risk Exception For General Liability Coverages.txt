CA 27 14 (2019-12) — Cannabis Exclusion With Hemp And Lessor Risk Exception For General Liability Coverages
============================================================================================================
Form number: CA 27 14
Edition: 2019-12
Title: Cannabis Exclusion With Hemp And Lessor Risk Exception For General Liability Coverages
Type: ISO Commercial Auto endorsement
States: All (multi-state; part of ISO's December 2019 cannabis exclusion filing)

Policy term: Matches underlying CA 00 25 Auto Dealers Coverage Form
Transaction types: New business; endorsement addition

Purpose:
Same cannabis exclusion as CA 27 13 — with both the hemp exception and
an additional lessor risk exception. Designed for auto dealers who also
lease premises to cannabis-related tenants and need GL coverage for
claims arising from those tenants' operations.

Captures:
- All exclusions and hemp exception identical to CA 27 13
- Lessor risk exception: the exclusion does NOT apply to claims arising
  from premises owned or maintained by the insured and leased to others
  — a dealer acting as landlord retains GL coverage for cannabis-related
  claims from a tenant's operations on leased premises

When used:
- Dealers who lease out building or lot space to cannabis-related
  businesses and need coverage for claims arising from tenants' cannabis
  operations
- Situations where a flat cannabis exclusion (CA 27 13) would
  inadvertently remove lessor coverage the dealer needs

Notes:
- CA 27 13 is the version without the lessor exception
- Both CA 27 13 and CA 27 14 are from the December 2019 filing cycle,
  separate from the November 2020 CA 27 revision
