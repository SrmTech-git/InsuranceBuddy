CA 25 04 (2013-10) — Fire, Fire And Theft And Limited Specified Causes Of Loss Coverage For Dealers
====================================================================================================
Form number: CA 25 04
Edition: 2013-10
Title: Fire, Fire And Theft And Limited Specified Causes Of Loss Coverage For Dealers
Type: ISO Commercial Auto endorsement
States: All

Policy term: Matches underlying CA 00 25 Auto Dealers Coverage Form
Transaction types: New business; endorsement addition

Purpose:
Provides a menu-driven, named-peril physical damage coverage for auto dealer
inventory as an alternative to comprehensive/collision. Three tiers are
available, each selected on the endorsement schedule.

Captures:
- Tier 1 — Fire: includes lightning, explosion, and loss from sinking,
  burning, collision, or derailment of a transporting conveyance
- Tier 2 — Fire and Theft: all Tier 1 perils plus theft
- Tier 3 — Limited Specified Causes of Loss: all Tier 2 perils plus
  windstorm, hail, earthquake, flood, and mischief/vandalism
- Coverage can be extended to the dealer, lender interests (floor-plan),
  or manufacturer interests as scheduled

When used:
- Auto dealers, manufacturers, or financial institutions (floor-plan
  lenders) seeking narrow named-peril physical damage protection rather
  than broad comprehensive coverage
- Situations where only collateral protection is needed on dealer inventory

Notes:
- Schedule must specify: applicable cause(s) of loss, covered parties and
  interests, limits, deductibles, and types of vehicles included
- Explicitly excludes business property, sales samples, and exhibition
  property regardless of tier selected
