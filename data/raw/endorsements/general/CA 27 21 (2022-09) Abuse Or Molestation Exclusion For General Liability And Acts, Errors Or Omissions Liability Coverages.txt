CA 27 21 (2022-09) — Abuse Or Molestation Exclusion For General Liability And Acts, Errors Or Omissions Liability Coverages
==============================================================================================================================
Form number: CA 27 21
Edition: 2022-09
Title: Abuse Or Molestation Exclusion For General Liability And Acts, Errors Or Omissions Liability Coverages
Type: ISO Commercial Auto endorsement
States: All except FL, NY, TX

Policy term: Matches underlying CA 00 25 Auto Dealers Coverage Form
Transaction types: New business; endorsement addition

Purpose:
Broadly excludes all claims arising from abuse or molestation — not
limited to sexual acts — from both the General Liability (Section II)
and Acts, Errors or Omissions Liability (Section III) coverages of
CA 00 25. This is the broader of the two abuse exclusion endorsements
in the CA 27 series.

Captures:
- Exclusion of bodily injury, property damage, and personal and
  advertising injury arising from any form of abuse or molestation by
  any person
- Exclusion of negligent employment, supervision, and retention claims
  connected to abuse or molestation
- Applies to both Section II — General Liability Coverages and
  Section III — Acts, Errors Or Omissions Liability Coverages

When used:
- Dealers or insurers who want to exclude all forms of abuse or
  molestation exposure from the CA 00 25 rather than only sexual abuse
- Part of the September 2022 ISO filing that restructured abuse/molestation
  exclusion and coverage options across commercial lines

Notes:
- CA 27 22 is the narrower alternative, excluding only sexual abuse or
  molestation while leaving other forms of abuse potentially covered
- Not available in FL, NY, or TX
- Part of the same 2022 ISO filing cycle that introduced parallel forms
  in the CGL program (CG 40 28/40 29)
