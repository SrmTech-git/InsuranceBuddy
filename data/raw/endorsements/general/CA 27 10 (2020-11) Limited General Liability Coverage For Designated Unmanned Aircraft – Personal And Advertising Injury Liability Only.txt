CA 27 10 (2020-11) — Limited General Liability Coverage For Designated Unmanned Aircraft – Personal And Advertising Injury Liability Only
===========================================================================================================================================
Form number: CA 27 10
Edition: 2020-11
Title: Limited General Liability Coverage For Designated Unmanned Aircraft – Personal And Advertising Injury Liability Only
Type: ISO Commercial Auto endorsement
States: All (multi-state)

Policy term: Matches underlying CA 00 25 Auto Dealers Coverage Form
Transaction types: New business; endorsement addition

Purpose:
Excludes Coverage B (Personal and Advertising Injury Liability) for
drones and restores it only for specifically scheduled unmanned aircraft
and operations. Coverage A (Bodily Injury and Property Damage Liability)
is not affected by this endorsement.

Captures:
- Exclusion of personal and advertising injury drone claims under
  Coverage B
- Giveback: Coverage B restored for designated unmanned aircraft and
  scheduled operations
- Does not touch Coverage A

When used:
- Dealers who want scheduled drone personal and advertising injury
  coverage (e.g., aerial photography privacy/copyright exposure) while
  leaving BI/PD coverage entirely unmodified

Notes:
- CA 27 08 is the full (both A and B) version with the same scheduled
  giveback structure
- CA 27 09 is the Coverage A-only (BI/PD) variant
- Part of the November 2020 ISO Auto Dealers form revision cycle
