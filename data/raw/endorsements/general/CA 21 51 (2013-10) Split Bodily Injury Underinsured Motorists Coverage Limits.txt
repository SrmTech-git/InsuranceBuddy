CA 21 51 (2013-10) — Split Bodily Injury Underinsured Motorists Coverage Limits
===============================================================================
Form number: CA 21 51
Edition: 2013-10
Title: Split Bodily Injury Underinsured Motorists Coverage Limits
Type: ISO Commercial Auto endorsement
States: All

Policy term: Matches underlying policy
Transaction types: New business; renewal; midterm change

Purpose:
Establishes split per-person/per-accident limits for bodily injury only
underinsured motorist (UIM) coverage on a commercial auto policy. The BI-only
version applies in states where UIM does not cover property damage, limiting
UIM recovery to bodily injury losses where the at-fault driver's coverage
is insufficient.

Captures:
- UIM bodily injury coverage structured as split per-person / per-accident
  limits
- Does not cover property damage; BI only
- Applies when the at-fault driver's liability is exhausted and additional
  BI damages remain uncompensated

When used:
- Commercial auto policies in states where UIM is limited to bodily injury
- Accounts where the carrier writes split-limit UIM on the BI side only

Notes:
- CA 21 51 (BI only UIM) vs. CA 21 46 (BI + PD UIM): select based on
  state statute
- CA 21 02 is the UM (uninsured) equivalent for BI-only split limits
- The 2013-10 edition is more current than CA 21 46 (1993-12); use CA 21 51
  in states where the 2013 version has been filed and approved
