CA 23 04 (2013-10) — Rolling Stores
===================================
Form number: CA 23 04
Edition: 2013-10
Title: Rolling Stores
Type: ISO Commercial Auto endorsement
States: All

Policy term: Matches underlying policy
Transaction types: New business; renewal; midterm add

Purpose:
Excludes liability coverage for bodily injury or property damage arising
from products sold, distributed, or dispensed from a vehicle (a "rolling
store") after those products leave the insured's possession. The base
commercial auto policy's products liability exclusion may not fully address
the mobile retail scenario; this endorsement specifically excludes
post-sale products liability for merchandise sold from covered autos.

Captures:
- Exclusion of BI/PD liability arising from products sold or distributed
  from the insured's vehicle after leaving the insured's possession
- Applies to covered autos liability coverage

When used:
- Policies for mobile vendors (food trucks, produce trucks, mobile retail)
  where the underwriter excludes post-sale products liability from the CA
  policy and expects it to be addressed under a separate CGL or products
  liability policy
- Accounts where the products-off-the-truck exposure is being carved out
  of the auto policy

Notes:
- This is an EXCLUSION endorsement — it removes coverage, it does not
  provide it; a card or summary describing it as extending coverage would
  be incorrect
- Rolling store operators typically need separate products liability
  coverage (under a CGL or BOP) for claims arising from merchandise sold
- The CA policy with this endorsement still covers auto liability
  (e.g., the truck striking a pedestrian); it excludes the products
  component after goods leave possession
