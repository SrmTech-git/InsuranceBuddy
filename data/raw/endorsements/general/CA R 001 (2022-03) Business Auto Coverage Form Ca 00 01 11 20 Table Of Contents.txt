CA R 001 (2022-03) — Business Auto Coverage Form Ca 00 01 11 20 Table Of Contents
=================================================================================
Form number: CA R 001
Edition: 2022-03
Title: Business Auto Coverage Form Ca 00 01 11 20 Table Of Contents
Type: ISO Commercial Auto form table of contents
States: All

Purpose:
Table of contents for the CA 00 01 (11 20) Business Auto Coverage Form,
providing a structural index to the coverage form's sections. Issued as a
companion reference document to help policyholders, agents, and adjusters
navigate the CA 00 01 policy. The table of contents identifies each major
section of the coverage form and the page or location of each coverage
section, definition, exclusion, and condition.

Content:
- Section index for CA 00 01 (2020-11 edition): coverage agreements,
  definitions, exclusions, conditions
- Page references for each component of the Business Auto Coverage Form
- Reference tool for locating specific policy provisions

When used:
- Issued with CA 00 01 policies as a navigation aid for the coverage form
- Used by agents, claims adjusters, and policyholders to locate specific
  provisions within the policy

Notes:
- The CA R series (R for "reference") provides table of contents documents
  for the major ISO commercial auto base forms; there is no coverage
  content in CA R forms — they are pure reference documents
- The 2022-03 edition corresponds to the 2020-11 CA 00 01 base form;
  the CA R 001 was issued in 2022-03 as a companion to the 2020-11 edition
- Knowing the table of contents structure helps with retrieval — Section I
  is covered autos, Section II is liability, Section III is physical damage,
  Section IV is conditions, Section V is definitions
