CA 25 15 (2013-10) — Full Covered Autos Liability Limit For Customers
======================================================================
Form number: CA 25 15
Edition: 2013-10
Title: Full Covered Autos Liability Limit For Customers
Type: ISO Commercial Auto endorsement
States: All (see Notes for state availability limitations)

Policy term: Matches underlying CA 00 25 Auto Dealers Coverage Form
Transaction types: New business; endorsement addition

Purpose:
Elevates the liability protection extended to dealer customers operating
covered autos from the state compulsory financial responsibility minimum
up to the policy's full covered autos liability limit. Without this
endorsement, customers are limited insureds capped at state minimums.

Captures:
- Removal of the financial responsibility minimum cap on customer
  liability coverage under Section I — Covered Autos Liability Coverage
- Customers (test-drive prospects, loaner recipients, etc.) receive
  insured status at the same full covered autos liability limit carried
  by the named insured

When used:
- Dealers who want customers operating dealer-owned vehicles to have
  full policy limit protection rather than state minimum coverage
- Dealers with loaner programs, courtesy vehicles, or active test-drive
  operations

Notes:
- An additional premium applies; industry practice indicates approximately
  25% of the liability premium
- Coverage applies only while the customer is operating a designated
  covered auto
- This endorsement is reported as not available (not filed) in New York
  and New Jersey, which impose their own mandatory coverage frameworks
  for dealer-loaned or dealer-operated vehicles; this state limitation
  has not been confirmed from a primary regulatory source and should be
  verified for specific state submissions
