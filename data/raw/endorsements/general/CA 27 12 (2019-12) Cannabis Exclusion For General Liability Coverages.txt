CA 27 12 (2019-12) — Cannabis Exclusion For General Liability Coverages
========================================================================
Form number: CA 27 12
Edition: 2019-12
Title: Cannabis Exclusion For General Liability Coverages
Type: ISO Commercial Auto endorsement
States: All (multi-state; part of ISO's December 2019 cannabis exclusion filing)

Policy term: Matches underlying CA 00 25 Auto Dealers Coverage Form
Transaction types: New business; endorsement addition

Purpose:
Adds a flat cannabis exclusion to the General Liability coverages
(Coverage A and Coverage B) of the CA 00 25. No hemp exception is
included — this is the broadest of the three cannabis exclusion options
in the CA 27 series.

Captures:
- Exclusion of bodily injury, property damage, and personal and
  advertising injury arising from cannabis operations (cultivation,
  manufacture, storage, sale, distribution, etc.)
- Exception: does not exclude BI/PD when someone merely consumes
  cannabis, as long as the insured was not the one who sold or
  furnished it to them

When used:
- Dealers or insurers who want a complete cannabis exclusion with no
  carve-outs for hemp-derived products
- The most restrictive cannabis exclusion option — no hemp exception
  and no lessor risk exception

Notes:
- CA 27 13 is the alternative that adds a hemp exception (products
  derived from hemp remain covered)
- CA 27 14 adds both the hemp exception and a lessor risk exception
- Filed December 2019 — separate from the November 2020 CA 27 revision
  cycle; the file was originally mislabeled with a 2020-11 edition date
