CA P 010 (2019-12) — Cannabis Exclusion Endorsements Advisory Notice To Policyholders
=====================================================================================
Form number: CA P 010
Edition: 2019-12
Title: Cannabis Exclusion Endorsements Advisory Notice To Policyholders
Type: ISO Commercial Auto policyholder advisory notice
States: All

Purpose:
Advisory notice explaining ISO's commercial auto cannabis exclusion
endorsements filed in the 2019-12 cycle. As cannabis legalization expanded
across U.S. states, ISO introduced endorsements allowing commercial auto
insurers to exclude auto-related losses arising from cannabis operations —
transportation of cannabis products, use of cannabis while driving, or
autos used primarily in cannabis operations. This notice explains those
exclusions and their impact on commercial auto policyholders.

Content:
- Explanation of the cannabis exclusion endorsements introduced in the
  2019-12 filing
- How cannabis-related exclusions affect covered auto liability and
  physical damage coverage
- What activities or autos may be subject to cannabis-related exclusions

When used:
- Issued with commercial auto policies where cannabis exclusion endorsements
  are attached
- Satisfies state notice requirements for new exclusion endorsements

Notes:
- Cannabis operations remain a rapidly evolving coverage area; insurer
  approaches range from full exclusion to specialized programs
- Policyholders involved in cannabis operations (transportation, delivery,
  dispensary operations) should carefully review whether their commercial
  auto policy includes cannabis exclusions
- The 2019-12 filing predates the 2020-11 major revision; cannabis
  exclusion provisions were carried forward or updated in the 2020 program
