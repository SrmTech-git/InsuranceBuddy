CA 27 08 (2020-11) — Limited General Liability Coverage For Designated Unmanned Aircraft
=========================================================================================
Form number: CA 27 08
Edition: 2020-11
Title: Limited General Liability Coverage For Designated Unmanned Aircraft
Type: ISO Commercial Auto endorsement
States: All (multi-state)

Policy term: Matches underlying CA 00 25 Auto Dealers Coverage Form
Transaction types: New business; endorsement addition

Purpose:
Excludes GL coverage (both Coverage A and Coverage B) for drones, but
restores coverage for specific unmanned aircraft and their operations
that are scheduled on the endorsement. Provides targeted coverage for
dealers who actually operate named drones rather than a blanket
exclusion.

Captures:
- Exclusion of all GL coverage for unmanned aircraft from Section II
- Giveback: coverage is restored for designated unmanned aircraft and
  their specific operations/projects as scheduled on the endorsement
- Option to apply a separate drone-specific aggregate limit (subject to
  the policy general aggregate)

When used:
- Dealers who actively operate drones (e.g., for aerial inventory
  photography or marketing) and want coverage for those specific
  scheduled operations while excluding all other drone exposure
- The most flexible drone endorsement for dealers with actual drone
  programs

Notes:
- CA 27 09 is the Coverage A-only (BI/PD) variant with the same
  scheduled giveback structure
- CA 27 10 is the Coverage B-only (personal and advertising injury)
  variant with the same structure
- CA 27 05 is the flat blanket exclusion with no giveback
- Part of the November 2020 ISO Auto Dealers form revision cycle
