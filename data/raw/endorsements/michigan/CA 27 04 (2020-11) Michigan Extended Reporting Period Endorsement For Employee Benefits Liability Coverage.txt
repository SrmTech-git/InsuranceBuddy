CA 27 04 (2020-11) — Michigan Extended Reporting Period Endorsement For Employee Benefits Liability Coverage
============================================================================================================
Form number: CA 27 04
Edition: 2020-11
Title: Michigan Extended Reporting Period Endorsement For Employee Benefits Liability Coverage
Type: ISO Commercial Auto endorsement — Michigan specific
States: MI

Policy term: Matches underlying CA 00 25 Auto Dealers Coverage Form with CA 27 01 (Michigan Employee Benefits Liability Coverage) attached
Transaction types: Endorsement addition (upon policy cancellation or non-renewal)

Purpose:
Provides a tail period following policy cancellation or non-renewal
during which Employee Benefits Liability claims can be reported for
acts, errors, or omissions that occurred during the policy period.
Attaches to CA 27 01 (Michigan Employee Benefits Liability Coverage),
which is written on a claims-made basis.

Captures:
- Extended reporting period for Employee Benefits Liability claims
  under CA 27 01 after the Michigan CA 00 25 policy terminates
- Claims arising from benefits administration errors during the policy
  period may be reported during the tail period

When used:
- Michigan dealers who carried CA 27 01 Employee Benefits Liability
  Coverage and are canceling or non-renewing the policy
- Michigan-specific equivalent of CA 25 67 for the 2020-11 cycle

Notes:
- Michigan-specific form; not available in other states
- Applies only on a claims-made basis consistent with CA 27 01
- Schedule must specify the limit of insurance, aggregate limit,
  retained limit, retroactive date, and tail premium
