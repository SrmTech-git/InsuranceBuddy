CA 27 01 (2020-11) — Michigan Employee Benefits Liability Coverage
==================================================================
Form number: CA 27 01
Edition: 2020-11
Title: Michigan Employee Benefits Liability Coverage
Type: ISO Commercial Auto endorsement — Michigan specific
States: MI

Policy term: Matches underlying CA 00 25 Auto Dealers Coverage Form
Transaction types: New business; endorsement addition

Purpose:
Adds Employee Benefits Liability coverage to the CA 00 25 Auto Dealers
Coverage Form for use in Michigan. Covers the named insured's liability
for errors or omissions in the administration of employee benefits
programs offered to employees.

Captures:
- Liability for acts, errors, or omissions by the named insured or any
  employee in the administration of the dealer's employee benefits
  program
- Written on a claims-made basis

When used:
- Michigan auto dealers under CA 00 25 who want employee benefits
  liability coverage incorporated into the dealers policy rather than
  on a separate policy
- Michigan-specific equivalent of CA 25 48 (Employee Benefits Liability
  Coverage) for the 2020-11 form cycle

Notes:
- Michigan-specific form; not available in other states
- CA 27 04 provides the Extended Reporting Period (tail) endorsement
  for this coverage
