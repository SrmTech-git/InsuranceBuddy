CA 27 02 (2020-11) — Michigan Customer Complaint Legal Defense Coverage
========================================================================
Form number: CA 27 02
Edition: 2020-11
Title: Michigan Customer Complaint Legal Defense Coverage
Type: ISO Commercial Auto endorsement — Michigan specific
States: MI

Policy term: Matches underlying CA 00 25 Auto Dealers Coverage Form
Transaction types: New business; endorsement addition

Purpose:
Adds defense-costs-only coverage for Michigan auto dealers responding
to customer complaints and regulatory proceedings arising from the
dealer's operations. Michigan-specific equivalent of CA 25 66
(Customer Complaint Legal Defense Coverage) for the 2020-11 form cycle.

Captures:
- Defense costs for responding to customer complaints or regulatory
  proceedings related to the named insured's auto dealer operations
  (sale, service, or repair of autos)
- Defense costs only — does not pay damages, judgments, or settlements

When used:
- Michigan auto dealers under CA 00 25 seeking coverage for legal
  defense costs from state regulatory complaints or customer-initiated
  proceedings

Notes:
- Michigan-specific form; not available in other states
- Claims-made basis
- Carries its own per-complaint and aggregate limits scheduled on the
  endorsement separately from main policy limits
