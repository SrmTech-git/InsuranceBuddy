CA 27 03 (2020-11) — Michigan Changes – Auto Dealers Coverage Form
===================================================================
Form number: CA 27 03
Edition: 2020-11
Title: Michigan Changes – Auto Dealers Coverage Form
Type: ISO Commercial Auto endorsement — Michigan specific
States: MI

Policy term: Matches underlying CA 00 25 Auto Dealers Coverage Form
Transaction types: New business; mandatory Michigan attachment

Purpose:
Modifies the CA 00 25 Auto Dealers Coverage Form to conform with
Michigan insurance law and regulatory requirements. This is a mandatory
state amendatory endorsement that adjusts policy terms, conditions, or
definitions as required by Michigan statute or the Michigan Department
of Insurance and Financial Services (DIFS).

When used:
- Required on all CA 00 25 policies issued in Michigan
- Attached automatically whenever a Michigan dealer policy is written
  on the CA 00 25

Notes:
- Michigan-specific mandatory amendatory endorsement
- Specific provisions modified are governed by Michigan filing
  requirements; full language requires reference to the actual ISO
  form or carrier policy specimen
