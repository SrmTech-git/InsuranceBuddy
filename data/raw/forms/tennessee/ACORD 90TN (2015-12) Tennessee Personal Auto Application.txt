ACORD 90TN (2015-12) — Tennessee Personal Auto Application
===========================================================
Form number: ACORD 90TN
Edition: 2015-12
Title: Tennessee Personal Auto Application
Type: Insurance form (ACORD industry standard, Tennessee state variant)
States: TN
Policy term: 6 months (typical for personal auto); 12-month terms also issued depending on carrier
Transaction types: New business; renewal applications (where required); adding driver/vehicle; significant midterm changes

Purpose:
Standalone application for a personal auto policy in Tennessee. Captures
the applicant, drivers, vehicles, coverages, and state-required
disclosures needed to underwrite, bind, and issue a Tennessee auto
policy under Tennessee's Financial Responsibility Law (Tenn. Code
Ann. § 55-12).

Captures:
- Producer / agency information
- Applicant: name, address, DOB, marital status, occupation, contact
- Driver list: license number/status, DOB, marital status, occupation
- Driver history (typically 3-5 years): accidents, violations, suspensions
- Vehicle list: VIN, year/make/model, body type, garaging address
- Vehicle use: pleasure / commute / business, annual mileage
- Lienholder / additional interest information
- Coverages: BI, PD, UM/UIM, MedPay, comp, coll
- Deductibles (comp and coll)
- Optional coverages
- Discounts claimed
- Prior insurance carrier and lapse history
- Loss history
- Tennessee-required disclosures and acknowledgments
- Fraud statement and applicant signature

When used:
- New business — applying for a Tennessee personal auto policy
- Renewal applications (where required)
- Adding a driver or vehicle to an existing Tennessee policy
- Midterm changes that require fresh underwriting

Notes:
- Tennessee operates an electronic insurance verification program (IVPS)
  — carriers report active policy status electronically
- UM coverage is mandatory; insured may reject in writing
- Section version (290TN) drops applicant identity in favor of ACORD 88
- Tennessee minimum liability limits: 25/50/15 (TCA § 55-12-107).
  Alternative: $65,000 surety bond or cash deposit with TN Dept of
  Revenue in lieu of insurance.
- Tennessee UM: INCLUDED BY DEFAULT in every auto liability policy at
  limits equal to BI; insured may reject in writing or select lower
  limits not below statutory minimums (TCA § 56-7-1201)
- Tennessee UIM: offered (not separately mandated); REDUCED-BY (DIL)
  structure — UIM pays difference between at-fault BI and insured UIM
- TN is at-fault with MODIFIED COMPARATIVE FAULT (50% bar)
- Primary statutes: TCA § 55-12-107 (minimums), § 56-7-1201 (UM/UIM),
  § 56-7-1301–1305 (cancellation/non-renewal), § 56-8-105 (Unfair
  Claims), § 56-7-105 (bad faith — 25% penalty)
- State regulator: Tennessee Department of Commerce and Insurance
  (TDCI); Tennessee Dept. of Safety and Homeland Security handles
  driver licensing and SR-22
- Electronic verification: "Drive Insured TN" verification system
