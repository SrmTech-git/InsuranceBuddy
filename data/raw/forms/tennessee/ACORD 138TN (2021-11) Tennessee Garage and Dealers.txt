ACORD 138TN (2021-11) — Tennessee Garage and Dealers
=====================================================
Form number: ACORD 138TN
Edition: 2021-11
Title: Tennessee Garage and Dealers
Type: Insurance form (ACORD industry standard, Tennessee state variant)
States: TN
Policy term: 12 months (typical for commercial garage policy)
Transaction types: New business, renewal, midterm change, audit-driven adjustment

Purpose:
Tennessee-specific Garage and Dealers section. Captures business
information, exposures, coverages, and Tennessee-required disclosures
for a Garage / Dealers policy under Tenn. Code Ann. § 55-12.
State-specific overlay to the national ACORD 128 (Garage and Dealers
Section).

Captures:
- Insured business type: new-car dealer, used-car dealer, service station,
  body shop, repair shop, towing operation, etc.
- Dealer / business license number(s)
- Premises: locations, age, square footage, lot size
- Operations: gross receipts, vehicles in inventory / sold / serviced
- Garage liability (premises and operations)
- Auto Liability (covered autos)
- Garagekeepers (CCC — care, custody, control)
- Products and Completed Operations
- UM/UIM for covered autos (TN UM mandatory)
- Tennessee-required dealer disclosures and acknowledgments

When used:
- New garage policy submission for a Tennessee auto-related business
- Renewal where Tennessee-specific coverages or dealer info are reselected
- Adding/removing locations or operations
- Midterm endorsement that changes coverage or operations

Notes:
- Tennessee operates an electronic insurance verification program (IVPS)
- UM coverage mandatory for covered autos; insured may reject in writing
- Tennessee dealer regulations are administered by the Tennessee Motor
  Vehicle Commission
- Tennessee personal-auto minimums applicable to garage covered autos:
  25/50/15 (TCA § 55-12-107). UM default at BI limits per § 56-7-1201.
- State regulator: Tennessee Department of Commerce and Insurance (TDCI)
