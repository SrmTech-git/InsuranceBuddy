ACORD 137TN (2021-11) — Tennessee Commercial Auto
==================================================
Form number: ACORD 137TN
Edition: 2021-11
Title: Tennessee Commercial Auto
Type: Insurance form (ACORD industry standard, Tennessee state variant)
States: TN
Policy term: 12 months (typical for commercial auto)
Transaction types: New business, renewal, midterm change, audit-driven adjustment

Purpose:
Tennessee-specific Commercial Auto Coverages section. Captures coverage
selections, limits, deductibles, and Tennessee-required disclosures for
a commercial auto policy under Tenn. Code Ann. § 55-12. Used alongside
national ACORD 125 (Applicant Info) and ACORD 127 (Business Auto
Section).

Captures:
- Liability limits (BI/PD split or Combined Single Limit)
- Comp / collision deductibles
- UM / UIM coverage selections and limits
- MedPay
- Hired Auto Liability
- Non-Owned Auto Liability
- Drive Other Car coverage (where applicable)
- Towing & labor / rental reimbursement / loss of use
- Trailer interchange (for trucking)
- Tennessee-required disclosures and acknowledgments

When used:
- New commercial auto submission for a Tennessee-based fleet
- Renewal where Tennessee-specific coverages are being reselected
- Midterm endorsement that changes coverage or limits

Notes:
- Tennessee operates an electronic insurance verification program (IVPS)
  — applies to commercial autos as well
- UM coverage mandatory; insured may reject in writing
- Federal motor carrier minimums (MCS-90) may override state minimums
  for interstate trucking risks
- Tennessee personal-auto minimum limits: 25/50/15 (TCA § 55-12-107).
  Commercial fleets subject to federal MCS-90 for interstate operations.
- TN UM included by default at BI limits (TCA § 56-7-1201)
- State regulator: Tennessee Department of Commerce and Insurance (TDCI)
- Electronic verification: "Drive Insured TN"
