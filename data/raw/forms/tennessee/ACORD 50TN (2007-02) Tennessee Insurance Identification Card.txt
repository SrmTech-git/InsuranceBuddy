ACORD 50TN (2007-02) — Tennessee Insurance Identification Card
===============================================================
Form number: ACORD 50TN
Edition: 2007-02
Title: Tennessee Insurance Identification Card
Type: Insurance form (ACORD industry standard, Tennessee state variant)
States: TN
Policy term: Matches the underlying auto policy term (typically 6 or 12 months); reissued at each renewal
Transaction types: N/A (proof-of-insurance card, not a transaction document)

Purpose:
Proof of mandatory liability insurance issued under a Tennessee personal
auto policy. Carried in the vehicle to satisfy Tennessee's Financial
Responsibility Law (Tenn. Code Ann. § 55-12), which requires drivers
to maintain minimum liability coverage.

Captures:
- Insured's name and address
- Policy number
- Policy effective and expiration dates
- Year, make, model, and VIN of the insured vehicle
- Insurer name and NAIC code
- Agent / agency name and contact (if applicable)

When used:
- Carried in the vehicle as proof of insurance
- Presented to law enforcement during a traffic stop or accident investigation
- Presented to the Tennessee Department of Revenue / county clerk for registration
- Required at accident scenes

Notes:
- Tennessee operates an electronic insurance verification system
  (Insurance Verification Program / IVPS) — carriers report active policy
  status electronically; the ID card supplements the electronic record
