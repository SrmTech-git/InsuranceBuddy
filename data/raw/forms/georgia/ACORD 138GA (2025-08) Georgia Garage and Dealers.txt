ACORD 138GA (2025-08) — Georgia Garage and Dealers
===================================================
Form number: ACORD 138GA
Edition: 2025-08
Title: Georgia Garage and Dealers
Type: Insurance form (ACORD industry standard, Georgia state variant)
States: GA
Policy term: 12 months (typical for commercial garage policy)
Transaction types: New business, renewal, midterm change, audit-driven adjustment

Purpose:
Georgia-specific Garage and Dealers section. Captures business
information, exposures, coverages, and Georgia-required disclosures
for a Garage / Dealers policy under O.C.G.A. § 33-34. State-specific
overlay to the national ACORD 128 (Garage and Dealers Section).

Captures:
- Insured business type: new-car dealer, used-car dealer, service station,
  body shop, repair shop, towing operation, etc.
- Dealer / business license number(s)
- Premises: locations, age, square footage, lot size
- Operations: gross receipts, vehicles in inventory / sold / serviced
- Garage liability (premises and operations)
- Auto Liability (covered autos)
- Garagekeepers (CCC — care, custody, control)
- Products and Completed Operations
- UM for covered autos (Georgia "added-on vs reduced-by" election)
- Georgia-required dealer disclosures and acknowledgments

When used:
- New garage policy submission for a Georgia auto-related business
- Renewal where Georgia-specific coverages or dealer info are reselected
- Adding/removing locations or operations
- Midterm endorsement that changes coverage or operations

Notes:
- Georgia's UM "added-on vs reduced-by" election applies to garage
  covered autos as well — captured here or on companion form 62GA
- Georgia uses electronic insurance database (GEICO)
- Georgia dealer regulations are administered by the Georgia State
  Board of Registration of Used Motor Vehicle Dealers (and the
  Department of Revenue for new dealers)
- Georgia personal-auto minimums applicable to garage covered autos:
  25/50/25 (O.C.G.A. § 40-6-10). UM add-on default per § 33-7-11.
- State regulator: Georgia OCI (Commissioner of Insurance and Safety Fire)
- Electronic verification: GEICS
