ACORD 51GA (2005-01) — Georgia Fleet Policy Information Card
=============================================================
Form number: ACORD 51GA
Edition: 2005-01
Title: Georgia Fleet Policy Information Card
Type: Insurance form (ACORD industry standard, Georgia state variant)
States: GA
Policy term: Matches the underlying commercial auto / fleet policy term (typically 12 months); reissued at each renewal
Transaction types: N/A (proof-of-insurance card, not a transaction document)

Purpose:
Proof of mandatory liability insurance for a fleet policy under Georgia
law. Issued at the policy level rather than per individual vehicle, for
commercial fleet operations where carrying a separate card for each
vehicle is impractical.

Captures:
- Named insured (typically a business entity)
- Policy number
- Policy effective and expiration dates
- Fleet description / coverage scope (e.g., "All owned vehicles")
- Insurer name and NAIC code
- Agent / agency name and contact

When used:
- Carried in any fleet vehicle as proof of insurance
- Presented to law enforcement during a traffic stop or accident involving a fleet vehicle
- Presented at vehicle registration for fleet-titled vehicles

Notes:
- The fleet card identifies the policy and the fleet; individual vehicles
  are typically covered under the policy's fleet endorsement schedule
- Used by trucking, delivery, rental, and large commercial-fleet operations
- Companion form to ACORD 50GA (the per-vehicle Georgia ID card) — 51GA
  is for fleet policies, 50GA is for non-fleet
