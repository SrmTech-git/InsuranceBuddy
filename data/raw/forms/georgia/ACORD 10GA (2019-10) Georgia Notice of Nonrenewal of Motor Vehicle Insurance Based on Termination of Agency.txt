ACORD 10GA (2019-10) — Georgia Notice of Nonrenewal of Motor Vehicle Insurance Based on Termination of Agency
==============================================================================================================
Form number: ACORD 10GA
Edition: 2019-10
Title: Georgia Notice of Nonrenewal of Motor Vehicle Insurance Based on Termination of Agency
Type: Insurance form (ACORD industry standard, Georgia state variant)
States: GA
Policy term: N/A (carrier-to-insured notification, not a coverage form)
Transaction types: Nonrenewal notification (agency termination)

Purpose:
Georgia-specific carrier notification informing the insured that the
auto insurance policy will not be renewed because the underlying
agency relationship between the carrier and the producer/agent has
ended. Unlike a standard nonrenewal (companion form 9GA) — which is
based on insured-specific factors — this notice addresses the
situation where the insured is being non-renewed for reasons external
to the insured's own conduct or risk profile.

Captures:
- Carrier information (named insurer, NAIC code)
- Policy number and expiration date
- Effective date of nonrenewal (typically the policy expiration date)
- Insured name and address
- Statement that nonrenewal is based on termination of the agency
  relationship — NOT on insured-specific factors
- Agency / producer information (former producer of record)
- Insured's rights to:
  * Obtain replacement coverage through another carrier or producer
  * Continue with the same carrier through a different producer
    (where applicable)
  * Obtain alternative coverage through Georgia Automobile Insurance
    Plan if needed
- Carrier signature / authorized representative
- Date of notice issuance

When used:
- Carrier nonrenewal of a Georgia auto policy specifically because
  the producer-carrier relationship has terminated (not because of
  insured-level factors)
- When an independent agency loses its appointment with the carrier
  and the carrier elects not to retain the agency's book of business

Notes:
- This is a separate notice from the standard nonrenewal (9GA)
  because the insured-protection considerations differ — a standard
  nonrenewal involves insured-level factors the insured may need to
  address; this nonrenewal is essentially external and the insured
  may retain coverage with the same carrier under a different producer
- Georgia O.C.G.A. § 33-24-46 et seq. and related statutes govern
  agency-termination nonrenewals
- Companion form: 9GA — standard Georgia auto nonrenewal notice
