ACORD 9GA (2019-10) — Georgia Notice of Nonrenewal of Motor Vehicle Insurance
==============================================================================
Form number: ACORD 9GA
Edition: 2019-10
Title: Georgia Notice of Nonrenewal of Motor Vehicle Insurance
Type: Insurance form (ACORD industry standard, Georgia state variant)
States: GA
Policy term: N/A (carrier-to-insured notification, not a coverage form)
Transaction types: Nonrenewal notification

Purpose:
Georgia-required carrier notification informing the insured that the
auto insurance policy will not be renewed at the upcoming expiration.
Georgia statute requires advance written notice of nonrenewal —
typically 30+ days before expiration — so the insured has time to
obtain replacement coverage. The notice must state the reason for
nonrenewal.

Captures:
- Carrier information (named insurer, NAIC code)
- Policy number and expiration date
- Effective date of nonrenewal (typically the policy expiration date)
- Insured name and address (mailing address used for legal notification)
- Reason(s) for nonrenewal — must be specific enough to satisfy
  statutory disclosure requirements:
  * Loss frequency / severity
  * Vehicle modifications or use changes
  * Material misrepresentation
  * Driving record changes
  * Carrier withdrawal from market or class
  * Other statutorily-permitted reasons
- Insured's rights to:
  * Obtain alternative coverage (including through Georgia
    Automobile Insurance Plan / GAIP if voluntary market unavailable)
  * Request additional information about the nonrenewal decision
  * Appeal the decision where applicable
- Carrier signature / authorized representative
- Date of notice issuance

When used:
- Carrier nonrenewal of a Georgia auto policy — sent in advance of
  expiration (typically 30+ days)
- Required documentation for both insured and Georgia insurance
  regulators

Notes:
- Georgia O.C.G.A. § 33-24-46 et seq. governs cancellation and
  nonrenewal notices for personal auto policies
- The reason must be specific — vague reasons may not satisfy the
  statutory requirement
- Distinct from cancellation (which terminates mid-policy-term) —
  nonrenewal terminates at expiration
- Companion form: 10GA — Notice of Nonrenewal Based on Termination
  of Agency (when the underlying cause is agent-carrier relationship
  ending, not insured-level factors)
