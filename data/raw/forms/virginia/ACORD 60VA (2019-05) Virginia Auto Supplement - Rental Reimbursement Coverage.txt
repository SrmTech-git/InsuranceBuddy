ACORD 60VA (2019-05) — Virginia Auto Supplement – Rental Reimbursement Coverage
================================================================================
Form number: ACORD 60VA
Edition: 2019-05
Title: Virginia Auto Supplement – Rental Reimbursement Coverage
Type: Insurance form (ACORD industry standard, Virginia state variant)
States: VA
Policy term: Matches the underlying auto policy term; election persists until changed in writing
Transaction types: New business (rental reimbursement election), change to rental coverage

Purpose:
Virginia-specific notice and election form for Rental Reimbursement
(also called Transportation Expense) coverage on a personal auto
policy. Rental reimbursement pays for substitute transportation
(rental car, ride-share, public transit) while the insured's
vehicle is being repaired after a covered loss. Coverage is typically
offered with daily and per-loss caps.

Captures:
- Description of rental reimbursement coverage
- Available limit options (typical examples: $30/day for 30 days /
  $900 maximum; $40/day; $50/day; higher tiers)
- Description of qualifying losses (typically those payable under
  comprehensive or collision)
- Insured's election to accept rental coverage at selected limits
  or reject
- Selected daily and per-loss maximum limits
- Insured signature and date

When used:
- New Virginia auto submission — rental reimbursement election
- Renewal where rental coverage is being modified
- Midterm endorsement adding or changing rental coverage

Notes:
- Rental reimbursement is a relatively low-premium coverage that
  many insureds elect because vehicle downtime from collision
  repairs averages 1-2 weeks
- Without rental reimbursement, the insured pays out-of-pocket for
  rental during repairs (or may not be eligible for a rental at all
  if the at-fault driver is uninsured / not yet identified)
- Coverage typically does not apply to mechanical breakdowns —
  only to losses covered under comp/coll
- Companion Virginia auto supplements: 61VA (Optional Medical and
  Disability Notice), 65VA / 66VA / 68VA / 69VA (UM/UIM elections)
