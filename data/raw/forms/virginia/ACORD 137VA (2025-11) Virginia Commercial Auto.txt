ACORD 137VA (2025-11) — Virginia Commercial Auto
=================================================
Form number: ACORD 137VA
Edition: 2025-11
Title: Virginia Commercial Auto
Type: Insurance form (ACORD industry standard, Virginia state variant)
States: VA
Policy term: 12 months (typical for commercial auto)
Transaction types: New business, renewal, midterm change, audit-driven adjustment

Purpose:
Virginia-specific Commercial Auto Coverages section. Captures coverage
selections, limits, deductibles, and Virginia-specific UM elections
for a commercial auto policy. Used alongside national ACORD 125 and 127.

Captures:
- Liability limits (BI/PD split or Combined Single Limit)
- Comp / collision deductibles
- UM / UIM coverage selections and limits (Virginia equal-to-liability rule)
- MedPay
- Optional medical and disability coverage
- Hired Auto Liability
- Non-Owned Auto Liability
- Drive Other Car coverage (where applicable)
- Towing & labor / rental reimbursement / loss of use
- Trailer interchange (for trucking)
- Virginia-required disclosures and acknowledgments

When used:
- New commercial auto submission for a Virginia-based fleet
- Renewal where Virginia-specific coverages are being reselected
- Midterm endorsement that changes coverage or limits

Notes:
- Virginia UM coverage typically required at limits equal to liability
  unless reduced/rejected via the appropriate election forms
- Companion Virginia auto-supplement forms apply where relevant:
  60VA (rental reimbursement), 61VA (optional medical/disability),
  65VA (UM notice), 66VA (UIM reduction), 68VA (UM alternative),
  69VA (UM selection/rejection)
- Virginia personal-auto minimum limits: 50/100/25 (INCREASED Jan 1, 2025
  from 30/60/20) under Va. Code § 46.2-472. Commercial fleets subject
  to federal MCS-90 for interstate operations.
- VA UMV fee option ELIMINATED July 1, 2024 — insurance now mandatory
- VA UM/UIM REQUIRED, must match liability (Va. Code § 38.2-2206);
  UIM ADDITIVE since July 1, 2023
- State regulator: Virginia Bureau of Insurance / State Corp Commission
