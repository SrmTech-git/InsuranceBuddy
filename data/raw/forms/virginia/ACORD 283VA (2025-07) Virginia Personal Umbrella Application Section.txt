ACORD 283VA (2025-07) — Virginia Personal Umbrella Application Section
=======================================================================
Form number: ACORD 283VA
Edition: 2025-07
Title: Virginia Personal Umbrella Application Section
Type: Insurance form (ACORD industry standard, Virginia state variant)
States: VA
Policy term: Matches the underlying umbrella policy (typically 12 months)
Transaction types: New business, renewal, increasing limits, midterm change to underlying policies

Purpose:
Personal umbrella-specific section of a Virginia personal lines
submission. Used in combination with ACORD 88 (Personal Insurance
Application — Applicant Information Section), which captures applicant
identity. The 283VA holds the umbrella-specific data: underlying
policies, household exposures, umbrella limit selection, and
Virginia-required disclosures.

Captures:
- Underlying auto policy: carrier, policy number, BI/PD limits,
  UM/UIM limits
- Underlying homeowners policy: carrier, policy number, personal
  liability and medical payments limits
- Underlying watercraft policy: carrier, policy number, BI/PD limits
- Underlying recreational vehicle policy: carrier, limits
- Other underlying policies as applicable
- Umbrella limit selected ($1M, $2M, $5M, $10M)
- Drivers and licensed members of household
- Vehicles, properties, watercraft, recreational vehicles owned
- Loss history (typically 5 years)
- Underwriting questions: prior umbrella, prior umbrella claims,
  professional/occupational exposures, youthful drivers, business use
- Virginia-required disclosures and acknowledgments
- Fraud statement and applicant signature
(NOTE: Applicant identity is captured on ACORD 88VA or national 88, not
duplicated here)

When used:
- New Virginia personal umbrella submission — when agency uses
  ACORD's modular section system (88 + 283VA) rather than standalone
  83VA
- Renewal where the umbrella limit is being modified while reusing
  applicant info from 88
- Adding/removing underlying policies in a modular-section package

Notes:
- Section version (283VA) drops applicant identity; standalone version
  is 83VA
- Companion forms: 88VA (Applicant Information Section), 290VA
  (Personal Auto Section — feeds underlying auto data), 81VA
  (Personal Inland Marine)
