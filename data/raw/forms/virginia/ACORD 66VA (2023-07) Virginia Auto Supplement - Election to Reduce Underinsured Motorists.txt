ACORD 66VA (2023-07) — Virginia Auto Supplement: Election to Reduce Underinsured Motorists
===========================================================================================
Form number: ACORD 66VA
Edition: 2023-07
Title: Virginia Auto Supplement: Election to Reduce Underinsured Motorists
Type: Insurance form (ACORD industry standard, Virginia state variant)
States: VA
Policy term: Matches the underlying auto policy term; election persists until changed in writing
Transaction types: New business (UIM reduction election); change to UIM coverage

Purpose:
Virginia-specific form capturing the insured's written election to
reduce Underinsured Motorists (UIM) coverage below the policy's BI
liability limits. By default Virginia UIM matches BI liability;
written election on this form is required to reduce UIM to a lower
amount (down to statutory minimum).

Captures:
- Statement of Virginia UIM equal-to-liability default
- Insured's election to reduce UIM to a specified lower limit
- Selected reduced UIM limits (BI per person / per accident / PD)
- Insured signature and date

When used:
- New Virginia auto submission where the insured wants UIM at less than
  BI liability
- Renewal or midterm change reducing UIM
- Anytime the insured wishes to lower UIM limits

Notes:
- UIM reduction requires written election under Virginia statute —
  must be a knowing, voluntary choice
- Companion forms: 65VA (notice), 68VA (UM alternative selection),
  69VA (UM standard selection/rejection)
- A future edition of 66VA dated 2026-01 was listed alongside this
  Current edition in the state catalog
- Virginia UIM became ADDITIVE (not reduced-by) on July 1, 2023 — UIM
  now STACKS on top of at-fault driver's BI limits under Va. Code
  § 38.2-2206. This 2023-07 edition was issued shortly after the
  additive-UIM statutory change; the form mechanics reflect this.
- Virginia UM/UIM defaults to match liability limits (50/100 as of
  Jan 1, 2025); reduction below requires written election on this form
