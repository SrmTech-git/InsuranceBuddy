ACORD 83VA (2025-07) — Virginia Personal Umbrella Application
==============================================================
Form number: ACORD 83VA
Edition: 2025-07
Title: Virginia Personal Umbrella Application
Type: Insurance form (ACORD industry standard, Virginia state variant)
States: VA
Policy term: 12 months (typical for personal umbrella)
Transaction types: New business, renewal, increasing limits, midterm change to underlying policies

Purpose:
Standalone application for a personal umbrella liability policy in
Virginia. Captures the applicant, household members, underlying
policies (auto, HO, watercraft, etc.) and their limits, and the
umbrella limit selected. The umbrella provides excess liability
coverage above the underlying policy limits, plus broader coverage
for certain claims (e.g., personal injury) that the underlying
policies may not cover.

Captures:
- Producer / agency information
- Applicant: name, address, DOB, contact, occupation
- Household members: spouse, resident relatives, licensed drivers
- Underlying auto policy: carrier, policy number, BI/PD limits,
  UM/UIM limits — Virginia underlyings typically require equal-to-
  liability UM unless reduced
- Underlying homeowners policy: carrier, policy number, personal
  liability limit (Coverage E), medical payments limit (Coverage F)
- Underlying watercraft policy: carrier, policy number, BI/PD limits
- Underlying recreational vehicle policy: carrier, limits
- Other underlying policies as applicable
- Umbrella limit selected (typically $1M, $2M, $5M, $10M)
- Drivers and licensed members of household
- Vehicles owned, leased, or regularly operated
- Properties owned (primary, secondary, rental)
- Watercraft, recreational vehicles, aircraft owned
- Loss history (typically 5 years)
- Underwriting questions: prior umbrella coverage, prior umbrella
  claims, professional/occupational exposures (e.g., serving on
  HOA boards), youthful drivers, business use exposures
- Virginia-required disclosures and acknowledgments
- Fraud statement and applicant signature

When used:
- New Virginia personal umbrella submission
- Renewal where the umbrella limit is being modified
- Adding/removing underlying policies (auto, HO, watercraft)
- Reporting significant household or exposure changes

Notes:
- Personal umbrella requires underlying liability minimums to be met
  (typically 250/500/100 auto and $300K HO liability) — verify
  carrier's required underlying limits
- Coverage broader than underlying: defense costs in addition to
  policy limits, personal injury coverage, worldwide coverage
- Companion forms: 88VA (Applicant Information Section), 283VA
  (Personal Umbrella Application Section — used when modular ACORD
  88 + 283VA structure is preferred over standalone 83VA)
