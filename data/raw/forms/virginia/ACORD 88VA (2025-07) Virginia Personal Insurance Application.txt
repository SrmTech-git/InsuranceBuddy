ACORD 88VA (2025-07) — Virginia Personal Insurance Application
===============================================================
Form number: ACORD 88VA
Edition: 2025-07
Title: Virginia Personal Insurance Application
Type: Insurance form (ACORD industry standard, Virginia state variant)
States: VA
Policy term: Matches the personal lines policies being underwritten (typically 6 or 12 months)
Transaction types: New business, renewal, changes to applicant identity / household composition

Purpose:
Virginia-specific Applicant Information Section. The modular ACORD
personal lines submission package starts with the 88 series for
applicant identity, then pairs with line-of-business section forms
(290VA auto, 282MN watercraft, 283VA umbrella, etc.). Captures
universal applicant identity that the line-of-business sections
don't need to duplicate.

Captures:
- Producer / agency information
- Applicant identity: name, address (mailing and physical),
  DOB, SSN (last 4 typical), marital status, occupation, employer,
  contact (phone, email)
- Spouse / domestic partner identity
- Resident household members (relationship, age)
- Prior addresses (typically 3-5 years for fraud / claim history)
- General Virginia-required personal lines disclosures
- Authorization to obtain consumer reports (credit, MVR, CLUE)
- Fraud statement and applicant signature

When used:
- Any personal lines submission in Virginia using ACORD's modular
  section system — paired with the appropriate line-of-business
  section (290VA auto, 283VA umbrella, etc.)
- Renewal where applicant identity has changed
- Initial onboarding submission across multiple personal lines policies

Notes:
- 88VA is the Virginia-adapted version of national ACORD 88
- Companion line-of-business sections in Virginia:
  290VA (Personal Auto Application Section), 283VA (Personal
  Umbrella Application Section), 81VA (Personal Inland Marine)
- Used when an agency prefers the modular 88 + section structure over
  standalone full applications (e.g., 90VA, 83VA)
