ACORD 69VA (2025-07) — Virginia Auto Supplement – Uninsured Motorists Coverage Selection/Rejection
===================================================================================================
Form number: ACORD 69VA
Edition: 2025-07
Title: Virginia Auto Supplement – Uninsured Motorists Coverage Selection/Rejection
Type: Insurance form (ACORD industry standard, Virginia state variant)
States: VA
Policy term: Matches the underlying auto policy term; election persists until changed in writing
Transaction types: New business (UM election required); change to UM coverage; UM rejection

Purpose:
Virginia-specific form capturing the insured's standard Uninsured
Motorists (UM) coverage selection at chosen limits or rejection. By
default Virginia UM matches BI liability; this form documents the
insured's affirmative selection of UM limits (which may differ from
liability) or rejection of UM coverage entirely.

Captures:
- Statement of Virginia UM equal-to-liability default
- Insured's selection of UM limits at specified amounts
- Insured's election to reject UM coverage entirely (where permitted)
- Insured signature and date

When used:
- New Virginia auto submission — UM selection/rejection required
- Renewal where the UM election is being modified
- Midterm change to UM coverage

Notes:
- Virginia UMV-fee-in-lieu-of-insurance option was ELIMINATED effective
  July 1, 2024 — insurance is now mandatory for all VA drivers; UM
  rejection on this form does NOT remove the underlying liability
  insurance requirement
- Companion forms: 65VA (notice), 66VA (UIM reduction), 68VA (UM
  alternative selection)
- Virginia UM minimum: must match liability limits (50/100 as of Jan 1,
  2025) under Va. Code § 38.2-2206. UIM is ADDITIVE since July 1, 2023.
