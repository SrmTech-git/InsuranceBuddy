ACORD 65VA (2025-04) — Virginia Auto Supplement – Notice Regarding Uninsured Motorist Coverage
===============================================================================================
Form number: ACORD 65VA
Edition: 2025-04
Title: Virginia Auto Supplement – Notice Regarding Uninsured Motorist Coverage
Type: Insurance form (ACORD industry standard, Virginia state variant)
States: VA
Policy term: Matches the underlying auto policy term; notice provided at issuance and renewal
Transaction types: New business; renewal (notice may be reissued)

Purpose:
Statutory notice form describing Virginia's Uninsured Motorists (UM)
coverage requirements to the insured. Virginia requires UM coverage at
limits equal to BI liability unless the insured elects in writing to
reduce or reject. This notice informs the insured of those rights and
limits.

Captures:
- Statutory disclosure describing UM coverage purpose and how it pays
- Statement of Virginia's equal-to-liability UM rule
- Description of the insured's right to reduce, alternative-select, or
  reject UM
- Insured acknowledgment of receipt

When used:
- New Virginia auto submission — notice delivered with election forms
  (66VA, 68VA, 69VA)
- Renewal — many carriers deliver the notice annually
- Whenever required by statute to refresh the insured's understanding

Notes:
- Pure informational notice — the actual election occurs on companion
  forms (66VA reduction, 68VA alternative selection, 69VA standard
  selection/rejection)
- Virginia UM is unique nationally for its equal-to-liability default
  and the multiple election form variants
