ACORD 67VA (2000-11) — Virginia Property Insurance Association
===============================================================
Form number: ACORD 67VA
Edition: 2000-11
Title: Virginia Property Insurance Association
Type: Insurance form (ACORD industry standard, Virginia state variant)
States: VA
Policy term: 12 months (typical for residual-market property)
Transaction types: New business (VPIA submission); renewal; midterm change

Purpose:
Application for property coverage through the Virginia Property
Insurance Association (VPIA) — Virginia's FAIR-Plan-equivalent
residual property insurance market. VPIA provides basic property
coverage for property owners who cannot obtain coverage in the
voluntary market.

Captures:
- Applicant / owner information (name, address, contact)
- Property location and address
- Property description: construction type, year built, square footage,
  occupancy (owner-occupied / rental / vacant / commercial),
  protection class
- Coverage selected: building, contents, additional living expense
  or fair rental value (depending on property type)
- Coverage amounts and deductibles
- Mortgagee / loss payee information
- Prior insurance carrier(s), denial dates, and reasons for denial
- Loss history (typically 5 years)
- Virginia VPIA statutory disclosures and acknowledgments
- Applicant signature and date

When used:
- Submission to VPIA after voluntary-market denial — applies to
  residential, dwelling fire, and commercial property exposures
- Renewal of existing VPIA policy
- Reporting property changes

Notes:
- VPIA = Virginia Property Insurance Association
- Coverage typically named-perils only (basic fire + extended
  coverage + VMM)
- VPIA premium is generally higher than voluntary-market coverage
