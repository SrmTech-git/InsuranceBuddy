ACORD 138VA (2025-11) — Virginia Garage and Dealers
====================================================
Form number: ACORD 138VA
Edition: 2025-11
Title: Virginia Garage and Dealers
Type: Insurance form (ACORD industry standard, Virginia state variant)
States: VA
Policy term: 12 months (typical for commercial garage policy)
Transaction types: New business, renewal, midterm change, audit-driven adjustment

Purpose:
Virginia-specific Garage and Dealers section. Captures business
information, exposures, coverages, and Virginia-specific UM elections
for a Garage / Dealers policy. State-specific overlay to the national
ACORD 128 (Garage and Dealers Section).

Captures:
- Insured business type: new-car dealer, used-car dealer, service station,
  body shop, repair shop, towing operation, etc.
- Dealer / business license number(s)
- Premises: locations, age, square footage, lot size
- Operations: gross receipts, vehicles in inventory / sold / serviced
- Garage liability (premises and operations)
- Auto Liability (covered autos) — Virginia UM equal-to-liability rule
- Garagekeepers (CCC — care, custody, control)
- Products and Completed Operations
- UM/UIM for covered autos (Virginia elections may apply via 66/68/69 VA)
- Virginia-required dealer disclosures and acknowledgments

When used:
- New garage policy submission for a Virginia auto-related business
- Renewal where Virginia-specific coverages or dealer info are reselected
- Adding/removing locations or operations
- Midterm endorsement that changes coverage or operations

Notes:
- Virginia UM coverage typically required at limits equal to liability
  unless reduced/rejected — applies to garage covered autos as well
- Companion Virginia UM forms apply where relevant: 65VA (notice),
  66VA (UIM reduction), 68VA (UM alternative), 69VA (UM selection/rejection)
- Virginia dealer regulations are administered by the Virginia Motor
  Vehicle Dealer Board
- Virginia personal-auto minimums applicable to garage covered autos:
  50/100/25 (INCREASED Jan 1, 2025 from 30/60/20) under Va. Code § 46.2-472
- VA UM/UIM REQUIRED, must match liability; UIM ADDITIVE since July 1, 2023
- State regulator: Virginia Bureau of Insurance / SCC
