ACORD 68VA (2025-07) — Virginia Auto Supplement – Uninsured Motorists Alternative Coverage Selection
=====================================================================================================
Form number: ACORD 68VA
Edition: 2025-07
Title: Virginia Auto Supplement – Uninsured Motorists Alternative Coverage Selection
Type: Insurance form (ACORD industry standard, Virginia state variant)
States: VA
Policy term: Matches the underlying auto policy term; election persists until changed in writing
Transaction types: New business (alternative UM coverage election); change to UM coverage structure

Purpose:
Virginia-specific form capturing the insured's election of an
"alternative" Uninsured Motorists (UM) coverage structure — distinct
from the standard UM coverage and from a flat UM rejection. The
alternative structure may differ in how UM stacks, applies, or pays
under specific Virginia statutory variants.

Captures:
- Description of the alternative UM coverage structure being offered
- Insured's election to accept the alternative UM coverage
- Limits or terms selected under the alternative structure
- Insured signature and date

When used:
- New Virginia auto submission where the insured is choosing an
  alternative UM structure rather than standard UM or rejection
- Renewal or midterm change electing the alternative
- Anytime the insured wishes to switch between standard UM and the
  alternative structure

Notes:
- Virginia offers multiple UM election paths: standard selection/
  rejection (69VA), reduction below liability (66VA), or alternative
  coverage (this form, 68VA)
