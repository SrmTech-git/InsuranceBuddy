ACORD 61VA (2026-01) — Virginia Auto Supplement – Notice of Optional Medical and Disability Coverage
=====================================================================================================
Form number: ACORD 61VA
Edition: 2026-01
Title: Virginia Auto Supplement – Notice of Optional Medical and Disability Coverage
Type: Insurance form (ACORD industry standard, Virginia state variant)
States: VA
Policy term: Matches the underlying auto policy term; election persists until changed in writing
Transaction types: New business (notice/election required), change to medical/disability coverage

Purpose:
Virginia-required notice and election form for optional Medical
Expense Benefits and Income Loss (Disability) Benefits coverage on
a personal auto policy. Virginia is not a no-fault state but does
require carriers to offer first-party medical payments and income-
loss benefits as elective coverages. This form notifies the insured
of availability and captures election or rejection.

Captures:
- Statutory description of optional Medical Expense Benefits coverage
  (similar in concept to MedPay in non-no-fault states)
- Statutory description of optional Income Loss / Disability Benefits
  coverage (pays for wages lost due to injury from auto accident)
- Available limit options for each
- Insured's election to accept (with limits selected) or reject each
  coverage
- Insured signature and date

When used:
- New Virginia auto submission — notice/election required
- Renewal where medical or disability coverage is being modified
- Midterm endorsement changing these coverages

Notes:
- Virginia is a tort state (not no-fault) — these optional coverages
  function as first-party benefits alongside the at-fault liability
  framework
- Income Loss / Disability coverage is less common than Medical
  Payments — many insureds rely on employer-provided short-term
  disability or personal health insurance instead
- Coverage limits are typically modest (e.g., $2K-$10K medical,
  $50-$200/week wage loss) — meant as gap-fill, not replacement for
  health/disability insurance
- Companion Virginia auto supplements: 60VA (Rental Reimbursement),
  65VA / 66VA / 68VA / 69VA (UM/UIM elections)
