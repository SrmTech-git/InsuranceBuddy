ACORD 853MN (2010-08) — Minnesota Homeowners Personal Property Supplement
==========================================================================
Form number: ACORD 853MN
Edition: 2010-08
Title: Minnesota Homeowners Personal Property Supplement
Type: Insurance form (ACORD industry standard, Minnesota state variant)
States: MN
Policy term: Matches the underlying homeowners policy (typically 12 months)
Transaction types: New business (additional personal property disclosures); scheduled item additions; coverage changes

Purpose:
Minnesota-specific supplement to a homeowners application capturing
additional personal property information — typically items requiring
extra detail beyond the base Coverage C limit and standard internal
sub-limits (jewelry, silverware, firearms, fine art, business
property in the home, etc.). Used to support proper coverage and
scheduling decisions for Minnesota homeowners.

Captures:
- Personal property categories with valuations:
  * Jewelry, watches, furs (subject to standard HO sub-limits — typical
    $1,500 per category — that this supplement may help schedule above)
  * Silverware, goldware, pewter (typical $2,500 sub-limit)
  * Firearms (typical $2,500 sub-limit)
  * Cameras, computers, electronics
  * Business personal property in the home (typical $2,500 sub-limit)
  * Fine art, antiques, collectibles
  * Other valuable items
- Notes on whether each category is to be scheduled separately (via
  ACORD 81 / personal inland marine floater) or kept under HO Coverage C
- Insured signature and date

When used:
- New Minnesota homeowners submission where additional personal
  property detail is needed
- Renewal where personal property situation has changed
- Adding scheduled items (links to ACORD 81 / personal articles)
- Reporting significant personal property acquisitions

Notes:
- HO Coverage C sub-limits often inadequate for valuable items — this
  supplement helps the producer identify items that should be
  scheduled separately under a personal articles floater
