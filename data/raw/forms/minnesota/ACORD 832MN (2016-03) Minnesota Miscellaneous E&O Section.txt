ACORD 832MN (2016-03) — Minnesota Miscellaneous E&O Section
============================================================
Form number: ACORD 832MN
Edition: 2016-03
Title: Minnesota Miscellaneous E&O Section
Type: Insurance form (ACORD industry standard, Minnesota state variant)
States: MN
Policy term: 12 months (typical for Misc E&O — claims-made)
Transaction types: New business, renewal, midterm change

Purpose:
Minnesota-specific section for Miscellaneous Errors & Omissions
(E&O) coverage — the catch-all professional liability section for
professions that don't have a named-line professional liability
policy (Lawyers, Accountants, Architects/Engineers, Medical
Professionals have their own). Common Misc E&O insureds include
real estate professionals, consultants, IT service providers (where
not on cyber/tech E&O), home inspectors, travel agents, etc. Used
alongside ACORD 125.

Captures:
- Insured entity / professional information
- Type of professional service performed (detailed description
  required for proper underwriting)
- Years in business / years performing this service
- Staff size, professional qualifications/licenses
- Annual gross receipts / professional service revenue
- Client / customer profile (number, types, contract structures)
- Standard contract terms used (LOEs, hold-harmless provisions)
- Prior E&O coverage and claims
- Underwriting questions:
  * Prior claims, regulatory actions, license suspensions
  * Subcontractor / vendor exposures
  * Highest-fee-per-engagement client
  * Largest contract / project size in past year
- Coverage selections:
  * Limits and retention
  * Retro date / prior acts coverage
  * Defense outside or inside limits
  * Specific service exclusions or sub-limits
- Minnesota-required disclosures and acknowledgments
- Authorized signatory and date

When used:
- New Minnesota Miscellaneous E&O submission — section attached to
  ACORD 125
- Renewal where Misc E&O coverage is being modified
- Reporting significant service-offering changes

Notes:
- Misc E&O is claims-made — retro date and continuity matter
- "Misc" is a category, not a coverage form — many carriers use
  insured-class-specific Misc E&O forms (consultant, real estate
  agent, etc.) rather than one generic form
- Companion forms: 807MN (D&O), 827MN (EPLI), 828MN (Fiduciary),
  833MN (Lawyers PL), 834MN (Cyber), 838MN (Accountants PL)
- For tech / IT exposures, may overlap with cyber (834MN) — careful
  coverage coordination needed
