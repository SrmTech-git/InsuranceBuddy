ACORD 827MN (2016-03) — Minnesota Employment Practices Liability Insurance Section
===================================================================================
Form number: ACORD 827MN
Edition: 2016-03
Title: Minnesota Employment Practices Liability Insurance Section
Type: Insurance form (ACORD industry standard, Minnesota state variant)
States: MN
Policy term: 12 months (typical for EPLI — claims-made)
Transaction types: New business, renewal, midterm change (employee count changes, M&A, layoffs)

Purpose:
Minnesota-specific section for Employment Practices Liability
Insurance (EPLI) — covering employer liability for wrongful acts in
employment relationships: discrimination, harassment, wrongful
termination, retaliation, failure to promote, failure to accommodate,
hostile work environment, and similar claims by employees, applicants,
or (with third-party coverage) non-employees. Used alongside ACORD 125.

Captures:
- Employer information
- Employee count: full-time, part-time, by state / location
- Industry and risk factors
- HR practices: employee handbook, anti-discrimination training,
  anti-harassment training, exit interview process, performance
  review process, complaint procedures
- Recent workforce actions: layoffs (number, timing), recent
  reorganizations, recent M&A activity
- Wage and hour compliance practices
- Prior EPLI coverage and claims
- Pending or threatened employment claims
- Underwriting questions: prior EEOC charges, prior MN Department
  of Human Rights complaints, terminated employees with severance
  agreements, pending NLRB actions
- Coverage selections:
  * Limits (per claim, aggregate)
  * Retention / deductible
  * Third-party coverage (claims by non-employees: customers,
    vendors — typically discrimination by your employees against them)
  * Wage and hour sub-limits (often excluded entirely or sub-limited)
  * Defense outside or inside limits
- Minnesota-required disclosures and acknowledgments
- Authorized signatory and date

When used:
- New Minnesota EPLI submission — section attached to ACORD 125
- Renewal where EPLI coverage is being modified
- Reporting significant employee-count or practices changes

Notes:
- EPLI is claims-made — retro date matters
- Wage and hour claims (FLSA-related) are increasingly excluded or
  heavily sub-limited; check carrier appetite
- Companion forms: 807MN (D&O), 828MN (Fiduciary), 832MN (Misc E&O)
- Minnesota-specific note: Minnesota Human Rights Act provides
  broader employment protections than federal Title VII — MN-specific
  exposure may be higher
