ACORD 807MN (2016-03) — Minnesota Directors & Officers Liability Insurance Section
===================================================================================
Form number: ACORD 807MN
Edition: 2016-03
Title: Minnesota Directors & Officers Liability Insurance Section
Type: Insurance form (ACORD industry standard, Minnesota state variant)
States: MN
Policy term: 12 months (typical for D&O — claims-made)
Transaction types: New business, renewal, midterm change (M&A activity, capital structure changes)

Purpose:
Minnesota-specific section for Directors & Officers (D&O) Liability
coverage. Captures the underwriting information needed to underwrite
a D&O policy — covering individual directors, officers, and (depending
on coverage) the company itself, for "wrongful acts" in their
capacity as such. Used alongside national ACORD 125 (Commercial
Applicant Information Section) as the line-of-business section.

Captures:
- Insured entity information (corporate name, FEIN, ownership)
- Entity type: public (SEC-reporting), private, non-profit, financial
  institution
- Industry / SIC / NAICS classification
- Financial information: revenue, total assets, net worth
- Subsidiaries and ownership percentages
- Number of directors and officers
- D&O coverage selections:
  * Side A — coverage for individual D&Os when the company cannot
    indemnify (e.g., bankruptcy, derivative suits, prohibited by law)
  * Side B — reimbursement to company for indemnifying D&Os
  * Side C — entity coverage for the company itself (typically
    securities claims for public companies)
- Limits and retention selected
- Defense outside or inside policy limits selection
- Prior D&O coverage carrier(s), limits, retro date
- Prior claims history (D&O claims, regulatory actions)
- Underwriting questions: prior bankruptcy, pending litigation,
  pending M&A activity, IPO plans, recent stock listing status,
  going-concern issues
- Minnesota-required disclosures and acknowledgments
- Authorized signatory and date

When used:
- New Minnesota D&O submission — section attached to ACORD 125
- Renewal where D&O coverage is being modified
- Reporting significant entity changes affecting D&O exposure
  (M&A, IPO, going public, governance changes)

Notes:
- D&O is claims-made coverage — retro date and prior-acts coverage
  matter significantly for continuity
- Companion to ACORD 125 (Commercial Applicant Info)
- Public-company D&O (with Side C entity coverage) is more complex
  underwriting than private/non-profit D&O
- Subset of related management liability forms: 827MN (EPLI),
  828MN (Fiduciary), 832MN (Misc E&O); often packaged together
