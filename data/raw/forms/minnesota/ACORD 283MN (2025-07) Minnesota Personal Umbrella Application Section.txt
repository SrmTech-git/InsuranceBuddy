ACORD 283MN (2025-07) — Minnesota Personal Umbrella Application Section
========================================================================
Form number: ACORD 283MN
Edition: 2025-07
Title: Minnesota Personal Umbrella Application Section
Type: Insurance form (ACORD industry standard, Minnesota state variant)
States: MN
Policy term: Matches the underlying umbrella policy (typically 12 months)
Transaction types: New business, renewal, increasing limits, midterm change to underlying policies

Purpose:
Personal umbrella-specific section of a Minnesota personal lines
submission. Used in combination with ACORD 88 (Personal Insurance
Application — Applicant Information Section), which captures applicant
identity. The 283MN holds the umbrella-specific data: underlying
policies, household exposures, umbrella limit selection, and
Minnesota-required disclosures.

Captures:
- Underlying auto policy: carrier, policy number, BI/PD limits,
  UM/UIM limits, no-fault PIP
- Underlying homeowners policy: carrier, policy number, personal
  liability and medical payments limits
- Underlying watercraft policy: carrier, policy number, BI/PD limits
  (significant in Minnesota)
- Underlying recreational vehicle policy: carrier, limits — snowmobile,
  ATV, recreational vehicle are common in Minnesota
- Other underlying policies as applicable
- Umbrella limit selected ($1M, $2M, $5M, $10M)
- Drivers and licensed members of household
- Vehicles, properties, watercraft, recreational vehicles owned
- Loss history (typically 5 years)
- Underwriting questions: prior umbrella, prior umbrella claims,
  professional/occupational exposures, youthful drivers, business use
- Minnesota-required disclosures, including Insurance Guaranty
  Association notice
- Fraud statement and applicant signature
(NOTE: Applicant identity is captured on ACORD 88, not duplicated here)

When used:
- New Minnesota personal umbrella submission — when agency uses
  ACORD's modular section system (88 + 283MN) rather than standalone
  83MN
- Renewal where the umbrella limit is being modified while reusing
  applicant info from 88
- Adding/removing underlying policies in a modular-section package

Notes:
- Section version (283MN) drops applicant identity; standalone version
  is 83MN
- Minnesota's recreational underlyings (snowmobile, ATV, watercraft)
  often factor prominently into the umbrella underwriting picture
- Companion forms: 88 (Applicant Info), 282MN (Watercraft Section),
  290MN (Auto Section)
