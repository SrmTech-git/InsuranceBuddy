ACORD 282MN (2025-07) — Minnesota Watercraft Section
=====================================================
Form number: ACORD 282MN
Edition: 2025-07
Title: Minnesota Watercraft Section
Type: Insurance form (ACORD industry standard, Minnesota state variant)
States: MN
Policy term: Matches the underlying watercraft policy (typically 12 months or seasonal)
Transaction types: New business, renewal, adding/removing watercraft

Purpose:
Watercraft-specific section of a Minnesota personal lines submission.
Used in combination with ACORD 88 (Personal Insurance Application —
Applicant Information Section), which captures applicant identity.
The 282MN holds the watercraft-specific data: vessels, operators,
coverages, and Minnesota-required disclosures.

Captures:
- Watercraft schedule, each with:
  * Type, make, model, year, length, beam, HIN
  * Engine details (HP, propulsion type)
  * Hull material, insured value
- Operators: name, DOB, boating experience, safety course completion
- Use: pleasure, water-skiing, fishing, etc.
- Storage: in-water, on-trailer, storage facility; seasonal vs
  year-round
- Coverage: hull, liability (with companion 67MN BI decline if
  applicable), medical payments, uninsured boater, fuel spill,
  towing, trailer
- Limits and deductibles
- Lienholder information
- Prior insurance and loss history
- Minnesota-required disclosures and acknowledgments
- Fraud statement and applicant signature
(NOTE: Applicant identity is captured on ACORD 88, not duplicated here)

When used:
- New Minnesota watercraft submission — when agency uses ACORD's
  modular section system (88 + 282MN) rather than standalone 82MN
- Renewal where watercraft data needs refresh while reusing applicant
  info from 88
- Adding watercraft to an existing modular-section package

Notes:
- Minnesota's boat BI decline option is captured on companion form
  67MN (Personal Boat Insurance Notice Election to Decline BI)
- Section version (282MN) drops applicant identity; standalone version
  is 82MN
