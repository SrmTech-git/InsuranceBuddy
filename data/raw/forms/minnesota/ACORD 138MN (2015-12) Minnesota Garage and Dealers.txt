ACORD 138MN (2015-12) — Minnesota Garage and Dealers
=====================================================
Form number: ACORD 138MN
Edition: 2015-12
Title: Minnesota Garage and Dealers
Type: Insurance form (ACORD industry standard, Minnesota state variant)
States: MN
Policy term: 12 months (typical for commercial garage policy)
Transaction types: New business, renewal, midterm change, audit-driven adjustment

Purpose:
Minnesota-specific Garage and Dealers section. Captures business
information, exposures, coverages, and Minnesota's no-fault PIP / UM /
UIM elections for a Garage / Dealers policy under Minn. Stat. § 65B.
State-specific overlay to the national ACORD 128 (Garage and Dealers
Section).

Captures:
- Insured business type: new-car dealer, used-car dealer, service station,
  body shop, repair shop, towing operation, etc.
- Dealer / business license number(s)
- Premises: locations, age, square footage, lot size
- Operations: gross receipts, vehicles in inventory / sold / serviced
- Garage liability (premises and operations)
- Auto Liability (covered autos)
- Mandatory PIP basic economic loss benefits for covered autos
- Garagekeepers (CCC — care, custody, control)
- Products and Completed Operations
- UM/UIM for covered autos (typically at limits equal to liability)
- Minnesota-required dealer disclosures, including Insurance Guaranty
  Association notice
- Acknowledgments and signatures

When used:
- New garage policy submission for a Minnesota auto-related business
- Renewal where Minnesota-specific coverages or dealer info are reselected
- Adding/removing locations or operations
- Midterm endorsement that changes coverage or operations

Notes:
- Minnesota is a no-fault state — mandatory PIP applies to garage
  covered autos as well as personal
- Companion forms: 65MN (Guaranty Association Notice), 62MN (Auto Supplement)
- Minnesota dealer regulations are administered by the Minnesota
  Department of Public Safety, Driver and Vehicle Services Division
- Minnesota personal-auto minimums applicable to garage covered autos:
  30/60/10 (Minn. Stat. § 65B). Mandatory PIP $40K applies.
- State regulator: Minnesota Department of Commerce
