ACORD 62MN (2000-01) — Minnesota Auto Supplement
=================================================
Form number: ACORD 62MN
Edition: 2000-01
Title: Minnesota Auto Supplement
Type: Insurance form (ACORD industry standard, Minnesota state variant)
States: MN
Policy term: Matches the underlying auto policy term; elections persist until changed in writing
Transaction types: New business (Minnesota-required disclosures and elections); change to coverages or limits

Purpose:
General-purpose Minnesota auto supplement attached to personal and
commercial auto submissions. Captures Minnesota-specific disclosures
and coverage elections required under Minnesota's No-Fault Automobile
Insurance Act (Minn. Stat. § 65B), including PIP basic economic loss
benefit selection and UM / UIM coverage elections.

Captures:
- PIP basic economic loss benefits selection (medical + non-medical /
  wage loss / replacement services / funeral — statutorily required)
- UM coverage selection (typically at limits equal to liability)
- UIM coverage selection (typically at limits equal to liability)
- Other Minnesota-required disclosures and acknowledgments
- Insured signature and date

When used:
- New Minnesota auto submission — PIP and UM/UIM elections required
- Renewal where Minnesota-specific coverages are being modified
- Midterm change

Notes:
- Minnesota is a no-fault state — PIP basic economic loss benefits are
  mandatory at statutory amounts
- UM and UIM typically required at limits equal to liability unless
  rejected in writing
- Companion forms: 38MN (Authorization), 65MN (Guaranty Association
  Notice), 67MN (Personal Boat Insurance BI Decline — for boats)
