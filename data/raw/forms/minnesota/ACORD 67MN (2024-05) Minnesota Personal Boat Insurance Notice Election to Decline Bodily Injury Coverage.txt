ACORD 67MN (2024-05) — Minnesota Personal Boat Insurance Notice Election to Decline Bodily Injury Coverage
===========================================================================================================
Form number: ACORD 67MN
Edition: 2024-05
Title: Minnesota Personal Boat Insurance Notice Election to Decline Bodily Injury Coverage
Type: Insurance form (ACORD industry standard, Minnesota state variant)
States: MN
Policy term: Matches the underlying watercraft policy term; declination persists until changed in writing
Transaction types: New business (BI decline election); change to boat liability coverage

Purpose:
Minnesota-specific notice and election form for declining Bodily
Injury (BI) liability coverage on a personal watercraft (boat) policy.
Minnesota statute requires carriers to offer BI liability on
watercraft policies; the insured may decline BI coverage in writing
on this form.

Captures:
- Statutory notice describing watercraft BI liability coverage and
  what it pays (injuries to other persons caused by the insured's
  watercraft operation)
- Disclosure of the consequences of declining BI coverage (insured
  bears personal responsibility for injury claims; no defense
  provided by the carrier)
- Insured's election to decline BI coverage on the boat policy
- Insured signature and date

When used:
- New Minnesota watercraft policy submission where the insured wants
  to decline BI coverage
- Renewal where the BI election is being changed (declining or
  reinstating)
- Midterm change to boat liability coverage

Notes:
- Declining boat BI is unusual but permitted in Minnesota; most
  insureds purchase BI coverage because watercraft-on-watercraft and
  watercraft-on-swimmer injuries can result in significant liability
- Property damage liability on the boat policy is typically separate
  and not declined on this form
- Companion forms: 82MN (Watercraft Application — standalone), 282MN
  (Watercraft Section), umbrella policy may also be implicated if BI
  is declined
