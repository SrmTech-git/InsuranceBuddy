ACORD 90IA (2015-12) — Iowa Personal Auto Application
======================================================
Form number: ACORD 90IA
Edition: 2015-12
Title: Iowa Personal Auto Application
Type: Insurance form (ACORD industry standard, Iowa state variant)
States: IA
Policy term: 6 months (typical for personal auto); 12-month terms also issued depending on carrier
Transaction types: New business; renewal applications (where required); adding driver/vehicle; significant midterm changes

Purpose:
Standalone application for a personal auto policy in Iowa. Captures the
applicant, drivers, vehicles, coverages, and state-required disclosures
needed to underwrite, bind, and issue an Iowa auto policy under Iowa's
Financial Responsibility framework (Iowa Code 321A).

Captures:
- Producer / agency information
- Applicant: name, address, DOB, marital status, occupation, contact
- Driver list: license number/status, DOB, marital status, occupation
- Driver history (typically 3-5 years): accidents, violations, suspensions
- Vehicle list: VIN, year/make/model, body type, garaging address
- Vehicle use: pleasure / commute / business, annual mileage
- Lienholder / additional interest information
- Coverages: BI, PD, UM/UIM, MedPay, comp, coll
- Deductibles (comp and coll)
- Optional coverages
- Discounts claimed
- Prior insurance carrier and lapse history
- Loss history
- Iowa-required disclosures and acknowledgments
- Fraud statement and applicant signature

When used:
- New business — applying for an Iowa personal auto policy
- Renewal applications (where required)
- Adding a driver or vehicle to an existing Iowa policy
- Midterm changes that require fresh underwriting

Notes:
- Iowa-specific supplements typically completed alongside this app:
  - 61IA — UM/UIM Offer/Rejection
  - 62IA — Notice regarding UM/UIM Coverage
  - 64IA — UM/UIM Stacking Coverage Notice
  - 65IA — Aftermarket Crash Parts
  - 66IA — Disclosure Use of Claims History
  - 67IA — Extraordinary Life Circumstances Disclosure
- Section version (290IA) drops applicant identity in favor of ACORD 88
- Iowa minimum liability limits: 20/40/15 (Iowa Code § 321A.21) — among
  the lowest BI minimums in the U.S. Alternative: $55,000 surety bond
  or cash deposit with Iowa DOT.
- NOTE: A proposed Iowa law to raise minimums to 50/100/25 has been
  discussed but not yet passed as of 2025
- Iowa UM/UIM: insurer must OFFER at minimum limits; insured may reject
  in writing (Iowa Code § 516A.1). UM applies to BI only — does NOT
  cover vehicle damage (explicit notice required). UIM is REDUCED-BY
  (DIL) — UIM limits must exceed tortfeasor's BI for coverage to apply.
- Iowa is at-fault with MODIFIED COMPARATIVE FAULT (51% bar)
  (Iowa Code § 668.3)
- Primary statutes: Iowa Code § 321A.21 (minimums), § 516A.1 (UM/UIM),
  Ch. 515D (Cancellation Control Act), § 507B.4 (Unfair Claims)
- State regulator: Iowa Insurance Division (IID), part of Iowa
  Department of Commerce
- Electronic verification: Iowa DOT system. Iowa does NOT require
  proof at vehicle registration but driver must carry proof while
  driving.
