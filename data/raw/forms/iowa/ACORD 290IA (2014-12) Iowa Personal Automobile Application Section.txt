ACORD 290IA (2014-12) — Iowa Personal Automobile Application Section
=====================================================================
Form number: ACORD 290IA
Edition: 2014-12
Title: Iowa Personal Automobile Application Section
Type: Insurance form (ACORD industry standard, Iowa state variant)
States: IA
Policy term: Matches the underlying personal auto policy term (typically 6 or 12 months)
Transaction types: New business, renewal, midterm change — used as the auto-specific section in a modular ACORD application package

Purpose:
Auto-specific section of a personal auto application in Iowa. Used in
combination with ACORD 88 (Personal Insurance Application — Applicant
Information Section), which captures applicant identity. The 290IA
holds the auto-specific data needed to underwrite an Iowa auto policy
under Iowa Code 321A.

Captures:
- Drivers: license number/status, DOB, marital status, occupation,
  3-5 year violation/accident history
- Vehicles: VIN, year/make/model, body type, garaging address
- Vehicle use: pleasure / commute / business, annual mileage
- Lienholder / additional interest information
- Coverages: BI, PD, UM/UIM, MedPay, comp, coll
- Deductibles (comp and coll)
- Optional coverages and discounts
- Prior insurance carrier and lapse history
- Loss history
- Iowa-required disclosures and acknowledgments
- Fraud statement and applicant signature
(NOTE: Applicant identity is captured on ACORD 88, not duplicated here)

When used:
- New business — when the agency uses ACORD's modular section system
  (88 + 290IA) rather than the standalone full app (90IA)
- Renewal where the carrier requires fresh auto-section data while
  reusing applicant info from 88
- Adding a vehicle or driver to an existing modular-section policy

Notes:
- Iowa-specific supplements typically completed alongside this section:
  61IA, 62IA, 64IA (UM/UIM offer / notice / stacking); 65IA (aftermarket
  parts); 66IA (claims history); 67IA (extraordinary life circumstances)
- Use 290IA with ACORD 88; use 90IA standalone
- Iowa minimum liability limits: 20/40/15 (Iowa Code § 321A.21) —
  proof of financial responsibility framework; insurance is the
  primary compliance method. Alternative: $55K surety bond.
- Iowa UM/UIM: must offer; rejectable in writing (Iowa Code § 516A.1);
  UIM is REDUCED-BY structure
- Iowa is at-fault with modified comparative fault (51% bar)
- State regulator: Iowa Insurance Division (IID)
- Electronic verification: Iowa DOT
