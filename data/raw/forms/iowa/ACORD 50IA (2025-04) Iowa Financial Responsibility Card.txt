ACORD 50IA (2025-04) — Iowa Financial Responsibility Card
==========================================================
Form number: ACORD 50IA
Edition: 2025-04
Title: Iowa Financial Responsibility Card
Type: Insurance form (ACORD industry standard, Iowa state variant)
States: IA
Policy term: Matches the underlying auto policy term (typically 6 or 12 months); reissued at each renewal
Transaction types: N/A (proof-of-insurance card, not a transaction document)

Purpose:
Proof of liability insurance issued under an Iowa personal auto policy.
Iowa requires drivers to demonstrate financial responsibility, typically
satisfied by carrying liability insurance meeting state minimum limits.

Captures:
- Insured's name and address
- Policy number
- Policy effective and expiration dates
- Year, make, model, and VIN of the insured vehicle
- Insurer name and NAIC code
- Agent / agency name and contact (if applicable)

When used:
- Carried in the vehicle as proof of insurance
- Presented to law enforcement during a traffic stop or accident investigation
- Presented to the Iowa Department of Transportation for registration / DOT processes
- Required after an at-fault accident to demonstrate financial responsibility

Notes:
- Iowa's terminology uses "Financial Responsibility" rather than
  "Identification Card" — reflects the state statute (Iowa Code 321A)
