ACORD 137IA (2015-12) — Iowa Commercial Auto
=============================================
Form number: ACORD 137IA
Edition: 2015-12
Title: Iowa Commercial Auto
Type: Insurance form (ACORD industry standard, Iowa state variant)
States: IA
Policy term: 12 months (typical for commercial auto)
Transaction types: New business, renewal, midterm change, audit-driven adjustment

Purpose:
Iowa-specific Commercial Auto Coverages section. Captures coverage
selections, limits, deductibles, and Iowa-required disclosures for a
commercial auto policy. Used alongside national ACORD 125 (Applicant
Info) and ACORD 127 (Business Auto Section).

Captures:
- Liability limits (BI/PD split or Combined Single Limit)
- Comp / collision deductibles
- UM / UIM coverage selections and limits
- MedPay
- Hired Auto Liability
- Non-Owned Auto Liability
- Drive Other Car coverage (where applicable)
- Towing & labor / rental reimbursement / loss of use
- Trailer interchange (for trucking risks)
- Iowa-required disclosures and acknowledgments

When used:
- New commercial auto submission for an Iowa-based fleet
- Renewal where Iowa-specific coverages are being reselected
- Midterm endorsement that changes coverage or limits

Notes:
- Iowa-specific UM/UIM supplement family (61IA, 62IA, 64IA) may apply
  to commercial autos as well as personal — depends on carrier and use
- Iowa personal-auto minimum limits: 20/40/15 (Iowa Code § 321A.21).
  Commercial fleets subject to federal MCS-90 for interstate operations.
- Iowa UM/UIM: must offer; rejectable in writing (Iowa Code § 516A.1)
- State regulator: Iowa Insurance Division (IID)
