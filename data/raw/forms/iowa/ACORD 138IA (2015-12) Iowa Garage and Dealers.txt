ACORD 138IA (2015-12) — Iowa Garage and Dealers
================================================
Form number: ACORD 138IA
Edition: 2015-12
Title: Iowa Garage and Dealers
Type: Insurance form (ACORD industry standard, Iowa state variant)
States: IA
Policy term: 12 months (typical for commercial garage policy)
Transaction types: New business, renewal, midterm change, audit-driven adjustment

Purpose:
Iowa-specific Garage and Dealers section. Captures business
information, exposures, coverages, and Iowa-required disclosures for a
Garage / Dealers policy. State-specific overlay to the national ACORD
128 (Garage and Dealers Section).

Captures:
- Insured business type: new-car dealer, used-car dealer, service station,
  body shop, repair shop, towing operation, etc.
- Dealer / business license number(s)
- Premises: locations, age, square footage, lot size
- Operations: gross receipts, vehicles in inventory / sold / serviced
- Garage liability (premises and operations)
- Auto Liability (covered autos)
- Garagekeepers (CCC — care, custody, control)
- Products and Completed Operations
- UM/UIM for covered autos
- Iowa-required dealer disclosures and acknowledgments

When used:
- New garage policy submission for an Iowa auto-related business
- Renewal where Iowa-specific coverages or dealer info are reselected
- Adding/removing locations or operations
- Midterm endorsement that changes coverage or operations

Notes:
- Iowa dealer regulations are administered by the Iowa Department of
  Transportation (Motor Vehicle Division)
- Iowa personal-auto minimums applicable to garage covered autos:
  20/40/15 (Iowa Code § 321A.21)
- State regulator: Iowa Insurance Division (IID)
