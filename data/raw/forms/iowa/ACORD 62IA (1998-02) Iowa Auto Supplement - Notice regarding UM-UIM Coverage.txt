ACORD 62IA (1998-02) — Iowa Auto Supplement – Notice regarding UM/UIM Coverage
===============================================================================
Form number: ACORD 62IA
Edition: 1998-02
Title: Iowa Auto Supplement – Notice regarding UM/UIM Coverage
Type: Insurance form (ACORD industry standard, Iowa state variant)
States: IA
Policy term: Matches the underlying auto policy term; notice provided at issuance and renewal
Transaction types: New business; renewal (notice may be reissued)

Purpose:
Statutory notice form describing Iowa's Uninsured Motorists (UM) and
Underinsured Motorists (UIM) coverage rights to the insured. Separate
from the election form (61IA), this is a pure informational notice
delivered as part of an Iowa auto policy issuance.

Captures:
- Description of UM coverage purpose and how it pays
- Description of UIM coverage purpose and how it pays
- Statutory disclosure language required under Iowa Code 516A
- Insured acknowledgment of receipt

When used:
- New Iowa auto submission — notice delivered with offer/rejection form
- Renewal — many carriers deliver the notice annually
- Whenever required by statute to refresh the insured's understanding

Notes:
- Pure informational notice — the actual election occurs on 61IA
- Companion forms: 61IA (UM/UIM Offer/Rejection) and 64IA (Stacking Notice)
- The 1998-02 edition is very old; [TBD — verify whether this is still
  the current ACORD-published edition or whether it has been superseded
  by a newer notice]
- Iowa UM/UIM statutory authority: Iowa Code § 516A.1; UM/UIM must be
  offered at minimum limits, rejectable in writing
- Iowa UM applies to BI ONLY — does not cover vehicle property damage
  (explicit notice required by Iowa Admin. Code 191-15.10)
- Iowa minimum BI: 20/40 (§ 321A.21); UM minimum tracks BI minimum
