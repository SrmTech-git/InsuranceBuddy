ACORD 61IA (2002-02) — Iowa Auto Supplement – UM/UIM Offer/Rejection
=====================================================================
Form number: ACORD 61IA
Edition: 2002-02
Title: Iowa Auto Supplement – UM/UIM Offer/Rejection
Type: Insurance form (ACORD industry standard, Iowa state variant)
States: IA
Policy term: Matches the underlying auto policy term (typically 6 or 12 months); election persists until changed in writing
Transaction types: New business (offer/rejection required); change of UM/UIM limits

Purpose:
Captures the Iowa insured's election regarding Uninsured Motorists (UM)
and Underinsured Motorists (UIM) coverage. UM is statutorily offered;
UIM is statutorily offered and may be rejected in writing under Iowa
Code 516A. This form documents both offers and any rejection.

Captures:
- UM coverage offer at statutory minimum (or higher selected limit)
- UIM coverage offer at statutory minimum (or higher selected limit)
- Selection of higher UM/UIM limits if elected
- Rejection of UIM coverage (in writing per statute)
- Insured acknowledgment and signature

When used:
- New Iowa auto submission — UM offer / UIM offer-or-rejection required
- Renewal where UM/UIM limits are being modified
- Midterm change to UM/UIM coverage

Notes:
- Iowa UM/UIM: insurer must OFFER at minimum limits; insured may REJECT
  in writing (Iowa Code § 516A.1). Both UM and UIM are rejectable.
- Iowa UM applies to BODILY INJURY ONLY — does not cover vehicle
  property damage (explicit notice required on all Iowa policies)
- Iowa UIM is REDUCED-BY (DIL) structure — UIM limits must exceed
  tortfeasor's BI limits for coverage to apply; pays the difference
- Iowa minimum BI/UM minimums: 20/40 (Iowa Code § 321A.21)
- Companion forms: 62IA (UM/UIM Notice) and 64IA (Stacking Notice)
- Authority: Iowa Code § 516A.1; Iowa Admin. Code 191-15.10
