ACORD 138OH (2015-12) — Ohio Garage and Dealers
================================================
Form number: ACORD 138OH
Edition: 2015-12
Title: Ohio Garage and Dealers
Type: Insurance form (ACORD industry standard, Ohio state variant)
States: OH
Policy term: 12 months (typical for commercial garage policy)
Transaction types: New business, renewal, midterm change, audit-driven adjustment

Purpose:
Ohio-specific Garage and Dealers section. Captures business
information, exposures, coverages, and Ohio-required disclosures for a
Garage / Dealers policy under ORC 4509 (Financial Responsibility) and
ORC 3937 (Auto Insurance). State-specific overlay to the national
ACORD 128 (Garage and Dealers Section).

Captures:
- Insured business type: new-car dealer, used-car dealer, service station,
  body shop, repair shop, towing operation, etc.
- Dealer / business license number(s)
- Premises: locations, age, square footage, lot size
- Operations: gross receipts, vehicles in inventory / sold / serviced
- Garage liability (premises and operations)
- Auto Liability (covered autos)
- Garagekeepers (CCC — care, custody, control)
- Products and Completed Operations
- UM/UIM for covered autos (Ohio rules apply per ORC 3937.18)
- Ohio-required dealer disclosures and acknowledgments

When used:
- New garage policy submission for an Ohio auto-related business
- Renewal where Ohio-specific coverages or dealer info are reselected
- Adding/removing locations or operations
- Midterm endorsement that changes coverage or operations

Notes:
- Ohio personal-auto minimum limits: 25/50/25 (ORC § 4509) — apply to
  garage covered autos
- Ohio UM/UIM is NOT required and has NO mandatory-offer requirement
  since 2001 (ORC § 3937.18 as amended by S.B. 97). Insurer may omit
  UM/UIM entirely — applies to garage covered autos as well.
- Ohio dealer regulations are administered by the Ohio BMV (Dealer
  Licensing Section)
