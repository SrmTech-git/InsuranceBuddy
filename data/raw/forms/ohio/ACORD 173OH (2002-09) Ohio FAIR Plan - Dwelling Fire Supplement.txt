ACORD 173OH (2002-09) — Ohio FAIR Plan – Dwelling Fire Supplement
==================================================================
Form number: ACORD 173OH
Edition: 2002-09
Title: Ohio FAIR Plan – Dwelling Fire Supplement
Type: Insurance form (ACORD industry standard, Ohio state variant)
States: OH
Policy term: 12 months (matches the master 171OH FAIR Plan submission)
Transaction types: New business; renewal; midterm change

Purpose:
Dwelling Fire (DP) supplement to the Ohio FAIR Plan Basic Property
Application (171OH). Captures information specific to non-owner-
occupied residential properties — rentals, seasonal homes, vacant
dwellings — covered under a DP-style FAIR Plan policy.

Captures:
- Occupancy: rental / seasonal / vacant / under renovation
- Tenant information (multi-unit dwellings)
- Dwelling coverage form selection (DP-1 / DP-2 / DP-3 style or FAIR
  Plan equivalent where available)
- Fair rental value coverage
- Additional living expense (where available)
- Personal property limits (insured's contents, where any are stored)
- Special endorsements or limits relevant to dwelling fire
- Applicant signature and date

When used:
- Non-owner-occupied residential FAIR Plan submission — attached to 171OH
- Renewal of existing FAIR Plan dwelling fire coverage
- Reporting occupancy or property changes

Notes:
- Used in combination with the master 171OH (Basic Property Application)
- Dwelling Fire FAIR Plan coverage typically excludes liability (no
  landlord liability) — separate liability needed
- Vacant properties typically have tighter underwriting and may
  require specific endorsements
- Companion supplements: 172OH (Homeowners — for owner-occupied),
  174OH (Commercial), 175OH (Farm), 176OH (Rehabilitation), 177OH
  (Bowling Alley)
