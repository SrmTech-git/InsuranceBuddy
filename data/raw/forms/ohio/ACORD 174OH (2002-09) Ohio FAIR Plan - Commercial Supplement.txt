ACORD 174OH (2002-09) — Ohio FAIR Plan – Commercial Supplement
===============================================================
Form number: ACORD 174OH
Edition: 2002-09
Title: Ohio FAIR Plan – Commercial Supplement
Type: Insurance form (ACORD industry standard, Ohio state variant)
States: OH
Policy term: 12 months (matches the master 171OH FAIR Plan submission)
Transaction types: New business; renewal; midterm change

Purpose:
Commercial property supplement to the Ohio FAIR Plan Basic Property
Application (171OH). Captures information specific to commercial
properties — retail, office, light manufacturing, vacant commercial,
small mercantile, etc. — covered under a commercial FAIR Plan policy.

Captures:
- Commercial occupancy classification (mercantile, service, office,
  manufacturing, vacant, etc.)
- Tenant information (multi-tenant buildings)
- Building coverage (building only, building + contents, contents only)
- Business personal property limits
- Tenants' improvements and betterments
- Business income / extra expense (if available through FAIR Plan)
- Special hazards or features of the operation
- Applicant signature and date

When used:
- Commercial property FAIR Plan submission — attached to 171OH
- Renewal of existing FAIR Plan commercial property coverage
- Reporting occupancy or commercial use changes

Notes:
- Used in combination with the master 171OH (Basic Property Application)
- Commercial FAIR Plan coverage is typically named-perils only (fire,
  extended coverage, VMM) — broader perils require voluntary-market or
  excess-and-surplus-lines placement
- General Liability is NOT included — separate GL needed
- Companion supplements: 172OH (Homeowners), 173OH (Dwelling Fire),
  175OH (Farm), 176OH (Rehabilitation), 177OH (Bowling Alley)
