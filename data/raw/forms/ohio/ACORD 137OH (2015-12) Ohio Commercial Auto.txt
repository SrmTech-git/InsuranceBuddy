ACORD 137OH (2015-12) — Ohio Commercial Auto
=============================================
Form number: ACORD 137OH
Edition: 2015-12
Title: Ohio Commercial Auto
Type: Insurance form (ACORD industry standard, Ohio state variant)
States: OH
Policy term: 12 months (typical for commercial auto)
Transaction types: New business, renewal, midterm change, audit-driven adjustment

Purpose:
Ohio-specific Commercial Auto Coverages section. Captures coverage
selections, limits, deductibles, and Ohio-required disclosures for a
commercial auto policy under ORC 4509 (Financial Responsibility) and
ORC 3937 (Auto Insurance). Used alongside national ACORD 125 and 127.

Captures:
- Liability limits (BI/PD split or Combined Single Limit)
- Comp / collision deductibles
- UM / UIM coverage selections and limits
- MedPay
- Hired Auto Liability
- Non-Owned Auto Liability
- Drive Other Car coverage (where applicable)
- Towing & labor / rental reimbursement / loss of use
- Trailer interchange (for trucking)
- Ohio-required disclosures and acknowledgments

When used:
- New commercial auto submission for an Ohio-based fleet
- Renewal where Ohio-specific coverages are being reselected
- Midterm endorsement that changes coverage or limits

Notes:
- Ohio personal-auto minimum limits: 25/50/25 (ORC § 4509). Commercial
  auto minimums may differ; federal MCS-90 minimums apply for
  interstate motor carriers (typically $750K-$5M depending on cargo).
- Ohio UM/UIM is NOT required and has NO mandatory-offer requirement
  since 2001 (ORC § 3937.18 as amended by S.B. 97). Insurer may omit
  UM/UIM entirely — applies to commercial as well as personal.
- Pairs with national ACORD 125 and 127
