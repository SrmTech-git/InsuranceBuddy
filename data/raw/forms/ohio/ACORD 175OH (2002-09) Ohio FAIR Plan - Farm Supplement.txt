ACORD 175OH (2002-09) — Ohio FAIR Plan – Farm Supplement
=========================================================
Form number: ACORD 175OH
Edition: 2002-09
Title: Ohio FAIR Plan – Farm Supplement
Type: Insurance form (ACORD industry standard, Ohio state variant)
States: OH
Policy term: 12 months (matches the master 171OH FAIR Plan submission)
Transaction types: New business; renewal; midterm change

Purpose:
Farm property supplement to the Ohio FAIR Plan Basic Property
Application (171OH). Captures farm-specific information — farm
dwelling, farm structures (barns, equipment sheds, silos), and other
agricultural property — covered under a FAIR Plan farm fire policy.

Captures:
- Farm acreage and use (crops, livestock, mixed agricultural)
- Farm dwelling description (separate from main 171OH if multiple)
- Farm structure schedule: each barn, equipment shed, silo, outbuilding
  with construction, year built, square footage, value
- Coverage for each scheduled structure
- Farm equipment coverage (where available)
- Livestock / produce exclusions
- Applicant signature and date

When used:
- Farm property FAIR Plan submission — attached to 171OH
- Renewal of existing FAIR Plan farm coverage
- Reporting farm property changes (added structures, structure damage,
  use change)

Notes:
- Used in combination with the master 171OH (Basic Property Application)
- Farm Fire FAIR Plan coverage is typically focused on dwelling and
  structures with named perils
- Livestock, growing crops, and farm liability typically excluded —
  separate coverage required
- Companion supplements: 172OH (Homeowners), 173OH (Dwelling Fire),
  174OH (Commercial), 176OH (Rehabilitation), 177OH (Bowling Alley)
