ACORD 66KY (2011-01) — Kentucky FAIR Plan Farm Fire Application
================================================================
Form number: ACORD 66KY
Edition: 2011-01
Title: Kentucky FAIR Plan Farm Fire Application
Type: Insurance form (ACORD industry standard, Kentucky state variant)
States: KY
Policy term: 12 months (typical for FAIR Plan farm fire)
Transaction types: New business (FAIR Plan submission); renewal; midterm change

Purpose:
Application for farm property coverage through the Kentucky FAIR
Plan — the state's residual property market — when the farm owner
cannot obtain coverage in the voluntary market. Covers the farm
dwelling, farm structures (barns, outbuildings), and other farm
improvements.

Captures:
- Insured / farm owner information
- Farm location and address
- Property description for the farm dwelling (construction, year built,
  square footage, protection class)
- Description of farm outbuildings / structures (barns, equipment
  sheds, silos, etc.) with values
- Farm acreage and use (crops, livestock, mixed)
- Coverage selected: dwelling, other structures, contents, farm
  equipment, loss of use
- Coverage amounts and deductibles
- Mortgagee / loss payee information
- Prior insurance and denial history
- Loss history
- Kentucky FAIR Plan statutory disclosures and acknowledgments
- Applicant signature and date

When used:
- Submission to the Kentucky FAIR Plan for a farm property after
  voluntary-market denial
- Renewal of existing FAIR Plan farm fire policy
- Reporting farm property changes

Notes:
- Farm Fire FAIR Plan coverage is typically more limited than voluntary
  farm policies — focused on basic fire/lightning/wind on structures
  rather than the comprehensive farm package
- Livestock, growing crops, and farm liability typically excluded —
  separate coverage required
- Companion forms: 64KY (Homeowners), 65KY (Dwelling Fire), 68KY
  (Commercial Fire)
