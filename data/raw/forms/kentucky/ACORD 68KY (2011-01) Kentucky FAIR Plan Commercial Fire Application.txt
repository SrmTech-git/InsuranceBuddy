ACORD 68KY (2011-01) — Kentucky FAIR Plan Commercial Fire Application
======================================================================
Form number: ACORD 68KY
Edition: 2011-01
Title: Kentucky FAIR Plan Commercial Fire Application
Type: Insurance form (ACORD industry standard, Kentucky state variant)
States: KY
Policy term: 12 months (typical for FAIR Plan commercial fire)
Transaction types: New business (FAIR Plan submission); renewal; midterm change

Purpose:
Application for commercial property coverage through the Kentucky
FAIR Plan — the state's residual property market — when the
commercial property owner cannot obtain coverage in the voluntary
market. Covers commercial buildings, contents, and business
improvements at properties such as small commercial buildings, vacant
commercial structures, or hard-to-place properties.

Captures:
- Insured / business information (named insured, business type)
- Property location and address
- Building description: construction type, year built, square footage,
  number of stories, occupancy class, protection class
- Tenant information (multi-tenant buildings)
- Coverage selected: building, business personal property, business
  income (if available), tenants' improvements
- Coverage amounts and deductibles
- Mortgagee / loss payee information
- Prior insurance and denial history
- Loss history
- Kentucky FAIR Plan statutory disclosures and acknowledgments
- Applicant signature and date

When used:
- Submission to the Kentucky FAIR Plan for commercial property after
  voluntary-market denial
- Renewal of existing FAIR Plan commercial fire policy
- Reporting commercial property or occupancy changes

Notes:
- Commercial FAIR Plan coverage is typically named-perils only — fire,
  lightning, extended coverage (windstorm, hail, explosion, riot,
  vehicle, smoke), vandalism / malicious mischief
- General Liability is typically NOT included in FAIR Plan commercial
  fire — separate GL coverage required
- Companion forms: 64KY (Homeowners), 65KY (Dwelling Fire), 66KY
  (Farm Fire)
