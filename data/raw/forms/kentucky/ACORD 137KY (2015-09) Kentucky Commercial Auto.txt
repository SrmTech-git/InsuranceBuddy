ACORD 137KY (2015-09) — Kentucky Commercial Auto
=================================================
Form number: ACORD 137KY
Edition: 2015-09
Title: Kentucky Commercial Auto
Type: Insurance form (ACORD industry standard, Kentucky state variant)
States: KY
Policy term: 12 months (typical for commercial auto)
Transaction types: New business, renewal, midterm change, audit-driven adjustment

Purpose:
Kentucky-specific Commercial Auto Coverages section. Captures coverage
selections, limits, deductibles, and Kentucky-required disclosures for
a commercial auto policy under Kentucky's Motor Vehicle Reparations
Act (KRS 304.39). Used alongside national ACORD 125 and ACORD 127.

Captures:
- Liability limits (BI/PD split or Combined Single Limit)
- Comp / collision deductibles
- UM / UIM coverage selections and limits
- PIP (no-fault) — applies to commercial autos in KY by default
- MedPay
- Hired Auto Liability
- Non-Owned Auto Liability
- Drive Other Car coverage (where applicable)
- Towing & labor / rental reimbursement
- Trailer interchange (for trucking)
- Kentucky-required disclosures and acknowledgments

When used:
- New commercial auto submission for a Kentucky-based fleet
- Renewal where Kentucky-specific coverages are being reselected
- Midterm endorsement that changes coverage or limits

Notes:
- Kentucky is a "choice no-fault" state — PIP applies by default to
  commercial autos; insured may reject no-fault tort threshold in writing
- Kentucky personal-auto minimum limits: 25/50/25 split or $60K CSL
  (KRS § 304.39). Commercial fleets subject to federal MCS-90 for
  interstate operations.
- KY default PIP: $10K minimum (KRS § 304.39-020); applies to commercial
  by default; rejection in writing required to remove (KRS § 304.39-060)
- State regulator: Kentucky Department of Insurance (KDI)
