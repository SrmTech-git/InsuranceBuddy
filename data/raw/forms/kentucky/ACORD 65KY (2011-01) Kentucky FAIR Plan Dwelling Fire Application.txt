ACORD 65KY (2011-01) — Kentucky FAIR Plan Dwelling Fire Application
====================================================================
Form number: ACORD 65KY
Edition: 2011-01
Title: Kentucky FAIR Plan Dwelling Fire Application
Type: Insurance form (ACORD industry standard, Kentucky state variant)
States: KY
Policy term: 12 months (typical for FAIR Plan dwelling fire)
Transaction types: New business (FAIR Plan submission); renewal; midterm change

Purpose:
Application for non-owner-occupied (rental, seasonal, vacant)
residential property coverage through the Kentucky FAIR Plan — the
state's residual property market. Dwelling Fire (DP) is the residual-
market equivalent to a voluntary DP-1/DP-2/DP-3 policy for properties
the owner does not personally occupy.

Captures:
- Insured / owner information
- Property location and address
- Property description: construction, year built, square footage,
  protection class, roof, heating
- Occupancy: rental / seasonal / vacant / under renovation
- Tenant information (where applicable)
- Coverage selected: dwelling, other structures, fair rental value,
  additional living expense, personal property of insured (if any
  stored at the location)
- Coverage amounts and deductibles
- Mortgagee / loss payee information
- Prior insurance and denial history
- Loss history (typically 5 years)
- Kentucky FAIR Plan statutory disclosures and acknowledgments
- Applicant signature and date

When used:
- Submission to the Kentucky FAIR Plan for non-owner-occupied dwellings
  after voluntary-market denial
- Renewal of existing FAIR Plan dwelling fire policy
- Reporting occupancy or property changes

Notes:
- Dwelling Fire coverage typically excludes the liability component
  (no personal liability for landlord vs. tenant disputes — separate
  landlord liability coverage may be needed)
- Vacant properties typically face higher premiums and tighter
  underwriting; some FAIR Plans restrict vacant property coverage
  to specific endorsements
- Companion forms: 64KY (Homeowners), 66KY (Farm Fire), 68KY
  (Commercial Fire)
