ACORD 137IN (2015-12) — Indiana Commercial Auto
================================================
Form number: ACORD 137IN
Edition: 2015-12
Title: Indiana Commercial Auto
Type: Insurance form (ACORD industry standard, Indiana state variant)
States: IN
Policy term: 12 months (typical for commercial auto)
Transaction types: New business, renewal, midterm change, audit-driven adjustment

Purpose:
Indiana-specific Commercial Auto Coverages section. Captures coverage
selections, limits, deductibles, and Indiana-required disclosures for a
commercial auto policy. Used alongside national ACORD 125 (Applicant
Info) and ACORD 127 (Business Auto Section).

Captures:
- Liability limits (BI/PD split or Combined Single Limit)
- Comp / collision deductibles
- UM / UIM coverage selections and limits
- MedPay
- Hired Auto Liability
- Non-Owned Auto Liability
- Drive Other Car coverage (where applicable)
- Towing & labor / rental reimbursement / loss of use
- Trailer interchange (for trucking risks)
- Indiana-required disclosures and acknowledgments

When used:
- New commercial auto submission for an Indiana-based fleet
- Renewal where Indiana-specific coverages are being reselected
- Midterm endorsement that changes coverage or limits

Notes:
- Indiana operates an electronic insurance verification system
- Indiana Financial Responsibility Law (IC 9-25) applies to commercial
  as well as personal autos
- Indiana personal-auto minimum limits: 25/50/25 (IC § 9-25). Commercial
  fleets subject to federal MCS-90 minimums for interstate operations
  (typically $750K-$5M depending on cargo).
- State insurance regulator: Indiana Department of Insurance (IDOI)
