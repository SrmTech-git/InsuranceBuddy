ACORD 290IN (2014-12) — Indiana Personal Auto Application Section
==================================================================
Form number: ACORD 290IN
Edition: 2014-12
Title: Indiana Personal Auto Application Section
Type: Insurance form (ACORD industry standard, Indiana state variant)
States: IN
Policy term: Matches the underlying personal auto policy term (typically 6 or 12 months)
Transaction types: New business, renewal, midterm change — used as the auto-specific section in a modular ACORD application package

Purpose:
Auto-specific section of a personal auto application in Indiana. Used
in combination with ACORD 88 (Personal Insurance Application —
Applicant Information Section), which captures applicant identity. The
290IN holds the auto-specific data needed to underwrite an Indiana
auto policy under IC 9-25.

Captures:
- Drivers: license number/status, DOB, marital status, occupation,
  3-5 year violation/accident history
- Vehicles: VIN, year/make/model, body type, garaging address, ownership
- Vehicle use: pleasure / commute / business, annual mileage
- Lienholder / additional interest information
- Coverages: BI, PD, UM/UIM, MedPay, comp, coll
- Deductibles (comp and coll)
- Optional coverages and discounts
- Prior insurance carrier and lapse history
- Loss history
- Indiana-required disclosures and acknowledgments
- Fraud statement and applicant signature
(NOTE: Applicant identity is captured on ACORD 88, not duplicated here)

When used:
- New business — when the agency uses ACORD's modular section system
  (88 + 290IN) rather than the standalone full app (90IN)
- Renewal where the carrier requires fresh auto-section data while
  reusing applicant info from 88
- Adding a vehicle or driver to an existing modular-section policy

Notes:
- Indiana minimum liability limits: 25/50/25 (IC § 9-25)
- Indiana UM/UIM: insurer must OFFER (IC § 27-7-6; UIM IC § 27-7-5-2);
  insured may reject in writing
- Primary statutes: IC Title 27 (Insurance), IC Title 9 (Motor Vehicles)
- State insurance regulator: Indiana Department of Insurance (IDOI)
- Electronic verification: Certificate of Compliance filed with Indiana BMV
- Use 290IN with ACORD 88; use 90IN standalone
