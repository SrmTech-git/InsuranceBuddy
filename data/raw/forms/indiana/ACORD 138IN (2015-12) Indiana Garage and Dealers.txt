ACORD 138IN (2015-12) — Indiana Garage and Dealers
===================================================
Form number: ACORD 138IN
Edition: 2015-12
Title: Indiana Garage and Dealers
Type: Insurance form (ACORD industry standard, Indiana state variant)
States: IN
Policy term: 12 months (typical for commercial garage policy)
Transaction types: New business, renewal, midterm change, audit-driven adjustment

Purpose:
Indiana-specific Garage and Dealers section. Captures business
information, exposures, coverages, and Indiana-required disclosures
for a Garage / Dealers policy. State-specific overlay to the national
ACORD 128 (Garage and Dealers Section).

Captures:
- Insured business type: new-car dealer, used-car dealer, service station,
  body shop, repair shop, towing operation, etc.
- Dealer / business license number(s)
- Premises: locations, age, square footage, lot size
- Operations: gross receipts, vehicles in inventory / sold / serviced
- Garage liability (premises and operations)
- Auto Liability (covered autos)
- Garagekeepers (CCC — care, custody, control)
- Products and Completed Operations
- UM/UIM for covered autos
- Indiana-required dealer disclosures and acknowledgments

When used:
- New garage policy submission for an Indiana auto-related business
- Renewal where Indiana-specific coverages or dealer info are reselected
- Adding/removing locations or operations
- Midterm endorsement that changes coverage or operations

Notes:
- Indiana dealer regulations are administered by the Indiana Secretary
  of State (Auto Dealer Services Division)
- Indiana personal-auto minimums applicable to garage covered autos:
  25/50/25 (IC § 9-25)
- Primary statutes: IC Title 27 (Insurance), IC Title 9 (Motor Vehicles)
