ACORD 171MI (2005-01) — Michigan Notice of Election
====================================================
Form number: ACORD 171MI
Edition: 2005-01
Title: Michigan Notice of Election (Application for Exclusion of Executive Officers of a Corporation or Members/Managers of a Limited Liability Company)
Type: Insurance form (ACORD industry standard, Michigan state variant)
States: MI
Policy term: Matches the underlying WC policy term (typically 12 months); exclusion election persists until revoked in writing
Transaction types: New WC business (officer/LLC member exclusion election); renewal; change in officer/member status

Purpose:
Michigan Workers' Compensation form by which an executive officer of
a corporation, or a member/manager of a Limited Liability Company,
elects to be individually EXCLUDED from WC coverage under the
policy. Authorized by Section 161(3) of the Michigan Workers'
Disability Compensation Act (MCL 418.161(3)), which allows an
employee who is an officer-stockholder of a corporation with no more
than 10 stockholders (and who owns at least 10% of the corporation's
stock), or a member/manager of an LLC, to elect to be individually
excluded from WC coverage by giving written notice with the
corporation's or LLC's consent.

Captures:
- Employer / policy information (named insured, FEIN, policy number)
- Entity type (corporation with ≤10 stockholders, or LLC)
- Individual's name, role (executive officer, stockholder, LLC
  member, LLC manager)
- Statement that the individual owns at least 10% of the corporation's
  stock (where applicable) and that the corporation has ≤10
  stockholders
- Statement that the individual elects to be excluded from WC coverage
- Statement that the corporation / LLC consents to the exclusion
- Acknowledgment that the excluded individual is not entitled to WC
  benefits if injured
- Individual's signature and date
- Entity authorized representative's signature

When used:
- New Michigan WC submission where an eligible executive officer or
  LLC member is electing to be excluded
- Renewal where the exclusion is being maintained or modified
- Midterm change when officer/member status changes

Notes:
- Authorizing statute: MCL 418.161(3) — Michigan Workers' Disability
  Compensation Act
- Form completes Michigan's three-form WC EXCLUSION family:
  * 171MI — Executive officers (corporations ≤10 stockholders, ≥10%
    ownership) and LLC members/managers (this form)
  * 172MI — Partner, spouse, child, or parent (family member exclusion)
  * 173MI — Specific persons excluded under MWCPF (assigned-risk) policy
- Form is executed in duplicate — one copy retained by employer, one
  filed with the carrier
- Despite the title "Notice of Election," the form is actually about
  election to be EXCLUDED — the "election" refers to the statutory
  election right under §161(3)
