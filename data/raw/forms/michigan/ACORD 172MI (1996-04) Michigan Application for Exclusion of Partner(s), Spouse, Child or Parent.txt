ACORD 172MI (1996-04) — Michigan Application for Exclusion of Partner(s), Spouse, Child or Parent
==================================================================================================
Form number: ACORD 172MI
Edition: 1996-04
Title: Michigan Application for Exclusion of Partner(s), Spouse, Child or Parent
Type: Insurance form (ACORD industry standard, Michigan state variant)
States: MI
Policy term: Matches the underlying WC policy term (typically 12 months); exclusion persists until revoked in writing
Transaction types: New business (exclusion election), renewal, change in family employee status

Purpose:
Michigan Workers' Compensation form documenting an employer's
election to exclude specified family members — partner(s), spouse,
child, or parent — from WC coverage under the policy. Michigan law
allows certain family-member employees to be excluded from WC
coverage at the employer's election; this form captures that
exclusion. Common in small family-owned businesses where the
business owner does not want to fund WC for family members.

Captures:
- Employer (named insured) information
- Policy number
- Name and role of each family member being excluded
- Relationship to employer (partner, spouse, child, parent)
- Statement that the excluded individual will not be entitled to WC
  benefits under the policy if injured
- Statement that the excluded individual's payroll will not be
  included in the WC premium calculation
- Excluded individual's signature acknowledging the exclusion and
  its consequences
- Employer / authorized signatory and date

When used:
- New Michigan WC submission where family members are being excluded
- Renewal where exclusions are being maintained or modified
- Midterm change when family member employment status changes

Notes:
- Michigan WC exclusion authorization: Michigan Workers' Disability
  Compensation Act (MCL 418.161 family-member exclusion provisions)
- Excluded family members lose WC benefit entitlement (medical and
  indemnity) — they should consider personal disability/health
  coverage alternatives
- Premium impact: excluded individuals' payroll is not subject to WC
  premium calculation
- Part of Michigan's three-form WC EXCLUSION family:
  * 171MI — Executive officers (corporations ≤10 stockholders) and
    LLC members/managers; authorized by MCL 418.161(3)
  * 172MI — Partner, spouse, child, or parent (this form)
  * 173MI — Specific persons excluded under MWCPF (assigned-risk)
