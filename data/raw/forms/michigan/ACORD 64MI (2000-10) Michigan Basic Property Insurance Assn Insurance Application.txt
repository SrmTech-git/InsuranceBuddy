ACORD 64MI (2000-10) — Michigan Basic Property Insurance Assn. Insurance Application
=====================================================================================
Form number: ACORD 64MI
Edition: 2000-10
Title: Michigan Basic Property Insurance Assn. Insurance Application
Type: Insurance form (ACORD industry standard, Michigan state variant)
States: MI
Policy term: 12 months (typical for residual-market property)
Transaction types: New business (MBPIA submission); renewal; midterm change

Purpose:
Application for property coverage through the Michigan Basic Property
Insurance Association (MBPIA) — Michigan's FAIR-Plan-equivalent
residual property insurance market. MBPIA provides coverage for
property owners who cannot obtain coverage in the voluntary market,
typically due to property condition, location (high-risk areas), or
loss history.

Captures:
- Applicant / owner information (name, address, contact)
- Property location and address
- Property description: construction type, year built, square footage,
  occupancy (owner-occupied / rental / vacant / commercial / mixed-use),
  protection class
- Coverage selected: dwelling / building, contents, additional living
  expense or fair rental value (depending on property type)
- Coverage amounts and deductibles
- Mortgagee / loss payee information
- Prior insurance carrier(s), denial dates, and reasons for denial
- Loss history (typically 5 years)
- MBPIA statutory disclosures and acknowledgments
- Applicant signature and date

When used:
- Submission to MBPIA after voluntary-market denial — applies to
  residential, dwelling fire, and commercial property exposures
- Renewal of existing MBPIA policy
- Reporting property changes

Notes:
- MBPIA = Michigan Basic Property Insurance Association
- Coverage typically named-perils only (basic fire + extended
  coverage + VMM)
- MBPIA premium is generally higher than voluntary-market coverage
