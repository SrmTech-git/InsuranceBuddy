ACORD 137MI (2020-07) — Michigan Commercial Auto Coverages (Coverages/Limits Section)
======================================================================================
Form number: ACORD 137MI
Edition: 2020-07
Title: Michigan Commercial Auto Coverages (Coverages/Limits Section)
Type: Insurance form (ACORD industry standard, Michigan state variant)
States: MI
Policy term: 12 months (typical for commercial auto)
Transaction types: New business, renewal, midterm change, audit-driven adjustment

Purpose:
Michigan-specific Commercial Auto Coverages section — the
coverages/limits portion of a Michigan commercial auto submission.
Captures coverage selections, limits, deductibles, and the PIP medical
tier selection required for commercial autos under Michigan's no-fault
statute (MCL 500.3101 et seq., as amended by the 2019 no-fault reform).
Used alongside national ACORD 125 (Applicant Info) and ACORD 127
(Business Auto Section).

Captures:
- Residual Liability (BI/PD split or Combined Single Limit)
- PIP medical (tier selection for commercial — see companion 136MI)
- PIP non-medical (wage loss, replacement services, survivor benefits)
- PPI (Property Protection Insurance — $1M statutory)
- UM / UIM coverage selections and limits
- Comp / collision deductibles
- Hired Auto Liability
- Non-Owned Auto Liability
- Drive Other Car coverage (where applicable)
- Towing & labor / rental reimbursement / loss of use
- Trailer interchange (for trucking)
- Michigan-required disclosures and acknowledgments

When used:
- New commercial auto submission for a Michigan-based fleet
- Renewal where Michigan-specific coverages are being reselected
- Anytime the PIP medical tier selection is being made or changed for
  commercial autos
- Midterm endorsement that changes coverage or limits

Notes:
- 2020-07 edition is the first major edition after the July 2, 2020
  effective date of the no-fault reform — incorporates PIP-tier
  disclosure for commercial autos
- Companion form for commercial PIP medical selection: 136MI (PIP
  Medical Selection — Commercial Business)
- Companion form for BI choice: 61MI (BI Liability Coverage Limits)
- MCCA per-vehicle assessment applies to unlimited-tier and higher
  PIP medical limits — applies to commercial fleets as well
- Federal motor carrier minimums (MCS-90) may override state minimums
  for interstate trucking risks
- Michigan default residual liability limits: 250/500 BI (MCL § 500.3009)
  with $50/100 statutory floor; PD $10K + separate mandatory PPI $1M
- Michigan UM/UIM NOT required for commercial fleets — optional
- Primary statute: MCL § 500.3101 et seq. (Michigan No-Fault Act)
- State regulator: Michigan DIFS
