ACORD 290MI (2020-07) — Michigan Personal Automobile Application Section
=========================================================================
Form number: ACORD 290MI
Edition: 2020-07
Title: Michigan Personal Automobile Application Section
Type: Insurance form (ACORD industry standard, Michigan state variant)
States: MI
Policy term: Matches the underlying personal auto policy term (typically 6 or 12 months)
Transaction types: New business, renewal, midterm change — used as the auto-specific section in a modular ACORD application package

Purpose:
Auto-specific section of a personal auto application in Michigan. Used
in combination with ACORD 88 (Personal Insurance Application —
Applicant Information Section), which captures applicant identity. The
290MI holds the auto-specific data and PIP medical tier selection
required under Michigan's no-fault statute (MCL 500.3101 et seq., as
amended by the 2019 no-fault reform).

Captures:
- Drivers: license number/status, DOB, marital status, occupation,
  3-5 year violation/accident history, qualifying health coverage
  (Medicare/Medicaid) for PIP-tier eligibility
- Vehicles: VIN, year/make/model, body type, garaging address
- Vehicle use: pleasure / commute / business, annual mileage
- Lienholder / additional interest information
- Coverages: BI/PD (Residual Liability), PIP medical (tier selection),
  PIP non-medical (wage loss / replacement services / survivor),
  PPI (Property Protection — $1M statutory), UM/UIM, comp, coll
- PIP medical tier choice: Medicaid-only opt-out (with proof),
  $50K (qualifying Medicare/health-plan coverage), $250K, $500K,
  or unlimited
- Deductibles (comp and coll)
- Optional coverages and discounts
- Prior insurance carrier and lapse history
- Loss history
- Michigan-required disclosures and acknowledgments
- Fraud statement and applicant signature
(NOTE: Applicant identity is captured on ACORD 88, not duplicated here)

When used:
- New business — when the agency uses ACORD's modular section system
  (88 + 290MI) rather than the standalone full app (90MI)
- Renewal or PIP-tier re-selection at the auto-section level while
  reusing applicant info from 88
- Adding a vehicle or driver to an existing modular-section policy

Notes:
- 2020-07 edition is the first major edition after the July 2, 2020
  effective date of the no-fault reform — incorporates PIP-tier
  disclosure
- Companion forms typically required: 61MI (BI choice), 62MI (collision
  options), 66MI (PIP medical selection — individual), 60MI / 860MI
  (MAIPF intent to reside, where applicable)
- MCCA per-vehicle assessment applies to unlimited-tier and higher PIP
  medical limit policies
- Use 290MI with ACORD 88; use 90MI standalone
- Michigan default BI: 250/500 (MCL § 500.3009); statutory floor 50/100
- Michigan PD: $10K + separate mandatory PPI $1M (stationary property)
- Michigan UM/UIM NOT required — optional; insurer must offer
- Primary statute: MCL § 500.3101 et seq. (Michigan No-Fault Act)
- State regulator: Michigan Department of Insurance and Financial
  Services (DIFS)
