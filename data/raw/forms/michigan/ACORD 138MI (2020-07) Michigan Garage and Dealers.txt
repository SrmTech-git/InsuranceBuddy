ACORD 138MI (2020-07) — Michigan Garage and Dealers
====================================================
Form number: ACORD 138MI
Edition: 2020-07
Title: Michigan Garage and Dealers
Type: Insurance form (ACORD industry standard, Michigan state variant)
States: MI
Policy term: 12 months (typical for commercial garage policy)
Transaction types: New business, renewal, midterm change, audit-driven adjustment

Purpose:
Michigan-specific Garage and Dealers section. Captures business
information, exposures, coverages, and the Michigan no-fault elections
required for a Garage / Dealers policy under MCL 500.3101 et seq.
State-specific overlay to the national ACORD 128 (Garage and Dealers
Section).

Captures:
- Insured business type: new-car dealer, used-car dealer, service station,
  body shop, repair shop, towing operation, etc.
- Dealer / business license number(s)
- Premises: locations, age, square footage, lot size
- Operations: gross receipts, vehicles in inventory / sold / serviced
- Garage liability (premises and operations)
- Auto Liability (covered autos) — Michigan Residual Liability rules apply
- PIP medical (commercial tier — see companion 136MI)
- PIP non-medical / PPI
- Garagekeepers (CCC — care, custody, control)
- Products and Completed Operations
- UM/UIM for covered autos
- Michigan-required dealer disclosures and acknowledgments

When used:
- New garage policy submission for a Michigan auto-related business
- Renewal where Michigan-specific coverages or dealer info are reselected
- Adding/removing locations or operations
- Midterm endorsement that changes coverage or operations

Notes:
- 2020-07 edition is the first major edition after the July 2, 2020
  effective date of the no-fault reform — incorporates PIP-tier
  disclosure for commercial / garage covered autos
- Companion forms: 136MI (PIP Medical Selection — Commercial), 61MI
  (BI Liability Choice)
- Michigan dealer regulations are administered by the Secretary of State
- MCCA per-vehicle assessment applies to unlimited-tier PIP medical
  for garage covered autos
- Michigan default residual liability limits applicable to garage
  covered autos: 250/500 BI (MCL § 500.3009); statutory floor 50/100;
  PD $10K + separate mandatory PPI $1M
- Michigan UM/UIM NOT required — optional
- State regulator: Michigan DIFS
