ACORD 173MI (2000-09) — Michigan WC Placement Facility Specific Person Exclusion
=================================================================================
Form number: ACORD 173MI
Edition: 2000-09
Title: Michigan WC Placement Facility Specific Person Exclusion
Type: Insurance form (ACORD industry standard, Michigan state variant)
States: MI
Policy term: Matches the underlying MWCPF WC policy term (typically 12 months); exclusion persists until revoked in writing
Transaction types: New MWCPF business (exclusion election), renewal, change in person status

Purpose:
Michigan Workers' Compensation Placement Facility (MWCPF) form
documenting the exclusion of a specific person from a WC policy
issued through the Placement Facility — Michigan's WC residual
market / assigned-risk pool. The MWCPF allows exclusion of specific
named individuals from coverage (typically owners, officers, family
members, or independent contractors); this form captures that
exclusion.

Captures:
- Employer (named insured) information
- MWCPF policy number
- Excluded individual's name and role
- Statement that the excluded individual will not be entitled to WC
  benefits under the policy if injured
- Statement that the excluded individual's payroll will not be
  included in the WC premium calculation
- Excluded individual's signature acknowledging the exclusion and
  its consequences
- Employer / authorized signatory and date
- MWCPF-specific underwriting acknowledgments

When used:
- New MWCPF submission where specific persons are being excluded
- Renewal where exclusions are being maintained or modified
- Midterm change when person's status changes

Notes:
- MWCPF = Michigan Workers' Compensation Placement Facility — the
  state's residual market / assigned-risk WC pool for employers
  unable to obtain coverage in the voluntary market
- Distinct from voluntary-market exclusions captured on 172MI
  (Application for Exclusion of Partner(s), Spouse, Child or Parent)
  — 173MI is the MWCPF version, 172MI is the voluntary-market version
- Companion forms: 133MI (MI WC Application), 172MI (voluntary
  exclusion form)
