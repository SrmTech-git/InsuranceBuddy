ACORD 138IL (2015-12) — Illinois Garage and Dealers
====================================================
Form number: ACORD 138IL
Edition: 2015-12
Title: Illinois Garage and Dealers
Type: Insurance form (ACORD industry standard, Illinois state variant)
States: IL
Policy term: 12 months (typical for commercial garage policy)
Transaction types: New business, renewal, midterm change, audit-driven adjustment

Purpose:
Illinois-specific Garage and Dealers section. Captures the
business-specific information, exposures, coverages, and Illinois-
required disclosures for a Garage / Dealers policy covering new- and
used-car dealers, service stations, body shops, repair shops, and
similar auto-related businesses. State-specific overlay to the
national ACORD 128 (Garage and Dealers Section).

Captures:
- Insured business type: new-car dealer, used-car dealer, service station,
  body shop, repair shop, towing operation, etc.
- Dealer / business license number(s)
- Premises: locations, age, square footage, lot size
- Operations: gross receipts by operation type, number of vehicles
  in inventory / sold / serviced annually
- Garage liability coverage (premises and operations)
- Auto Liability (covered autos — symbols, limits)
- Garagekeepers (customers' vehicles in care, custody, control)
- Products and Completed Operations
- UM/UIM for covered autos (Illinois rules apply)
- Illinois-required dealer disclosures and acknowledgments

When used:
- New garage policy submission for an Illinois auto-related business
- Renewal where Illinois-specific coverages or dealer info are reselected
- Adding/removing locations or operations
- Midterm endorsement that changes coverage, limits, or operations

Notes:
- Illinois UM in limits equal to BI applies to covered autos
- Illinois dealer regulations are administered by the Secretary of State
  (Vehicle Services Department)
- Companion form: national ACORD 128 (Garage and Dealers Section) where
  agency uses modular ACORD 125 + 128 + 138IL structure
- Illinois personal-auto minimums applicable to garage covered autos:
  25/50/20 (625 ILCS 5/7-203). UM REQUIRED for covered autos.
- Primary statutes: 215 ILCS 5, 625 ILCS 5
- State regulator: Illinois Department of Insurance (IDOI)
