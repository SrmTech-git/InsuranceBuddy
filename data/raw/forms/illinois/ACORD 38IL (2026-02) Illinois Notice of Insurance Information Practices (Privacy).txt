ACORD 38IL (2026-02) — Illinois Notice of Insurance Information Practices (Privacy)
====================================================================================
Form number: ACORD 38IL
Edition: 2026-02
Title: Illinois Notice of Insurance Information Practices (Privacy)
Type: Insurance form (ACORD industry standard, Illinois state variant)
States: IL
Policy term: Notice delivered at issuance and at periodic renewals; not tied to a specific policy term
Transaction types: New business (notice required), renewal (re-disclosure where required)

Purpose:
Illinois-required privacy notice informing insurance applicants and
insureds about the information practices of the carrier: what
personal information is collected, how it is used, how it is shared
with third parties, the insured's rights to access and correct
information, and how to opt out of certain disclosures. State-level
counterpart to federal Gramm-Leach-Bliley Act privacy disclosures,
adapted for Illinois insurance regulations.

Captures:
- Statutory description of the carrier's information-collection
  practices
- Categories of personal information collected (identity, financial,
  consumer report, MVR, CLUE, medical where applicable)
- Categories of information sharing: with affiliates, with non-
  affiliates for service, for marketing
- Insured's right to access information held by the carrier
- Insured's right to correct inaccurate information
- Insured's right to opt out of certain disclosures (where applicable)
- Contact information for the carrier's privacy officer
- Insured acknowledgment of receipt
- Insured signature and date (where applicable)

When used:
- New Illinois personal lines or commercial submission — notice
  delivered with the application
- Renewal (annual or biennial — carrier-specific)
- Anytime privacy practices materially change

Notes:
- Illinois Insurance Code privacy provisions (215 ILCS 5/1014 et seq.)
  and Illinois insurance regulations require this disclosure
- Federal GLBA also requires privacy notices; carriers typically
  satisfy both with a single notice
- Pure informational notice — no coverage election occurs
- Companion forms across states: 38VA (Virginia), 38MN (Minnesota
  Authorization), and similar state variants
