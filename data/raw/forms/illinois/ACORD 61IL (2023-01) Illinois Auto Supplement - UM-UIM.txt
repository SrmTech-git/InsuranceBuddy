ACORD 61IL (2023-01) — Illinois Auto Supplement – UM/UIM
=========================================================
Form number: ACORD 61IL
Edition: 2023-01
Title: Illinois Auto Supplement – UM/UIM
Type: Insurance form (ACORD industry standard, Illinois state variant)
States: IL
Policy term: Matches the underlying auto policy term (typically 6 or 12 months); election persists until changed in writing
Transaction types: New business (UM/UIM election required); change of UM/UIM limits; UIM rejection/reduction

Purpose:
Captures the Illinois insured's Uninsured Motorists (UM) and
Underinsured Motorists (UIM) coverage offer, selection, and election.
Documents the insured's choices around UM and UIM limits, including
any election to reduce UIM below the policy's BI liability limits — a
choice that under Illinois law (215 ILCS 5/143a / 215 ILCS 5/143a-2)
must be made in writing.

Captures:
- UM coverage offer and selection at specified limits
- UIM coverage offer and selection at specified limits
- Election to reduce UIM below BI liability limits (in writing)
- Insured acknowledgment of UM/UIM rights
- Insured signature and date

When used:
- New Illinois personal or commercial auto submission — UM/UIM election
  is required at issuance
- Renewal or midterm change where UM/UIM limits are being modified
- Whenever UIM is being reduced below BI liability limits (written
  election required)

Notes:
- Illinois UM coverage must be carried at limits equal to BI liability
- UIM coverage must be carried at limits equal to BI unless the insured
  elects in writing to reduce UIM to a lower amount (down to statutory
  minimum)
- Companion form: 64IL — UM Property Damage Coverage Notice and Election
- Illinois UM is REQUIRED — cannot be waived (625 ILCS 5/7-601);
  must be carried at limits equal to BI (minimum 25/50)
- Illinois UIM is not mandatory but insurer must OFFER (215 ILCS 5/143a);
  reduction below BI liability limits requires written election
- Illinois minimum liability limits: 25/50/20 (625 ILCS 5/7-203)
