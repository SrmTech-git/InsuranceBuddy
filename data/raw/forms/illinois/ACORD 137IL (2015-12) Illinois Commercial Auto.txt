ACORD 137IL (2015-12) — Illinois Commercial Auto
=================================================
Form number: ACORD 137IL
Edition: 2015-12
Title: Illinois Commercial Auto
Type: Insurance form (ACORD industry standard, Illinois state variant)
States: IL
Policy term: 12 months (typical for commercial auto)
Transaction types: New business, renewal, midterm change, audit-driven adjustment

Purpose:
Illinois-specific Commercial Auto Coverages section. Captures the
coverage selections, limits, deductibles, and Illinois-required
disclosures for a commercial auto policy. Used alongside the broader
commercial auto submission (ACORD 125 Applicant Information + ACORD 127
Business Auto Section + this state-coverages form 137IL).

Captures:
- Liability limits (BI/PD split or Combined Single Limit)
- Comp / collision deductibles by vehicle class
- UM / UIM coverage selections and limits
- MedPay
- Hired Auto Liability
- Non-Owned Auto Liability
- Drive Other Car coverage (where applicable)
- Towing & labor / rental reimbursement / loss of use
- Trailer interchange (for trucking risks)
- Illinois-required disclosures and acknowledgments

When used:
- New commercial auto submission for an Illinois-based fleet
- Renewal where Illinois-specific coverages are being reselected
- Midterm endorsement that changes coverage or limits

Notes:
- Illinois requires UM in limits equal to BI; UIM rejection/reduction
  below BI must be in writing — applies to commercial as well as personal
- Pairs with national ACORD 125 (Applicant Info) and ACORD 127 (Business
  Auto Section — vehicles, drivers, garaging)
- Illinois personal-auto minimum limits: 25/50/20 (625 ILCS 5/7-203).
  Commercial fleets subject to federal MCS-90 minimums for interstate
  operations (typically $750K-$5M depending on cargo).
- Illinois UM REQUIRED (625 ILCS 5/7-601) — applies to commercial as
  well as personal
- State regulator: Illinois Department of Insurance (IDOI)
