ACORD 851IL (2025-01) — Illinois Property Supplement
=====================================================
Form number: ACORD 851IL
Edition: 2025-01
Title: Illinois Property Supplement
Type: Insurance form (ACORD industry standard, Illinois state variant)
States: IL
Policy term: Matches the underlying property policy (typically 12 months); elections persist until changed in writing
Transaction types: New business (Illinois-required property disclosures and elections); change to coverages or limits

Purpose:
General-purpose Illinois property supplement attached to homeowners
and dwelling submissions. Captures Illinois-specific disclosures
and property-coverage elections required under state statute that
are not part of the standard ACORD 80IL homeowners application or
the more specific notice forms (62IL earthquake, 64IL UMPD, etc.).

Captures:
- Illinois-specific statutory property disclosures
- Coverage selections and acknowledgments specific to Illinois
- Insured signature and date

When used:
- New Illinois homeowners or dwelling fire submission — companion to
  80IL or 85IL applications
- Renewal where Illinois-specific elections are being modified
- Midterm change
