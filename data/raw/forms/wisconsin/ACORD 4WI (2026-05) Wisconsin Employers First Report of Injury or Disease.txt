ACORD 4WI (2026-05) — Wisconsin Employers First Report of Injury or Disease
============================================================================
Form number: ACORD 4WI
Edition: 2026-05
Title: Wisconsin Employers First Report of Injury or Disease
Type: Insurance form (ACORD industry standard, Wisconsin state variant)
States: WI
Policy term: N/A (claim/loss event, not a policy form)
Transaction types: N/A (loss notification — first report of injury / disease)

Purpose:
Wisconsin-specific First Report of Injury (FROI) form for Workers
Compensation claims. Used by Wisconsin employers to report an
employee injury, occupational disease, or fatality to the WC carrier
and to the Wisconsin Department of Workforce Development (DWD) within
the statutory reporting timeframe. Wisconsin state variant of the
national ACORD 4 WC FROI.

Captures:
- Employer information (named insured, FEIN, NAICS, location)
- Injured / ill employee information: name, DOB, SSN, address, hire
  date, occupation, department, wage information
- Injury / disease details:
  * Date and time of injury / disease onset
  * Location of injury (specific premises area)
  * Body part(s) affected
  * Nature of injury (laceration, fracture, strain, etc.)
  * Cause of injury (slip, struck-by, lifting, repetitive motion, etc.)
  * Equipment / object involved
- Whether work was lost and expected duration
- Medical treatment information: provider, facility, treatment received
- Witnesses to the injury
- Whether OSHA-recordable
- Whether fatal
- Wisconsin-specific statutory disclosure compliance
- Employer signature and date submitted

When used:
- Within statutory timeframe (typically days to weeks depending on
  severity) after a Wisconsin work-related injury or occupational
  disease
- Submitted to the WC carrier for claim setup
- Submitted to Wisconsin Department of Workforce Development (DWD)
  Worker's Compensation Division as required by Wis. Stat. Ch. 102

Notes:
- Wisconsin WC is administered by the DWD Worker's Compensation Division
- Wis. Stat. Ch. 102 governs WC reporting requirements and benefit
  schedules
- Companion forms: 133WI (WC Insurance Pool / Assigned Risk),
  134WI, 135WI (Election/Non-Election supplements), 136WI (Other
  States Coverage)
