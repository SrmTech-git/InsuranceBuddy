ACORD 134WI (1999-08) — Wisconsin Supplementary Non-Election Form
==================================================================
Form number: ACORD 134WI
Edition: 1999-08
Title: Wisconsin Supplementary Non-Election Form
Type: Insurance form (ACORD industry standard, Wisconsin state variant)
States: WI
Policy term: Matches the underlying WC policy term (typically 12 months); non-election persists until changed in writing
Transaction types: New business (officer/owner non-election); election change

Purpose:
Wisconsin Workers' Compensation supplement capturing the non-election
of WC coverage by an officer, owner, partner, or family member who
would otherwise be eligible to elect coverage under Wisconsin WC law.
Wisconsin WC default treatment varies by entity type (e.g., corporate
officers are typically auto-covered with opt-out option; sole
proprietors and partners are typically excluded with opt-in option) —
this form documents the explicit non-election.

Captures:
- Employer / policy information
- Individual's name, role (officer, owner, partner, family member)
- Statement that the individual is NOT electing WC coverage under
  the policy
- Statement that the individual understands they will not be entitled
  to WC benefits under the policy if injured
- Individual signature and date

When used:
- At new business when an eligible officer / owner / partner / family
  member is opting OUT of WC coverage
- At renewal where the non-election is being maintained or changed
- Midterm if a newly added officer/owner is making the non-election
  decision

Notes:
- Wisconsin Wis. Stat. Ch. 102 governs WC coverage eligibility and
  election/non-election rights
- Default treatment varies by entity:
  * Corporate officers — typically included; may opt out (this form)
  * Sole proprietors / partners — typically excluded; may opt in
    (companion 135WI)
  * Family members of sole proprietors — typically excluded; may
    opt in (companion 135WI)
- Companion form: 135WI — Supplementary Election of Coverage Form
  (the opt-in version)
