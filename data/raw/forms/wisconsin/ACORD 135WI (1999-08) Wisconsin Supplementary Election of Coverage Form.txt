ACORD 135WI (1999-08) — Wisconsin Supplementary Election of Coverage Form
==========================================================================
Form number: ACORD 135WI
Edition: 1999-08
Title: Wisconsin Supplementary Election of Coverage Form
Type: Insurance form (ACORD industry standard, Wisconsin state variant)
States: WI
Policy term: Matches the underlying WC policy term (typically 12 months); election persists until changed in writing
Transaction types: New business (officer/owner election into coverage); election change

Purpose:
Wisconsin Workers' Compensation supplement capturing the election of
WC coverage by an officer, owner, partner, sole proprietor, or family
member who would otherwise be excluded from coverage by default.
Wisconsin WC default treatment varies by entity type; this form
documents the explicit election into coverage.

Captures:
- Employer / policy information
- Individual's name, role (sole proprietor, partner, family member,
  officer in opt-out entity, etc.)
- Statement that the individual is ELECTING WC coverage under
  the policy
- Statement that the individual will be subject to the policy's
  WC benefits and the corresponding payroll inclusion for premium
- Individual signature and date

When used:
- At new business when a sole proprietor, partner, or eligible family
  member is opting IN to WC coverage
- At renewal where the election is being maintained or changed
- Midterm if a newly added owner/partner/family member is making the
  election decision

Notes:
- Wisconsin Wis. Stat. Ch. 102 governs WC coverage eligibility and
  election/non-election rights
- Default treatment varies by entity:
  * Sole proprietors / partners — typically excluded; may elect IN
    (this form)
  * Family members of sole proprietors — typically excluded; may
    elect IN (this form)
  * Corporate officers — typically auto-included; may opt OUT
    (companion 134WI)
- Election to include adds the individual's payroll to the WC premium
  calculation
- Companion form: 134WI — Supplementary Non-Election Form (the
  opt-out version)
