ACORD 61WI (2025-02) — Wisconsin Auto Supplement
=================================================
Form number: ACORD 61WI
Edition: 2025-02
Title: Wisconsin Auto Supplement
Type: Insurance form (ACORD industry standard, Wisconsin state variant)
States: WI
Policy term: Matches the underlying auto policy term; elections persist until changed in writing
Transaction types: New business (Wisconsin-required disclosures and elections); change to coverages or limits

Purpose:
General-purpose Wisconsin auto supplement attached to personal and
commercial auto submissions. Captures Wisconsin-specific disclosures
and coverage elections required under Wisconsin's compulsory auto
insurance statute (Wis. Stat. § 632.32), including UM (mandatory)
and UIM (offered) selections.

Captures:
- UM coverage selection at statutory minimum or higher selected limits
  (UM is mandatory in Wisconsin)
- UIM coverage offer / selection / rejection (UIM is offered; insured
  may reject in writing)
- Medical Payments coverage selection (where applicable)
- Other Wisconsin-required disclosures and acknowledgments
- Insured signature and date

When used:
- New Wisconsin auto submission — UM/UIM selection and disclosures
  required
- Renewal where UM/UIM or other Wisconsin-specific coverages are being
  modified
- Midterm change

Notes:
- Wisconsin moved from financial responsibility to compulsory auto
  insurance in 2010 — UM coverage is mandatory; UIM is offered but
  may be rejected

- Wisconsin UM MANDATORY at 25/50 (Wis. Stat. § 632.32(4)); cannot be
  waived. UM covers BI only — not property damage.
- Wisconsin UIM: NOT mandatory (since 2011 amendments to § 632.32);
  insurer must NOTIFY policyholder UIM is available; insured may
  reject in writing. If purchased, minimum 50/100.
- Wisconsin liability minimums: 25/50/10 (§ 344.15)
