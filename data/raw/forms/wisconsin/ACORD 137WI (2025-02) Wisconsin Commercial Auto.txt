ACORD 137WI (2025-02) — Wisconsin Commercial Auto
==================================================
Form number: ACORD 137WI
Edition: 2025-02
Title: Wisconsin Commercial Auto
Type: Insurance form (ACORD industry standard, Wisconsin state variant)
States: WI
Policy term: 12 months (typical for commercial auto)
Transaction types: New business, renewal, midterm change, audit-driven adjustment

Purpose:
Wisconsin-specific Commercial Auto Coverages section. Captures coverage
selections, limits, deductibles, and Wisconsin-required disclosures for
a commercial auto policy under Wis. Stat. § 632.32 (compulsory auto
insurance). Used alongside national ACORD 125 and 127.

Captures:
- Liability limits (BI/PD split or Combined Single Limit)
- Comp / collision deductibles
- UM (mandatory in Wisconsin) and UIM (where offered)
- MedPay
- Hired Auto Liability
- Non-Owned Auto Liability
- Drive Other Car coverage (where applicable)
- Towing & labor / rental reimbursement / loss of use
- Trailer interchange (for trucking)
- Wisconsin-required disclosures and acknowledgments

When used:
- New commercial auto submission for a Wisconsin-based fleet
- Renewal where Wisconsin-specific coverages are being reselected
- Midterm endorsement that changes coverage or limits

Notes:
- Wisconsin moved from financial responsibility to compulsory auto
  insurance in 2010 — applies to commercial as well as personal autos
- Wisconsin requires UM; UIM is offered but may be rejected
- Wisconsin personal-auto minimum limits: 25/50/10 (Wis. Stat. § 344.15).
  Commercial fleets subject to federal MCS-90 for interstate operations.
- WI UM MANDATORY at 25/50 (Wis. Stat. § 632.32(4)); UIM optional
- State regulator: Wisconsin OCI; WisDOT for SR-22
