ACORD 138WI (2025-02) — Wisconsin Garage and Dealers
=====================================================
Form number: ACORD 138WI
Edition: 2025-02
Title: Wisconsin Garage and Dealers
Type: Insurance form (ACORD industry standard, Wisconsin state variant)
States: WI
Policy term: 12 months (typical for commercial garage policy)
Transaction types: New business, renewal, midterm change, audit-driven adjustment

Purpose:
Wisconsin-specific Garage and Dealers section. Captures business
information, exposures, coverages, and Wisconsin-required disclosures
for a Garage / Dealers policy under Wis. Stat. § 632.32 (compulsory
auto insurance). State-specific overlay to the national ACORD 128
(Garage and Dealers Section).

Captures:
- Insured business type: new-car dealer, used-car dealer, service station,
  body shop, repair shop, towing operation, etc.
- Dealer / business license number(s)
- Premises: locations, age, square footage, lot size
- Operations: gross receipts, vehicles in inventory / sold / serviced
- Garage liability (premises and operations)
- Auto Liability (covered autos)
- Garagekeepers (CCC — care, custody, control)
- Products and Completed Operations
- UM (mandatory in Wisconsin) and UIM for covered autos
- Wisconsin-required dealer disclosures and acknowledgments

When used:
- New garage policy submission for a Wisconsin auto-related business
- Renewal where Wisconsin-specific coverages or dealer info are reselected
- Adding/removing locations or operations
- Midterm endorsement that changes coverage or operations

Notes:
- Wisconsin moved from financial responsibility to compulsory auto
  insurance in 2010 — applies to garage covered autos as well
- Wisconsin requires UM; UIM is offered but may be rejected
- Wisconsin dealer regulations are administered by the Wisconsin
  Department of Transportation (Dealer Section)
- Wisconsin personal-auto minimums applicable to garage covered autos:
  25/50/10 (Wis. Stat. § 344.15). UM mandatory at 25/50 (§ 632.32(4)).
- State regulator: Wisconsin OCI; WisDOT for SR-22
