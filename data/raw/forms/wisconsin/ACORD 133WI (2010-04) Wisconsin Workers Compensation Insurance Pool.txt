ACORD 133WI (2010-04) — Wisconsin Workers Compensation Insurance Pool
======================================================================
Form number: ACORD 133WI
Edition: 2010-04
Title: Wisconsin Workers Compensation Insurance Pool
Type: Insurance form (ACORD industry standard, Wisconsin state variant)
States: WI
Policy term: 12 months (typical for WC; subject to audit)
Transaction types: New business (Pool submission), renewal, midterm change

Purpose:
Application for Workers' Compensation coverage through the Wisconsin
Workers Compensation Insurance Pool — Wisconsin's residual market /
assigned-risk facility for employers unable to obtain WC coverage in
the voluntary market. The Pool is administered by the Wisconsin
Compensation Rating Bureau (WCRB).

Captures:
- Employer information (named insured, FEIN, business structure)
- Business description, operations, NAICS / SIC
- Years in business, number of employees
- Locations
- Payroll by NCCI / Wisconsin classification:
  * Class code
  * Class description
  * Annual payroll
  * Number of employees
- Officer / owner / partner inclusion or exclusion elections (see
  companion 134WI and 135WI for election/non-election forms)
- Subcontractor exposure
- Prior carrier(s) and reasons for voluntary-market denial (required
  for Pool submission)
- Experience modification factor
- 3-5 year loss history
- Safety programs, drug testing, return-to-work
- Other states coverage needed (see companion 136WI)
- Pool-specific disclosures and acknowledgments
- Authorized signatory and date

When used:
- New Wisconsin WC submission to the Pool after voluntary-market denial
- Renewal of existing Pool coverage
- Reporting significant exposure changes

Notes:
- Wisconsin WC Insurance Pool is administered by the Wisconsin
  Compensation Rating Bureau (WCRB) — Wisconsin's WC rating
  organization and Pool administrator
- WC in Wisconsin is governed by Wis. Stat. Ch. 102; the DWD
  Worker's Compensation Division administers state WC law
- Pool submission typically requires documentation of voluntary-
  market denials (usually 2+ rejections)
- Companion forms:
  * 134WI / 135WI — Election supplements (officer/owner coverage
    elections)
  * 136WI — Other States Coverage Request
  * 4WI — Employers First Report of Injury or Disease (FROI)
